Yep, it's a 2 session ticket for both Saturday and Monday. It's face value plus a 20 dollar application fee per ticket.

You have to pay for it now.
You get confirmation of your ticket in August. (or a refund minus the 20 dollars but I've never heard of anybody getting an app declined)
You find out where your seats are in November.
You have to use Flash Seats to get your tickets and those tickets get delivered later in November.
For entry, you have to use the credit card that you used to purchase the tickets.
After the Final Four, you get mailed the real tickets as a souvenir.

Included with the ticket purchase is one free FanFest ticket, one free voucher for a Final Four Program at the stadium and your Music Fest ticket (which is free anyway).
