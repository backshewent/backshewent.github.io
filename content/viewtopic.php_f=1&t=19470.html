An interesting tidbit I learned while reading "The Betrayal", which is about the Black Sox scandal:

If the A's, Indians, Tigers, Browns, and Senators didn't go along with appointing Landis as commissioner, the White Sox, Red Sox, and Yankees were going to leave the AL and join the NL and form a 12 team league. The 12th team was going to be the first of the remaining AL teams to jump ship or a new team placed in either Cleveland or Detroit. It got so far as the three teams dissolving their relationship with the AL and joining the NL, only to have the other 5 teams acquiesce later that day or the next day.
