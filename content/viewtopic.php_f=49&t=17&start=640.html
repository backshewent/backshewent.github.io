GMAN wrote:Any word if the Class is going to make it another year ? I may be the only one, but I really like the show.

Most shows don't receive pick-ups until late April or May, so it's too early to tell. Sitcoms aren't popular these days with audiences or the networks. ABC, NBC, and CBS each only have two hours now, while Fox has around 3.5. The Class is the 4th highest rated sitcoms on TV and it gets decent numbers. That's the good news. The three highest are CBS' three other Monday night shows. If Earl or George Lopez aired there, they'd get better ratings than they do now. Still, The Class holds almost all of Your Mother's audience and that's a big deal to the networks. I'd guess it'll be back.
