#32 Aklinski (the GRIT kid)

UK center fielder Ben Aklinski will sign with the Philadelphia Phillies this weekend, he told Catspause.com. Aklinski was selected in the 32nd round by the Phillies on Wednesday.

The junior from Phoenix, Arizona, was fourth on the team in average this year with a .304 mark. He hit six home runs and drove in 42 RBIs while slugging .444. Aklinski spent one season in Lexington after having an All-American sophomore season at the junior college level.

Aklinski was First-Team All-ACCAC in 2017 at Phoenix College. He was honored as the NJCAA Division II Defensive Player of the Year and won the Rawlings JUCO Gold Glove award.
