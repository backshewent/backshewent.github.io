by EndlessSummer » Wed Oct 21, 2009 17:55:46

Barry's right, there aren't too many bars, but David's Mai Lai Wah is a restaurant that has a tiny, tiny craphole bar up front that's pretty comical and cheap as hell. It's at 10th and Race so it's near the Troc. I don't think it's a place you'd spend much time, but it could work for a shot and some drinks for like 3 or 4 people. And the upstairs bar at Vietnam is pretty cool--they play kung fu movies and have ridiculously named drinks. Could be cool before a show, probably not after. And Yakitori Boy is a giant karaoke spot, which is good if you're in the mood for karaoke.
