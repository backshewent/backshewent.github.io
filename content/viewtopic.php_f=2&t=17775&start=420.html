Mother! is batshit. Pretty straightforward allegory that just keeps pushing further into ultraviolence to prove its point. Really well shot, really great sound editing, really cool visual style, and it's genuinely frightening at times. Bardem and Pfeiffer are great. But it's one of the most harrowing film experiences I've ever sat through and I'll never do it again. I'm not even sure some of the most disgusting of the characters' actions have any real connection to the allegory it's trying to tell. And the implications of the moral are cynical and depressing. Aronofsky is a terrific filmmaker but he seems like such a dickwad
