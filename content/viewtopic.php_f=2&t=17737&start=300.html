Massage/trigger point therapy has been a godsend. It's helped out directly with an old snowboarding shoulder injury. It also worked to eliminate carpal tunnel with massage on the forearms and plantar fasciitis with massage of the calves. Damn it sucks getting old and staying active.

Stretch out your arm, Palm down and find the trigger point just below the joint on the top of the forearm. It should hurt with decent pressure and actually send a sensation down to your thumb and forefinger. Keep working on that everyday for about 5 minutes here and there a few times a day for a few months.
