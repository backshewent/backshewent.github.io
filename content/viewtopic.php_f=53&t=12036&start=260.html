WheelsFellOff wrote:Fan who fell Monday from Coors Field stairs dies

Witnesses told police the man was trying to slide down the staircase railing, lost his balance and fell, striking his head. The stairs lead to the center field seats.


This is incredibly similar to what happened after game 2 of the 2008 NLCS at CBP. Phils beat the Dodgers to go up 2-0 in that series, and a fan did a very similar thing just behind sections 418/419/420...somewhere in that range. The Phils, somehow, managed to keep this extremely quiet. I emailed a few of the beat writers at the time attempting to find out what eventually happened to the fan, and the only response I received was from Paul Hagen saying he had heard nothing about it but would look into it. I emailed him for a follow-up a month or so later but did not hear back from him again.

I recall discussing it with someone here at BSG who also witnessed it (not the actual fall, but the fan being attended to on the ground...as was my experience), and that individual knew someone who knew someone in the Philly medical arena who thought they had heard the fan had a serious brain injury. That's about all I ever learned after the fact, but the story from onlookers at the scene was that he had attempted to ride the railing down and upon reaching the landing between the two portions of the staircase, had flipped over the railing and fallen to the concrete mezzanine below.
