Some thoughts on FFXV for those of you who were curious:

1) It's fun! Not a masterpiece or anything but you have fun playing it.
2) The combat isn't always button mashy...but it can get button mashy.
3) The camera angles legit suck. Sometimes in combat you get stuck with the camera behind a bush so all you see is some half rendered shrubbery.
4) The main plot line is suuuuuuper short and it's pretty easy to accidentally hit the point of no return.

[Reveal] Spoiler:
There's a time traveling dog thing that let's you go back in time and keep playing. I'm assuming you can use this to go back and explore at a higher level like a new game+ thing.


The dialog is corny, plot is kinda contrived but I kinda liked the characters.
