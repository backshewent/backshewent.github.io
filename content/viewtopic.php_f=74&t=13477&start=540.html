I try to use Amex as much as I can because of their cash back rewards program. I have my card linked with a mint.com account, which is set to send me email alerts when I get close to the point where I wouldn't be able to pay off the entire balance that month. For places that don't take Amex I'll generally use the Visa debit card that's linked directly to my checking account (which is also linked to mint.com and has similar email alerts configured). Initial setup took maybe an hour or two, but it's been pretty much hands off since then, except for when I occasionally update passwords or adjust alerting thresholds.
