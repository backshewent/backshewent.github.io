by FTN » Mon Nov 16, 2009 23:38:30

I think they deserve their own breakout thread.

The whole "where do they rank all time" argument is tired. But I often times think about where I put them in my own personal list. I've loved them since I was a child. My mom was a big Beatles fan, she played lots of their stuff when we'd drive around in the car. So I've been exposed to their music since I'm old enough to remember. I think its plausible to say I haven't gone more than a week in the last 15 years without listening to a Beatles song. Sometimes I've ranked them like 10th. Sometimes 2nd. I think at one point in college I considered them my favorite band ever.

I think right now I'd rank them #3.

If you like the Beatles and you have a bit of disposable income, I highly recommend you pick up the Stereo Remasters. If nothing else, you should grab Revolver, Sgt Pepper, White Album, and Abbey Road. But all of them really. Fantastic.

Also, lets do 10 fav songs.

01. A Day in the Life
02. Blackbird
03. I Want You (She's So Heavy)
04. Something
05. When I'm Sixty Four
06. Across the Universe
07. Dear Prudence
08. Drive My Car
09. Tomorrow Never Knows
10. Getting Better
