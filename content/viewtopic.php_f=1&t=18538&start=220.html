just got around to checking bp's win projection update.
phils up to 81. nl 2nd wildcard (stl) at 85. if standard error of projections is 6 wins (reported by fangraphs earlier this offseason), phils odds of 85+ wins based on 81 projected would be about 1 in 4. if standard error is 8 (what i think i remember reading in previous offseasons), the odds of 85 plus would be 3 in 10.

meanwhile, fangraphs, with their usual weird daily fluctuations, is back down to 75 for the phils and just 82 for the second wc in the nl. the phils odds of 82+ wins based on a projection of 75 would be 8/1 to 5/1 for win projection standard errors ranging form 6 to 8.

most pessimistic odds would be fangraphs phillies wins, bp's nl wildcard threshold, and fg's optimistic standard error. that gives 25/1 odds against the phils reaching the playoffs... which, oddly enough, seems about right to me.

depending on which standard error one uses, adding two wins via the arrieta signing improved the phils chances 50 to 100%.
