by Philly the Kid » Thu Jun 25, 2009 18:49:56

A lot can and will be said about Jackson... childhood abuse, pressure of being a child star. I luv the old skool Jackson 5 stuff, his solo stuff never did much for me, but I recognize its freshness and impact in the early 80's. I do think like many, he got stuck, he got old and all his self-indulgences and eccentricities over-shadowed any actual contribution in the last 15 years. But his impact on pop culture is undeniable. Like Elvis, they all become distortions and die young... not sure what a 50 year old pop-star could have achieved. The spectre of the child molestation stuff will always be disturbing along with his increasingly effeminate qualities and the horrific mangling of his human form. Having white children with a woman paid off to be a womb doner... freakish stuff, but ABC and I'll be There -- will always bring a smile to my face...

I know Thriller is seminal to many... dated to me, but respect for its time and place.
