Werthless wrote:
The Savior wrote:Gonna buy a 2015 Honda Pilot. Good choice?

Just get the minivan, bro.


As minivan driver, I have to say, there are many pluses to the minivan over the SUV. A lot of it stems from the sliding doors and the "aisle" you get in the middle of the 2nd row, and the lower floor. (If you don't have/take out the middle seat. When we just had two kids, we took out one of 2nd row seats so there was a huge space to get to the back - I learned that from another mom.) Anyway, with small parking spaces, it's really hard to get the kids in and out if you can't open the SUV door all the way. The lower floor of the minivan makes it easier to climb in and unbuckle the kids from the inside if it's rainy, or if you're parked on a city street and don't want to get the kid out on the traffic side of the vehicle. There's more cargo room, which gives you room to keep the stroller in the car AND have room for groceries (or when the kids are older, the sports equipment.)

I've had two minivans in about 8 years or so, and in that time had the opportunity to drive a couple different SUVs during that period. All the problems with SUVs are problems I personally experienced. From when I was shopping, I do remember that price points are usually lower for equivalent options, and the gas mileage is better.
