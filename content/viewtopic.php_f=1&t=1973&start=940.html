by seke2 » Sat Nov 24, 2007 09:44:22

well, thats really like a 3 outer, since you have to count both kings, the other seven (plus the small chance of running hearts).

still sucks though

you played it fine, rationale and all. the only other note that makes it even more mandatory to bet hard on the flop on that board is the texture. 2 suited and with a variety of possible draws, with that action preflop, there's a good chance someone has an overpair and if they don't they could have 2 overs with a nut flush draw or even another set or something like 66 where they have a pair + OESD.

so you definitely have to bet the flop, and bet pot or near pot (as you did).
