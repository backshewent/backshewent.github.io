by smitty » Tue Nov 12, 2013 13:56:48

Byrd is a guy who has had one weird career. Had a fine rookie year at the not young age of 25. Fell off a cliff and didn't have his second good year until age 29. Had a pretty fair run from age 29-31. Then he fell off another cliff, assisted by PEDs.

Finally has his best season last year at the age of 35.

He is a good corner outfield defender it looks like. Never was a base stealer but was a pretty fast runner when young.

He has more value than Delmon Young most likely. Biggest question is when he will fall offa cliff again. Third time will be the last I'd bet.

He's no Wiley Coyote.

He is an unusual player. Another one of those guys the computers could never quite get a handle on.
