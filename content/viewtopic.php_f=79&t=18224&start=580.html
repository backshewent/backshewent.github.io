-I think we saw what we would with Torrey, and if Carson puts some more air under that one and the bomb hits it's 'wow great deal' there
-Welcome back Nelson Agolhar - great to see
-If Peters is hurt I guess we'll see how the line is going to look in the next years..
-If Sturgis is hurt...uh oh
