by TenuredVulture » Sat Jan 19, 2013 12:43:13

Ramon Gris wrote:I'm going to post here, and assume you fuckers won't notice it. I'm blowing off a friend's wedding to go to jazz fest in New Orleans this year. All my favorite drinking buddies will be there, and I can't help but think it'll be one of the funnest weekends of my life. Any thoughts, suggestions?


What kind of suggestions? Port of Call on Esplanade makes a really good hamburger and has pretty good drinks--a world class dive. It's likely to be packed. Jazz fest might be different than a typical weekend, but if it's possible, get in a cab and go to Bullett's on Tuesday evening and see Kermit Ruffins. (You'll probably have to have the exact address, most cabbies don't seem to know where it is.) There are also lots of great live music in Fauborg Marigny, again, I have no idea what will be going on during jazz fest. Make your hotel reservation early. We stayed at Place D'Armes hotel, and I thought it was a great value at $85. Obviously, you'll pay more during Jazz Fest. And parking was $32 a night.

If you want a really great meal, I strongly recommend Herbsaint. Of the traditional New Orleans places, I understand that Arnaud's is one of the best--we ate there and really enjoyed our meal. During jazz fest, I'd imagine reservations are essential at both places. Arnaud's has several rooms, I guess, that vary in their level of formality.

Wear a hat. Bring sunscreen. You will burn (even if it's overcast) and a hangover+bad sunburn is really unpleasant.
