Ill take the opposite side, for a few reasons.

1. Even with our recent run of success, it was preceded by a long run of shit luck. I think its easy for people to still always dread what is right around the corner, even after the last 4+ years.
2. At the same time, I think people take offense when all of the "experts" turn after seemingly predicting another World Series in December, simply because Utley is out.
3. Any time you have a good team, and people doubt you, I think its easy to feel slighted and take it as a chip on your shoulder, even when its not YOU they are talking about. The best players or performers in any venue always have a chip on their shoulder, even when there isn't any reason to have one. Like Howard saying that everyone "counted out" the offense. I mean, people said maybe we weren't the best offense in the league, but no one said we'd be the Astros. But like 4 games into the season, Howard adopted the siege mentality. Lots of athletes do it.

As an aside, I'm not worried at all right now, and wasn't at the beginning of the season, even with the Utley news. But I can understand why some people get upset.
