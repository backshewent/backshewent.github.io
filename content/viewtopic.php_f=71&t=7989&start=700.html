Since Orel Hershiser recorded his record-breaking 59th consecutive scoreless inning, does anyone know who has come closest to matching him?

And which record is more likely to last: the scoreless inning streak record or the consecutive game hitting streak record? The knee-jerk reaction would be the consecutive game hitting streak record, but the more I think about Hershiser's 59 consecutive scoreless innings (and the fact that he got the 59th and record breaking inning in his last start of the season, and only because that game went into the tenth with the Dodgers scoring, so Hershiser had an opportunity to pitch 10, not 9, innings), the more it blows my mind.
