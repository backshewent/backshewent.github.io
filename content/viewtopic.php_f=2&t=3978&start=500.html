Wow. this new Galaxy Tab 2 is SO much better than my Sony it is remarkable. The screen is AMAZING. Their Pyle "Smart Remote" app is even head and shoulders above the Sony app. It even downloads TV listings, so you just scroll through the listings and punch it up and it will change the channel. I was thinking it was wasted on me since I hardly watch TV except for the Phillies, but in fact it probably helps someone like me even more, because I don't know where the channels are.

Now something that would work on the Sony too...I just discovered the Xfinity TV app. It also gives the TV listings as well as the on-demand stuff, so you can pick what you want and your TV will dial it up (it does this via a digital signal from the Home Office, not via RF, so you don't event have to be in the same room). Figures I found it on episode #10 (of 10) of "The Staircase". The first 9 I mostly struggled with the Comcast remote trying to search for the listing and the proper show and finally would pull it up. Would've saved me a lot of aggravation. And it doesn't make the Smart Remote I just talked about obsolete either- the xfinity app only works on the xfinity digital boxes- not the cheap DCAs.

Back to the tablet, the Wifi is superb. My sony would consistently drop signal in the living room. This thing gets 4 bars.
