by Ramon Gris » Sun Dec 02, 2007 02:24:16

td11 wrote:my friend just showed me www.jango.com

impressed, so far.


Check out pandora.com. It's the best internet radio I've found. It uses information from something called the music genome project to pick out songs based on the characteristics of the music you tell it you like. It plays a lot of great stuff I'd never heard before, and a lot of stuff I already listen to that I hadn't told it about. It's really incredible.
Just an example, on jango, I typed in Arab Strap, and it predictably came back with a bunch of Belle and Sebastian, because their record labels are the same, and they're considered like the same genre, even though their music is totally different. Pandora comes back with really varied stuff that actually shares musical similarities, instrumentation, song structure, etc., rather than just other "indie" music. If you think jango's impressive, you should love pandora.
