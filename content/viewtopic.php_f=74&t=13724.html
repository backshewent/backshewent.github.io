Steve Rothman and Bill Pascrell, two Democrats from the northeast corner of the state, faced off against each other due to redistricting. It was thought to be a toss up but Pascrell won going away. Some people were spinning it as a Clinton (who backed Pascrell who backed Hillary in 2008) vs. Obama (who backed Rothman who backed Obama in 2008) fight, but it was more a battle of county bases. Pascrell dominted in Passaic, Rothman didn't do as well as he needed to in Bergen.

Donald Payne Jr. won the special election to succeed his dead dad. He's an Essex County freeholder. Newark based district, majority minority. Will hold the seat until he dies.

Joe Kyrillos, GOP state senator/former NJ GOP chairman, won the primary to face Senator Menendez in the fall comfortably. He'll lose but not be embarrassed.

Nothing else happened that mattered.
