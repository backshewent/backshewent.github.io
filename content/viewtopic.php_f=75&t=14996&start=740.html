selling a house while having young kids is one of the most stressful possible things you can do. I would not wish that on anyone. I think the sellers were being a short sighted because if someone's spending 45 minutes in a house, they're almost certainly interested. The exquisite pain of having to clean up and clear out your house with kids every time there is a showing could be over soon. Not for nothing though, I can't recall ever spending anywhere close to 30 minutes, let alone 45 minutes in a house I was touring. What were you doing in there? Honest question, maybe I should be testing some obvious things that I haven't thought of (are you turning on the stove to make sure the burners work, the faucets to make sure you get hot water, etc.?)
