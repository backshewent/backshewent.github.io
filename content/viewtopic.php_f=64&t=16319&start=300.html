Re: Hex & Hacks: Flyers 2015 Offseason

by mcare89 » Sat Jun 27, 2015 21:30:25

The Savior wrote:Ok so some added flexibility today, but not an overly impactful trade for a Day 1 of the season cap perspective.

Depends. For one, if they buyout Gagner, it's a few million saved. Regardless, I think it would have been hard for them to spend any real money in free agency.

Here's the thing with LTIR. You can go one of two ways with it.

1) You can get under the cap on day 1, put Pronger on IR, and build up your cap space that way for in-season moves.

2) You can go over the cap on day 1 and basically lose the ability to use LTIR.

Either way, it's not optimal for flexibility. It's not a move where you can point to it and show the tangible results, but it's a lot fewer moving parts at either a low cost (and quite frankly, Gagner is an upgrade over Grossmann anyway) or with cap savings.
