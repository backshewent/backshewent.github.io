by Phan In Phlorida » Wed Jul 25, 2012 19:33:26

Fraud by individual voters is an irrational concern. It would require a massive effort beyond reasonable immagination to have any effect on an election... an actual tin-foil hat conspiracy. Voting "irregularities" are almost always honest mistakes by polling place staff and are either rectified or insignificant. The few reported instances of "individual voter fraud" (voting multiple times, dead people voting, etc.) have either been proven false or were in conjunction with election misconduct by officials. Election fraud/misconduct doesn't occur in the booth, it occurs either before or after the booth... "misplaced" and "lost" ballots, etc. If you want to focus on voter fraud, the focus should be on election officials, missing ballot boxes, machines rigged to tally improperly, etc.
