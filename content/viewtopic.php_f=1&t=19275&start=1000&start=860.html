I don't really see how that's a disaster. If you give him huge money and a three year opt out, there are several possible scenarios:

1. He plays 3 seasons like 2015 and opts out. This is good for the Phils because if he's putting up MVP numbers then we're going to the playoffs and everyone is happy for however long it lasts.

2. He's good not great and opts out. Again this scenario doesn't seem bad as you may decide he's not worth what you're paying him and you have the chance to move on and shift payroll in other directions.

3. He sucks and doesn't opt out. This seems least likely and it would be the worst case scenario as you'd be stuck with him for 10 years at that point. However even "bad" Harper is likely to be pretty good and his skill set should age pretty well particularly if universal DH is coming.

So to sum up, pay the man his money and get it done Middleton!
