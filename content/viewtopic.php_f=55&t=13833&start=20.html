-- A 30-win drop from 102 to 72 would be the largest since the Marlin firesale of 1998 (92 to 54).
-- Chooch's .356 is the highest Phillie BA after 81 games since Ashburn in 1951 (.359) and the highest Phillie catcher BA after 81 games since Spud Davis in 1931 (.368).
-- Only 3 catchers in MLB history hit .350+ and 20+ HRs in the same season: Bill Dickey in 1936, Piazza in 1997 and Mauer in 2006.
-- Vida Blue in 1983 is the only pitcher to go winless for the season having at least 13 starts. (Lee has 13)
-- The Pierre/Victorino/Rollins trio could be the first in franchise history to have 30+ steals in the same season. (Jimmy is on pace for 28)
