by Bucky » Sun Nov 24, 2019 23:57:48

I haven't been able to find any info about the lack of mirrors yet. Elon tried to do this before with the 3, but regulators shot him down. I'd wager by the time this rolls of the line* there will be side view mirrors.

Other things of note that I've gleaned from the fanboy world:

there is no frame. The body is 300 stainless steel. thick and strong, so the body itself is the frame.

It is made by crimping the stainless- no stamping required. as such, assembly time and complexity is greatly reduces. In fact they say there will be no assembly line for these, and the assembly area(s) will be contained in a much smaller footprint than a typical build. No paint also helps shrink the assembly footprint.

Some interesting speculation. given the price points and battery capacities, it may just be that tesla has new battery technology that they feel will be ready for prime time in the two year timeframe before this is slated to ship. Indeed, a 500 mile range vehicle for $70K does seem amazing.

There;s 120 and 240V electrical outlets for the jobsite. Oh, and air ports for air tools. Apparently they run off the compressor used for the dynamic air suspension.

There is a solar cap available, and a standard ramp.

They also teased a little electric ATV that may be an option and some point.
