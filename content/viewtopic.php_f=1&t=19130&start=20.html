So I went to this outdoor mall today that has tons of shops and restaurants, including some American franchises. I was surprised to see a MLB store. Wondering if there might be some weird phillies stuff, I went inside. Almost the entire store was filled with Yankees stuff. Literally every hat in the store was a Yankees hat. There was one diamondbacks jacket and a few Tigers and Red sox things. That's it. 4 teams represented in the entire store and 99+% was Yankees. I tried to ask the clerk why, but my app didn't seem to translate my sentiment well enough for her to get it. I finally just started pointing at everything saying Yankees, Yankees, Yankees, Yankees, then pointed to the MLB sign and asked Yankees? She shook her head yes, which I'm not sure meant anything other than her acknowledging that everything was yankees.

So this huge advantage the Yankees have that partly comes from their merchandizing is completely manufactured. This is the ex-pat section of Beijing and there are plenty of other fans out there, but this store only offers Yankees stuff. Gee, no wonder their sales dwarf all other teams. I don't know if the store chooses what is sold or if mlb suggests merchandise or what, but they are really missing the boat either way.
