Re: but I won't cry for RANDOM THOUGHTS

by TenuredVulture » Mon Jul 08, 2013 11:24:35

I'm pretty sure damage to other property is under liability. Remember, if the accident isn't your fault, the other party's liability insurance will cover you. Comprehensive would cover damage to your car that is your own fault, theft, and stuff like hail and tree limbs falling on your car.

Given that you need your car, the real advantage to the additional coverage may be the rental that you would get if you lost the use of your car. But as Doc Smooth pointed out, these aren't necessarily catastrophic losses. If you bought your insurance through an agent, it might not be the worst idea to discuss your coverage with the agent. And you never know--I've found it makes sense to shop around on insurance every couple of years--do you have renters insurance? You should. And you can probably get a discount if you combine coverage.
