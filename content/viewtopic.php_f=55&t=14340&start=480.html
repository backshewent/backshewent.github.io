by JFLNYC » Wed Jan 02, 2013 07:54:32

Revere may very well not be the worst bat in the lineup. His offensive WAR last year was exceeded by only Chooch, Rollins and Utley. His OPS may be less than others, but his OBP will be valuable in a lineup that's OBP-challenged which is, of course, the chief argument (along with speed) for batting him at the top of the lineup. If you bat him 8th, you're minimizing his two offensive strengths. If last year is any indication, they're better off batting Brown 8th since he brings nothing special to the lineup in terms of power, speed or OBP.
