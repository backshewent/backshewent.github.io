i don't think halladay gets dealt to anyone. the jays are convinced they will lose lots of fan loyalty and revenue if he goes for anything less than an obviously great return in young talent. they would rather get the draft picks and see him sign a crazy-high deal elsewhere that their fans would find forgiveably unaffordable. plus, with him, there's still a decent chance of having everything come together to put them in the 2010 playoffs. without him, there's no chance. their minimum price really is still the equivalent of happ+drabek+brown/taylor + francisco/mayberry, and nobody's going to pay that and halladay's salary too.

the phils just need to keep enough on the table to make sure he doesn't go to a key rival at a cheap price if someone in the jays organization panics.

i'm pretty ok with this, especially since it sounds like getting him means losing both happ and blanton, and not having cash to sign pedro.
happ+blanton+pedro + (moyer and kendrick in the pen) could well better overall than halladay+moyer+kendrick + (2 vacancies in the pen).
if smuggy signs pedro and keeps halladay out of the nl in 2010, he can still be justifiably smug.

of course, he can be a lot more smug if he turns victorino into a good shortstop prospect.
