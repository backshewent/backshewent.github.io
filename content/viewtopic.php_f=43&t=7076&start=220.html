by TenuredVulture » Sun Nov 30, 2008 16:26:51

This thread has gone off the rails. I think everything that could be said about Amaro's hiring has been said.

Since I lack the power to lock threads I would like to make the following observations.

1. So far, Amaro has not done anything stupid. True, he hasn't done anything yet, but so far there are no actual trades or deals to evaluate.

2. This team did just win a world series, so they must be doing some things right.

3. Lots of people though Gillick was not doing much, and that Amaro was really running the show for at least the last year (which would mean that the worst deals of the Gillick era (Eaton and Abreu) were not on his watch) and that again, this team just won a world series.

4. There do not need to be any major changes in the line up or in the rotation. They need a #4 or better pitcher, and they have not signed a credible left fielder yet.

5. It isn't surprising that Amaro will not be very aggressive this year, and that he will hold his cards close to his vest. It is a mistake to criticizen a GM on the basis of less than half baked rumors dreamed up by so-called sports reporters who are doing little more than making up stuff because there's no real new to report.
