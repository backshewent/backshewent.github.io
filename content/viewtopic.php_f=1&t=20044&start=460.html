by Rockinghorse » Tue Jun 23, 2020 18:51:34

My daily take that sports should be fine. All the pro leagues are doing this right so far. A sound plan crafted in collaboration with the medical community, leadership at the league and club levels (and maybe most importantly, TV networks) who fully understand how much is riding on getting this right. Still several weeks to get teams into their quasi-bubbles and recuperate their positive cases.

Player behavior may be a challenge, but I think things will calm down. It's painfully obvious that the Phillies, Orlando soccer, LSU football, K-State football and maybe other mini-outbreaks stem from going to indoor bars/parties. I doubt college athletes can kick that habit, but the pros for the most part should be able to manage.

Finally, one key ingredient to avoiding outbreaks is to have a ton of money. For testing, staffing, lodging and generally not having to mingle with normal folks. So American sports are on solid ground in that sense.
