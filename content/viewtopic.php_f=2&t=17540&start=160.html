1- my sil and bil have a catering business and they get rave reviews for their brisket. And that's who cooked this on easter. So either their reviewers are just being nice or I just don't like it.

2- Trader Joe's pancakes are the best. Well, I haven't actually EATEN any since i've been LoCa since the wife started buying them, but the packaging and preparation are a breeze. no stupid boxes or inner wrap you need to rip open and try to secure again- just untwist, rip open one side of a sleeve (3 pans), spread on a plate and nuke for 60 seconds. My kid will have like 3-4 plates of those on a good day and it really makes life easier.

3- I will never have mission bbq brisket (nor any brisket), but the mission jalapeno cheddar sausage (i.e. kielbasa) is pretty awesome. Much better than Dickey's.

3a- even better than that is the "cheesy kabonosa" (i.e. "polish slim jim"; i.e. a skinny link of smoked hot kielbasa infused with cheese) from swiacki's is even more awesome. I may start a semi-weekly excursion to pick up 10 or 20 lbs of that as it's great loCA stuff.
