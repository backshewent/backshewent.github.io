Crockpots are hit or miss. Most recipes out there are based on campbell's cream of X soup or onion soup mix -- not much for originality.

I use mine for soup -- cook the meat on the stove, then throw together all the ingredients and let it cook overnight.

I got a cookbook called "not your mother's crockpot cookbook", which has an interesting variety of recipes, although not necessarily food my family would be interested in eating. Two of the recipes I''ve tried from it have been compete flops. Ironically, the best recipe i got from it has been for pizza dough made in the bread machine (the authors also did a bread machine cookbook).
