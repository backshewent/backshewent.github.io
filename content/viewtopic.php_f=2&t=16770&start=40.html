by RichmondPhilsFan » Thu Oct 22, 2015 11:46:10

Napalm wrote:i used to read a lot, and would like to begin making it a habit again. have a stack of about 7-8 books i wanna dip into these next few months. i usually have energies that keep me moving around, tell me how to get in the mindset and set the mood

Get in the routine of reading for 15-20 minutes in bed before you turn out the light. I always did that when I was a kid, but I got out of the habit during law school until I started again about 8 years ago (right when I left private practice). Now it's so ingrained in me that I have trouble going to sleep unless I've read for a while.

Once you get into a book, you'll probably find yourself picking it up at random times during the day instead of turning on the TV or using the computer/phone.

The flip side of that is it can be hard to get into a more dense/difficult book if you're exclusively reading at bed time.

Along those same lines, I love having the kindle app on my phone. I used to be an avid reader and after having kids and too much life going on, I got out of the routine.
