this thread just reminded me of the day of the Abreu trade. I was supposed to go to a graduation party for my friend and her brother(they're twins). My friend and her brother were both "big" Yankees fans, in the sense that they watched their playoff games and tried to convince me that I should sympathize with Yankees fans because Boston fans were mean. Keep in mind this is the early 2000s. Anyway, the Phillies were playing a 1 pm game that day and Bobby was out of the lineup and as you all know his trade was announced during the game. After reading the details of the trade, I immediately realized, like most rational baseball fans, that the trade was horrible for us. CJ fucking Henry and Matt Smith!?!?!

Anyway, I decide I'm not going to let sports affect my RL, so I go to the party. Talk to my friend's brother who declares it an even trade and basically tries to tell me how Bobby isn't very good and CJ Henry is a legit prospect. I tried to explain how Abreu was worth more than what we got alone, even at his age. He ends up cursing me out and takes a swing at me(he's drunk and 18), misses and ends up falling into a pile of random stuff in his garage. I tried to calm him down, tell him we're only discussing a sport, it's not worth being a moron over, etc... he finally calms down. later he pours a beer over my head and i totally kick his ass. the year was 1976 and i was living in texas.
