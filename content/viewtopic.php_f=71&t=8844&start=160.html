The four rounds were Baseball, Food, Music and History.

We got 7/10 on baseball, but it was hard. We were 2nd after round one, with one team out of 20 or so getting 8/10. Third after getting 5/10 on food. Fell into like 10th after music. We were at 31 after three. Two points a question (14, 10, 7 (two artist-song title, two artist only, one song title only)), and first was 42. It was funny because we are good friends with a big bunch of people who were there who were in first after two rounds. There were 11 of them on their team, and they ended up third.
