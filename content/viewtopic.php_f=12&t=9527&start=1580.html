CalvinBall wrote:this board is the best out there. look at all these highlights and pictures that get posted instantly. thank you for those who do it.


seconded

And even more than that, this board has, somehow, made me even more in love with the Phillies than I was when I started reading the Inquirer sports page every day when I was 7, craving any and all games that I could get on TV (we didn't have Prism, so it was all Channel 17 and Sports Channel for me). I was in love then, obsessed, starved for everything Phillies. And now, my life revolves around the Phillies. And it's because of you guys.

I only mentioned this in passing on the off-topic board, but I was at The Fieldhouse at Market East on Saturday and Darren Daulton came in with a couple other guys. I hyperventilated for about 30 seconds but ultimately decided not to bother him while he was out for dinner. My reaction was like a starstruck little teenage girl who spots Zac Efron or something.

But if I walked down the street tomorrow and saw anyone from the core of the past two seasons' teams, from Eric Bruntlett or Scott Eyre to Ryan Howard or Chase Utley, I wouldn't hesitate to say hello. Because while I was a 7-year-old who looked up to Darren Daulton in the early to mid-'90s, now I'm an adult who appreciated this team more than anything else in the world. It's so crazy that a team, an organization of so many people that I have never met and will never know, can bring so much joy to me, my friends, my family, and you guys. Hell, most of us communicate on this board every day and a lot of us will never meet each other. But we're family. And we're family because of the Phillies. And it's nights like tonight that make me realize why it's all so beautiful.
