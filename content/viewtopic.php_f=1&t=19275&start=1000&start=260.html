Just my two cents here. I think if Harper cares about winning, the Phillies won’t need to top an SF offer by all that much in order to get him. The Dodgers are scary (if they go long term) because they would check all the boxes we think we know he wants between money, term, lifestyle, and winning. Philly would have to *blow away* their offer. I’m just not sure the Giants are an appealing destination besides the city they reside in. Bad team that didn’t do much in the way of improving their roster to compete in the near future, and the two most recognizable faces on the team (MadBum and Bochy) are on expiring deals. I’m skeptical that because they were good during the early part of his career that they hold much more clout. They seem to be in a similar spot to the 2014 Phillies in terms of their rebuild. If SF has the highest offer, they’ll get him because that’s what matters, but I dont think he turns down a better deal from Philly to go there like he might for LA. Could be wrong, but that’s my read on it.

I think the Phillies would certainly get a chance to beat a 10 year offer from the Giants, and if they didn’t at that point it becomes entirely their problem.
