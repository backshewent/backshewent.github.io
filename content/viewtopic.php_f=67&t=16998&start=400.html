Get real about the draft --

If it's all about the draft - then people need to look at the draft history and unpack the facts. Then compare to other teams including those with SB appearances and see how many Danny Watkins and Marcus Smith's those teams had??

In Jeff Lurie's 21 drafts as owner he's had 4 top 10 picks:

Donovan #2
Corey Simon #6
Lane Johnson #4
Mike Mamula #7

He's had eight picks between 11-20

Tra Thomas #11
Fletcher Cox #12
Brandon Graham #13
Brod Bunkley #14
Jerome McDougle #15
Shawn Andrews #16
Jeremy Maclin #19
Nelson Agholor #20

So all these busts? Freddie Mitchell,Marcus Smith III, Danny Watkins - they were all in the 20's. And everyone knows that once you are picking in the bottom 1/3 of the 1st round, you are not going to nail it every time. You just aren't.

The worst non-pick of the Lurie Eagles, was passing on Warren Saap for Mike Mamula. 2nd worst - passing Reggie Wayne for Freddie Mitchell.

After Marcus Smith, were a lot of good players - but not OLBs for a 3-4. They messed up. Allen Robinson, Kelvin Benjamin, Derek Carr and Teddy Bridgewater were all still on the board. They were looking Defense - and OLB. But if you scan the 2nd and 3rd rounds and remainder of the 1st after Smith - there weren't a lot of stars or emerging D stars.

I'm not saying the Birds are an upper echelon drafting org, but you have to be realistic about drafts. And blowing a top 10 is different than blowing #25 overall.

2012 was a solid overall draft. 2014 wasn't too bad other than Smith. 2015 I'm still bullish on Agholor.

Not sure what mediots and fans expect??
