by drsmooth » Mon Jan 18, 2010 11:05:23

The Dude wrote:that part is already taken care of, basically my boss and the students share a % on each sale, paid by my company. Then they pay the university 15% out of those earnings. it's a huge pain in the ass, as I'm not a lawyer by any means


Consider this:

You're not Larry Page/Sergei Brin yet, so draw up a Memorandum of Understanding. Lay out in simple terms what all parties understand as far as

a) who created the machine
b) who owns the IP behind it
c) the divvy on sales

then note that the parties have agreed to formalize the terms by X date or by attainment of Y revenues, etc. Then have everyone sign your document.

Getting the basic agreements in writing, with evidence everyone is on board, is first. Legal mumbo-jumbo can come later. Lawyers will tell you different. Eff 'em.
