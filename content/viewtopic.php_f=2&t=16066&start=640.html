Re: PIZZA is the New Orange

by TomatoPie » Thu Oct 25, 2018 07:10:20

BIGA Pizza & Beer in Bryn Mawr is a great spot for Neapolitan pizza. Tried four different ones last night, every one was a winner.

The crust is excellent, typical puffy charred cooks-in-90-seconds stuff. Not better than Vetri or Capofitto but a pretty ideal vehicle for the toppings.

The red sauce is vibrant, the specialty pizzas are especially well chosen and balanced in flavors and textures.

On bad pizza, you sprinkle on grated cheese, oregano, garlic powder, pepper flakes, etc to get some flavor.

On good pizza I sprinkle on salt because well salt makes everything better.

On great pizza - like this - there is no need to enhance. On a Wednesday night, no waiting for a table, either. Happy Hour drink prices until 6pm.
