by Dick Allen HOF » Sun Mar 15, 2009 12:27:22

It has been a while since I have posted here, but I wanted to share a couple of photos from my WBC trip. I am going to all the "Miami" games with my son this week. I'll get in a couple of Phils spring training games later this week.

This was my first trip to Dolphin Stadium. I now understand why the Marlins can't draw many fans. This place is a joke for a MLB venue.


If you have never been to a game involving a Latin team, it is a different (and great) experience. It would best compare to a college football crowd rather than a baseball crowd.


Team Venezuela was pretty pumped after beating the Netherlands.


Sadly, the National Anthem was the high point of the night for Team USA. Puerto Rico beat lumps on us all night long.


Team USA 3rd base coach: Michael Jack Schmidt.
