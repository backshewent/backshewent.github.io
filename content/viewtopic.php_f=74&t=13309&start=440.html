The Republican despair is easy enough to explain:

1) It's generally believed that because of baggage, ideology or both, no non-Romney can win
2) But to get nominated, Romney's had to go too far right (see: anti-contraception) and go negative, tanking his favorability
3) He's also just not a very good politician, committing a seemingly endless sequence of unforced errors all of which reinforce an existing negative perception of him (unprincipled, out of touch rich dude)
4) The economy seems to be getting better

Some, maybe most, candidates improve with practice, though after six years running for president Romney might be as good as he'll get. Either way, at this point I think it'll take one or more disasters for Obama to lose. But that's entirely plausible.
