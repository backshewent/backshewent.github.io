failure to trade either byrd or papelbon for good prospects = F.
failure to trade both for good prospects = maximum grade of C-.

howard getting the chance to start at 1b for the phils next season = max grade of D for offseason as a whole. put out the word that the first team to offer a ptbnl and $5million for him gets him. if he's still around after a month, put the word out that the fist team to offer a ptbnl OR $5million for him gets him.
after a month, give up trying to get anything for him and cut him loose. this team needs to prove they understand sunk costs and can give up on running out guys they made a mistake on hoping to eventually be proven right (i'm worried that will happen with brown or tomas as well).

failure to sign yoan = maximum grade of B- (and that comes only if the right things happen with all of byrd, papelbon, and howard and nothing else is screwed up, like a schillingesque trade of hamels).

a trade of hamels for anything but a slam-dunk prospect package is an automatic F.
a trade of giles for anything but a slam-dunk prospect package is an automatic reset of the maximum to a C-.

a big contract for tomas will earn an automatic "incomplete" grade for the offseason, unless it otherwise earns an automatic f.
