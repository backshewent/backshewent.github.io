Last thing before bed:

I refuted the report earlier that the Phillies consider their chances remote to get Harper, but it is true their confidence is wavering. They still don't fear the Giants. That doesn't mean Harper won't sign with the Giants, I'm not saying that, but in the minds of the Phillies, that isn't the fear. What is causing their confidence to waver is they feel they may drag this out indefinitely until the Dodgers finally decide to match the Phillies offer. They feel now that the Dodgers are involve they're going to keep poking and prodding the Dodgers and hope they finally get them to get to 10/300+. The Phillies internally believed they thought they were starting to make real progress toward a deal before, and the fact that it isn't done is starting to indicate to them that this is just baiting the Dodgers into a record breaking deal until they finally bite. And they know if the Dodgers go there, they're in deep shit, which is why their confidence is wavering.

So if the Phillies are correct in their internal feelings, just cross your fingers the Dodgers don't go there. If a report comes out the Dodgers have gotten a 2nd meeting with Harper, we might be in some trouble. And let's hope that the Phillies up their offer and they just fucking take it so we don't have to worry about it.

But don’t panic. The Phillies still are confident they’ll make the largest offer, which is why their confidence has wavered, not they lost all hope. But it’s kinda like if the Dodgers eventually decide to go to 10/300 what’s stopping them from going all the way? In for a penny in for a pound type of deal.
