by Shore » Mon Jan 28, 2008 23:35:14

There's no WAY this is a good signing.

Feliz. Cannot. Hit. I see people gleeful over 20 homers... the AVERAGE player in MLB hits about 18 per 600 AB.

Feliz had 590 PA last year; given 590 PA, the average player in MLB would:

- make 35 fewer outs
- get 2 more hits
- hit 1 more double
- hit 1 more triple
- hit 4 fewer homers
- score 13 more runs
- drive in 1 fewer run
- walk 22 more times
- steal 7 more bases
- get caught 1 more time
- ground into 3 fewer double plays
- get hit by 4 more pitches
- hit 2 more sac flies
- get 3 more sac bunts
- put up an OPS 61 points higher (.769 to .708) - the same difference as between Grady Sizemore and Reds SS Alex Gonzalez.


Fortunately, we're only paying for his age 33, 34, and (optionally) 35 seasons. Really, those are prime years, no?

you know, this would be a much better deal if we had been targeting groundball pitchers all along. hamels is a flyball pitcher if anything and doesn't allow too many balls in play in the first place. myers also gets a lot of Ks and doesn't allow many balls in play, and isn't a major groundball pitcher or flyball pitcher. moyer is a bit of a flyball pitcher, and eaton is just a gopherball pitcher. we just picked up a flyball/strikeout closer too. there's just not that many groundballs.
