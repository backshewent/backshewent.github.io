everything's more complicated these days. 2 flavors of walk-in clinics:

a) retail clinics, which post prices for all servicres and take cash and most forms of health insurance (think MinuteClinic)

b) urgent care clinics, staffed by MDs, which can do heavier duty stuff and which may-or may not - post their charges for all of their services (Concentra, MedExpress)

tl;dr typically the compenents of care will be billed separately and may or may not be covered separately by your insurance. see that phone # in 6-point type on the front or back of your ID card? call it & confirm.
