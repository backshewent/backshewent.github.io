gr wrote:Warszawa, what's your setup?


I keep it pretty simple.
I have a Pentax K-3 and use three lenses and two filters (CP, ND)
For basic shots I use a Sigma 17-50mm f/2.8
For most telephoto I use a Pentax 60-250mm f/4
When I feel like carrying a heavy load I'll bring my Sigma 150-500mm f/5-6.3
I also have a modified Lumix camera converted to take Infrared.
For post-processing I use Lightroom 5.
