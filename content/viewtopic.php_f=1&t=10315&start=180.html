Studs? No. Some potentially decent players, but no studs IMO. And drafted someone big? No again IMO. Well, their two 6th round picks Labrecquen (C) and Wellwood (LW) had very nice years. I had high hopes for Riopel (G, 5th round), but he didn't perform too well in the AHL early and was sent back to the QMJHL. Morrison (G, 3rd round) regressed. I don't know much about Bertilsson (D, 3rd round). IIRC people have high hopes for him.

They do seem to be making some bigger undrafted signings over the past few months (they are obviously doing this because of Homer's love for trading away draft picks). Most recently Sergei Bobrovsky.

Bobrovsky, 21, spent his entire career with Metallurg Novokuznetsk and is the team's starter goalie for the past three seasons and he always had a saves percentage of more than .900. Playing for one of the weakest KHL teams he faced every night a good amount of shots and showed that he can deal well with pressure.
