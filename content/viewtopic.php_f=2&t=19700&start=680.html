You may not believe that you're in any particular danger from the flu, but even if you contract it and survive with nothing more than a couple days' misery, you put others in danger if you do not self-quarantine while contagious (and going to CVS to get some Tylenol Cold & Flu counts as breaking quarantine). The very young, the elderly, and the immuno-compromised in particular are vulnerable, as well as everyone else who doesn't get flu shots because they do not have access, cannot afford it, or are an idiot.

100% or close to it is an impossible standard for the flu virus as you note, but every percentage point helps protect you, as well as everyone else you could come in contact with while sick.
