Went to Liberty Union Grill in Chester Springs for the "Baked Colorado" pizza featuring elk sausage and hemp seeds.



Yeah, a novelty pie for sure, but it was really good. The elk was chewy and spicy, the crust was thin, crispy, chewy, almost flaky. Unique. Overall really flavorful white pie. The hemp seeds were just hippie decoration.

Nice craft beer selection, I had some brown Kona sumpin from Hawaii with great flavor, toffee overtones, and smooth full body for $6 a pint.
