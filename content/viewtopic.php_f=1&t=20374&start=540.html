by Gimpy » Fri Aug 21, 2020 13:12:12

Marco and Ersan not being re-signed (based on the contracts they ended up getting) was a good move. It would’ve been nice if we successfully replaced them though.

Coming out of our first playoff stint, we needed a two way wing, a couple of shooters, a point guard to run the half-court offense, and a backup big. The only guy closer to the end of his career than the beginning was Redick (it’s not easy to replace an automatic three pointer, but he still had enough mileage to go and he’s not good enough to warrant moving up your timeline for him). We gave away Richaun (who grew into a decent NBA big) and TLC (who is looking like a two way wing), traded Shamet (who was a good shooter while he was here and may have ended up growing into the Redick role), gave up on/got nothing substantive for Fultz (who still isn’t great, but is looking like a decent third guard at least), traded the Bridges pick for a guy that has had a rough go of it so far. We also got good progression from Furk and Shake.

If we just didn’t do any of that and didn’t ship out anyone else our roster now would be:

Embiid / Holmes
Covington / Dario
Bridges / TLC / Thybulle
Redick / Shamet / Korkmaz
Simmons / Fultz / Shake

That team wouldn’t have nearly as much “star power” as our current roster, but it would be good and probably better than why we’ve got. Would’ve been nice if Ed Wade were in charge and stood pat everywhere.
