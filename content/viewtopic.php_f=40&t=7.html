Welcome to BackSheGoes.com!

by VoxOrion » Thu Dec 28, 2006 09:49:53

Welcome!

We hope you enjoy the site, and we can't wait to see it grow.

The intention of BackSheGoes is to create a Phillies baseball community that straddles the line between groupthink of any kind, and challenges every member to contribute to intelligent baseball analysis the best way they know how.

My wife and I are the administrators and proprietors of the site, we're Don and Wendy, or VoxOrion and MrsVox. We wouldn't have made the decision to take on this adventure without the encouragement, assistance, and input of several people.

Running the site is important, but it's an empty vessel without the contributions of you - the most important factor in becoming a fantastic forum.
