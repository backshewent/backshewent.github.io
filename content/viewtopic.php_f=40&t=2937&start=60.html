Jimmy now has 373 total bases on the season. The National League record for total bases by a shortstop in a season is 379 by Ernie Banks in 1958. The only other middle infielders (second basemen included) in the history of the game with more than 380 total bases in a season are Alfonso Soriano in 2002; Rogers Hornsby in 1922, 1925, and 1929; and A-Rod in 1998, 2001, and 2002. Good company.
