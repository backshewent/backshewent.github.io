Former Phillies' minor league catcher Chace Numata was found injured in Erie, PA this morning in a skateboarding accident. According to the linked article surveillance video shows he was riding a motorized skateboard when he fell down and hit his head. He was playing with the Erie Seawolves of the Eastern League, AA affiliate of the Tigers.

Officials are being a bit mum about his condition, which has me worried. The Tigers put out a statement:
“We have been in contact with hospital officials and SeaWolves leadership about his condition,” the statement said. “All of us with the Detroit Tigers ask for Chace to be kept in your thoughts and prayers at this time.”

When people put out statements like that without saying what his condition/prognosis is, that's usually a bad sign.
