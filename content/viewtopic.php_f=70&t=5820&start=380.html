There are some significant advantages and disadvantages to each.

Emailing to a mobile device requires you have a device that can receive email and pay for unlimited data (or pay per MB).

Text messaging can be done on almost any phone for a fraction of the cost.

Email is stored on a server (either Blackberry or somewhere else).

Text is stored nowhere but your phone, so it can easily be discarded and you can go, what what I have no idea what you are talking about.

Email requires more effort to send out than a text message (usually).
