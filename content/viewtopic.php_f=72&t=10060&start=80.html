by Werthless » Thu Mar 04, 2010 00:13:57

So you showed up in the libertarian quadrant?

(Of course you didn't. Because the point of the quiz, upon reflection, was to figure out where along the 2d spectrum someone lies. it can't be done in 10 questions if all the answers neatly sum up the obvious liberal/conservative/etc position. Having 10 liberal answers wouldn't tell you much. It's when you have to decide if the "classic" liberal position is a little too over-the-top to support where the ability to segment the population comes into play. The idea is that some of the answers are going to be libertarian leaning to see if they can ensnare any liberals or conservatives, and some answers will all be different shades of liberal to see if a libertarian or statist is tempted to choose it. )
