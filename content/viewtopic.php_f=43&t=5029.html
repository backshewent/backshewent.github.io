by Drugs Delaney » Sat Apr 05, 2008 16:45:27

The first Phillies game I ever attended was on July 31, 1988. I was a Phillies fan since I was five years old, but it took me until I was 14 to talk my non-baseball fan dad to take me to a game. Highlights of the game include seeing Mike Maddux beat his younger, more successful brother Greg with each brother getting a hit off the other. I also got to see Ricky Jordan's first career double and it ended up being the only game I ever saw Mike Schmidt play in person. He was my hero as a little kid so I'm glad I did get to see him once, even though he did go 0-3 with a walk.
