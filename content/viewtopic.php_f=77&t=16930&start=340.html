by Werthless » Mon Dec 28, 2015 12:46:43

My siblings and spouses do a secret Santa at this point, so we only have to buy for 1 out of 6 people. Makes it a lot easier to just think hard about getting 1 person a good gift.

This year, I did a little more. In addition to my secret santa, I also ordered/made Christmas Tree ornaments for each of my family members. Each ornament referenced something embarrassing from 20 years ago. On each wrapped ornament, I wrote "To: ?" My Mom and Dad opened each present, and held it up, and everyone knew who it was for. For example:

My Dad never reads books, but for some reason, he always brings an old copy of the Catcher in the Rye on vacation. He's probably read the first chapter a dozen times, but never completed the book. I found the cover of his edition, shrank down a version of it I found online, printed it out, and attached it to a business card wrapped around 2"x1" stack of yellow post it notes. Put a hook on it, and the end result was a mini Catcher in the Rye booklet.

I used a bunch of dollhouse parts for other ornaments. For example, we've teased my sister about spilling root beer on herself at a restaurant when she was 6 years old. I mounted this to a small piece of wood. Big success, highly recommended.
