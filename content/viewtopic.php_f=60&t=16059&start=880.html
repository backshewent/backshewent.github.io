The drag of it all, is that they didn't capitalize as much on 2010 and 2011. A 2nd WS win say in 2011, with 3 apperances in 4 years and we'd have a lot more to bouy us in these dark years.

I would say this ... the reason for new leadership at the top, is that over the next couple years the books will get cleared of some of the mega-salaries, and a GM with better instincts and vision, and use of funds -- could compensate for lack of draft prospects coming in to their own, by assembling a team smartly. Maybe one of the last 2 drafts and next 2-3 to come will yield something to get us out of the bottom, and what a bottom. The order of magnitude with which we were worse than the other bad clubs, is shocking.

So odd, that the 76 Phils of 101 wins, and the 11 phils of 102 wins, could not reach the WS.

You could say the Phils of the mid -late 70's that got good was also from good drafts and developing their own talent...they sort of did the same thing at the end of that run, trying to bring in veterans (Rose, Morgan, Perez, Dejesus, Matthews, Bradley, etc) rather than use the last of their own talent like Sandberg and Franco.
