I think I just visited the twilight zone, or at least candid camera....

went to the pizza stand at the plymouth meeting mall. a place we've been going to for YEARS. My kid always gets the same thing. This time he wasn't there, but I was picking up his dinner.

"2 slices with extra cheese, please". I get a puzzled look from the guy behind the counter. "You mean you want a personal pizza with extra cheese? Because all we have in sliced is plain cheese". "No", I continue. "You just take two slices, put some more cheese on the top, then pop it in the oven for a minute or two until it melts". "OH!" he says. "You mean this??", as he picks up the shaker of parmesan from the counter. "No", I say again. "The same mozzarella you use when you make the pizza". Counter guy turns to look at baker guy, who apparently doesn't speak any english. He shrugs the "I have no idea " shrug, then picks up two slices and puts them in the oven. The guy tells me how much it is, I pay and wait. Baker guy takes the slices out, puts them in a box, and hands it to me. Uber eats guy- who had arrived in the middle of all this- then hands me the parmesan and says "I didn't see him put any extra cheese on them". "No thanks, I wanted mozzarella".


Now I'm not kidding when I say we have gone to this place for years and years on and off. Usually the owner is working, but not this time. I wonder if it changed hands again. I mean, who doesn't know how to prepare slices to order???
