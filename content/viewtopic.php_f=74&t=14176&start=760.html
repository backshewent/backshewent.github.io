by Bill McNeal » Fri Oct 12, 2012 21:26:33

BoA's iPhone app lets you deposit checks as well. I work in banking and I NEVER step foot in a branch. Between ATMs, online banking and smartphone apps, there is pretty much no need to ever go. The only reason I go to the branch is to pick up my mail because I can't have certain things delivered to my house.

Also, Jamie, talk to an accountant about that 401k thing. I know there are benefits and things you can use for home purchase, but you really should think very hard about taking it out if the 401k. The money you invest in your 20's is the most important, it will accrue interst for the next 40 years, and the interest that you earn on it accruing interst during that time as well. I know you say you are going to pay it back, but be careful, owning a home is expensive and it might sound easy now to sock away 450/month (on top of regular 401k contributions) but what happens when the water heater breaks? Or the basement floods? Not saying not to do it, just consider all of the issues.
