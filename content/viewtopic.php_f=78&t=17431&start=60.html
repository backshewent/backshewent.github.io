When you request a return the seller has an option of prepaying a return shipping label and waiting until the item gets back to them to refund the money or just give a straight full refund and tell you to keep it. Normally it should let you know which option they picked, but if they aren't responding to you my guess is they just decided to give you the refund and let you keep the item. If its something that was just a few dollars (less than $10-$15) that's normally what I do. You can probably contact eBay to ask them what the deal is if you want to know for sure.
