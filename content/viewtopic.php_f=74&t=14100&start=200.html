However, if there were high technology jobs in Northern Lancaster, the pay would be normalized or the cost of living much higher.

When I occasionally visit my parents I notice that groceries are 10-25% cheaper. Gas cost less. Housing is around 1/3 netting property with usable land. So what is middle class income? It is not definable for a diverse country with so much land with varied Industries. I spent time in Nebraska and would say that $60k annually could be a lot of scratch in Lincoln, but what would that buy you in Boston?
