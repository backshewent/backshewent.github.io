Re: 2016 Draft: Phillies picks, bios, signings, etc.

by thephan » Thu Jun 23, 2016 08:50:10

So the total up becomes important to see if any flyer picks can get done. I looked it up to refresh the pool and penalty stuff. So as a refresher for anyone like me who cannot remember, here is the deal:

    - All bonus money directed toward a player selected in the Top 10 rounds of the draft counts against a team’s bonus pool
    - Any bonus money that exceeds $100K to players selected in rounds 11 through 40 counts against the bonus pool
    - Penalties for exceeding by 0 to 5 percent: 75 percent tax on the overage.
    - Penalties for exceeding by 5 to 10 percent: 75 percent tax on the overage plus the loss of a first-round pick in the following year’s draft.
    - Penalties for exceeding by 10 to 15 percent: 100 percent tax on the overage plus the loss of a first- and second-round pick in the following year’s draft.
    - Penalties for exceeding by more than 15 percent: 100 percent tax on the overage plus the lost of a first-round pick in the following two drafts.

I am not sure it matters, but there it is.
