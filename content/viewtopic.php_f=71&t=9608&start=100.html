Not to get all maudlin, but... my sense has been that this playoff run is probably the end, for me, of 20 years of hideously protracted post-adolescence. My wife and I are hoping that we'll be parents, or about to become parents, a year or so from now. That means, at least for awhile, no more freedom to spend hours a day, close to every day, thinking about/writing about/obsessing over baseball, getting drunk and yelling at the TV and/or computer, spending hundreds of bucks for a stray World Series ticket, belting myself in for the emotional rollercoaster ride the Phillies always deliver, etc. It'll likely never be the "most important thing" again, or at least not until around when Domonic Brown and Kyle Drabek are grizzled old vets bringing their careers to a close.

So it would be nice to win this fucking Series and go out on top.
