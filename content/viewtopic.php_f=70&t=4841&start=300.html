by MrsVox » Tue Apr 01, 2008 15:35:00

It's crazy overpriced, and annoyingly overcrowded.

However, I went there looking for (a) low-sodium foods, and (b) to generally check out the bulk foods section, because I heard you can get some deals there.

Overall, in my search for low sodium foods over the past week, I've found that generics and organic products are higher in sodium content than name-brand foods. I did make some good finds, including no salt potato chips, which isn't healthy (chips) but will make the person I was shopping for very happy. I also found some cookies than were pretty good too.

The bulk foods that were pre-packed were good prices, for things like almonds, dried berries. And the frozen berries seemed to be the best prices I've seen (my comparisons have been between Whole Foods, Trader Joes, and my local ShopRite).

So, while (thankfully) I won't be doing my weekly shopping at WF, I will probably end up going once or twice a month to pick up some things, same with trader Joes.
