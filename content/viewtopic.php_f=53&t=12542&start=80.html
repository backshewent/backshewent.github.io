I do think that a video replay ref is long overdue. It's not hard to implement, and the time taken in checking with ref on rugby matches (and bear in mind how quickly they have to review fairly complex situations regarding trys, illegal handling of players and the like) seems to be not much more than a couple of minutes.

I don't think either team getting the opportunity to appeal would be a solution, though.
