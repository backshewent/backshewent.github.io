by JFLNYC » Tue Mar 18, 2014 14:04:38

Ripping BD's to mkv's is easy with makemkv. There's no loss of quality and you can choose which titles (so you can save space and just rip the main title, if you want). They then can be played with various media players, e.g., a WDTV Live or Roku (with Plex) (but an AppleTV must be jailbroken) or in Windows with Arcsoft's TotalMediaTheatre (which is the best software player, IMO). If you want to rip for software playback, you can also use Slysoft's AnyDVD HD. And if you're looking to rip your BD's to mp4/m4v files, of course Handbrake is your best bet.

The phone mount portion is a micro suction cup and also has 2 magnets in it. My phone(nexus 5) has two magnets in it that work very well with the mount. Other phones i've been told stick to the surface very well. Its a wireless QI charger as well. so i just hop in the car and put the phone on the mount. no sliding in and out. easy to arrange from portrait to landscape. comes with a USB car charger that has two USB plugs(a 2amp and a 1amp). The phone showed no signs of slipping or move when attached to the mount. the magnet and suction surface works perfectly.

its kinda pricy but the convenience to me is worth it.
