But what is a combination and how does one combination differ from another? Is it a bunch or numbers? And what do those numbers mean?

It's a combination of four numbers in a specific order. Think of how many of these you can make with 14 different numbers, and imagine how long a list of a team's potential combinations are:

1-2-3-4
1-3-2-4
1-2-4-3

No numbers repeat in a combination. They use 14 different numbers for the 14 different teams, but it's a way of making the process completely random. And as I said above, it's a way of using a normal sized machine instead of trying to shove 30,000 team logo balls into one machine.

Also, only one team is assigned each combo. So there's a team that has 1-2-3-4 IN THAT ORDER, but no other team has it.
