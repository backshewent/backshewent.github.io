I wonder if it would make sense to have an airport in say the middle of the country in a more or less rural area that was pretty much exclusively a hub. Few passenger trips would originate or terminate there. Such a facility would need substantially less infrastructure in terms of road/rail connections, there would be little NIMBY objection, expanding operations would be pretty easy. Presumably, managing air traffic would be easier. You'd choose a place in Texas or Arkansas or Mississippi something so winter weather would rarely be an issue.
