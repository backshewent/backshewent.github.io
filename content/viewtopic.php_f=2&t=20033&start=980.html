also, it's especially important in this day and age to document your accounts. In the "old days", you would just watch the deceased's mail for statements and stuff like that, but with everything electronic these days, that usually won't happen. When I took over my mom's finances, due to her dementia she really had no idea what was where, but by monitoring the mail I figured it all out.

I recently got this book which guides you into filling in all of your account info and such. maybe i'll actually get around to filling it out! prime example- my robinhood account. if i got hit by a bus tomorrow, nobody would think to look there for funds.
