Blue Jays Reveal Halladay Intentions


Friday, July 24, 2009

RHP Roy Halladay's desire to test the free agent market after the 2010 season is the primary reason that the Blue Jays are trying to trade their staff ace, GM J.P. Ricciardi said Thursday.

"That's the whole reason we're going down this avenue," Ricciardi said, hours after revealing Halladay's intentions in a pregame chat with a Toronto radio station.

Baseball's non-waiver trade deadline is July 31. Ricciardi said July 6 he would field offers for Halladay, who has a full no-trade clause. The Jays have set a loose deadline of July 28 to make a deal.
- Washington Post
