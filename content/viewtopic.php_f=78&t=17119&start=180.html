smitty wrote:Going to Lehigh Valley game tonight with bro in law and nephew.


Beautiful night in Allentown. Very nice stadium. Stayed for 13 1/2 innings. Missed the rousing finish.

Toledo had to use a catcher as a pitcher and it didn't work.

Nick Williams went 0-7 with four Ks. He is a very wild swinger. Needs plate discipline.

Knapp and Perkins didn't start. Knapp had two doubles late in the game after going in as a pinch runner (and getting thrown out at third on a Alonso double!!). Perkins was a pinch runner and scored late.

Logan Moore looks very good behind the plate but he's awful at the plate.

Buchanan got the start and was peppered for eight hits but only one run in seven IP. He was not impressive. Set up man Garcia threw heat. 97-101!

Closer Mujica wasn't very impressive. Fastball barely cracked 90. Was mostly in the high 80s.
