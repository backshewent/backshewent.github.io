My wife made a friend and we went to dinner tonight at their house, they have kids at comparable ages to our kids plus a 13 year old, we had a good time hanging out drinking and chatting. They just moved here from New Hampshire and are getting acclimated to the area. Was talking to the husband and he's telling me how he was in Dallas a few weeks ago for work and eating dinner at a nice restaurant and Jerry Jones was next to him and he said hey to him. Jerry asked him where he was from and he said outside Philly and then Jerry asked him if he was an Eagles fan he replied no he's a bills fan and Jerry said "good, I fucking hate the eagles." Then the guy tells me "my cousin works for the NFL and talks to owners regularly, I didn't tell Jerry that, bit he definitely knows my cousin, I just didn't want to be that guy". I didn't really say anything after that, then later on in the evening he's telling me how his cousin is taking his dad to the super bowl this year with his tickets from work, but his dad wasn't sure about it. I was like oh that's awesome, you've gotta do that if you get the chance! He says I know, that's what I told my dad, you never know if Roger is going to go on to something else so do it while he works there. So I said "lol, yeah Cuz Goodell probably isn't gonna have those tickets next year!" And he says "actually yeah, Roger Goodell is my cousin".

Mind blown. He then proceeds to tell me all about the Goodell family and how they grew up together. So in the past month my wife has made friends with the sister of the Arizona Cardinals vp of PR and Roger Goodell's cousin.
