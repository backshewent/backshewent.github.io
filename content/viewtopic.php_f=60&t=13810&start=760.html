One thing that's fun about making fun of Scouty talk is understanding that they don't agree among themselves all the time. All of them are wrong a lot. It's part of the game.

I respect and love to read and listen to my insider Scouty guys. They do great work. They are good at what they do. I take what they say and write seriously. But it's important to understand there are always exceptions and outliers.
