BigEd76 wrote:TSN's off-season game plan for the Flyers

TLDR version:
-- Get a head coach
-- Buy out Lecavalier
-- Trade Luke Schenn
-- Re-sign Del Zotto
-- Bring back Justin Williams and also sign Jarret Stoll and Colin McDonald
-- Sign Jhonas Enroth to replace Emery
-- Give Morin, Ghost and Alt a shot at making the team
-- Draft the #1 Euro skater (Rantanen), #5 NA skater (Crouse) or #11 NA skater (Barzal) with the #7 pick

Obviously getting a HC is priority one. We all know that Berube was death on Vinny. The coach may be able to get something out of Vinny, Umberger and AMac so the buyout may be premature. I'm into moving Schenn. In fact I'd move both Schenns and Grossmann if possible this off season. Signing Williams may have merit but I'd pass on Stoll and McDonald. Better to re-sign White. Enroth would be a decent pick up. Adding young defenseman is going to happen. Drafting Crouse would fit. If he's gone take Rantanen.
Look for big surprise moves by Hextall. Adding a #1 defenseman and perhaps more. Hexy with a new coach may shock the fans with the make over that takes place. I'm really looking forward to what happens over the summer. This team could really improve itself. There is a lot of talent on the club and in the system. That's why Berube was fired.
