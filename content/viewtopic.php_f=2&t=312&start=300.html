I'm on vacation, so this isn't "everyday", so I didn't know where else to put this.

Michelle and I are at the Westin Diplomat, which we got for $60/night on Priceline. Nice staying at a resort for the price of a Days Inn.

Anyway, the Braves were staying here, they just checked out a few hours ago. I know this because as I was going down to the pool, I saw a player's luggage with "Atlanta Braves" on the luggage ticket (I didn't recognize the name, just a middle reliever/AAA callup I guess).

So, I get down to the lobby, and I look, and there's a few guys sitting around a table. One of them is definitely Billy Wagner.

"Wagner!" I yell. He looks at me, holding a beach umbrella and beach chair around my shoulder and sunglasses in my face. "Yeah?" he says.

"We actually liked you in Philly when you where there. You just can't. take. a. joke."

Then I put on my sunglasses CSI Miami-style and walked away (okay, the sunglass thing isn't true, but the rest is).

Later on, Chipper Jones is at the ritzy pool bar. Jayson Heyward and a lady friend where next to us on the beach. They left early, presumably when they were all vacating the premises.
