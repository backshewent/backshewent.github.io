Yep. I linked to Will Carroll's comments on that earlier.

When he really was struggling, they completely shut him down, and while he was rehabbing he studied lots of old video and corrected the mechanical flaws in his delivery.

ERA+ of 143 this year. The Indians are 25th in the bigs in Defensive Efficiency, the Phillies are 11th, so he gets a bump there. His home/road splits aren't drastic at all (3.00 at home, 3.27 road), and lefties have a .511 OPS against him this year. His BABIP against RHB is .345, thats slightly high.

He has two starts this year where he's been bombed (7 ER both times), one of them in the first start of the year. If you remove his first start, his line is

147 IP - 2.82 ERA - 102 K - 32 BB

He doesn't walk anyone and is murder on lefties. Hes gonna be juuuust fine.
