by td11 » Fri Mar 06, 2015 11:56:58

Napalm wrote:td, what growing up in india like


i have to remind myself from time to time that i've actually spent more of my conscious like in the USA now. i haven't even been back since 2000 so now i feel kind of weirdly distanced from my "homeland." we barely have any strong family ties remaining either, so that furthers that feeling of alienation. it's weird man

all that said, i would say that as a 13 year old kid leaving the country, my main takeaways were that school blew (it was extremely hard and no extra-curriculars to speak of), there is a lot poverty all around you all the time, the food is delicious, it smells like something all the time, everything was extremely cheap, it was always hot and humid, bad traffic, pollution, stray animals everywhere, and cricket.

most of my lived experience was limited to a couple states in the east, though. i want to go back and visit many different places in the north, south, and west still. i never even seen the Taj Mahal or been to Mumbai
