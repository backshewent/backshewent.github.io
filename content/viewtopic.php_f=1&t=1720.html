by Phight On! » Sat May 05, 2007 16:54:02

It's next to impossible to handicap a 20 horse field because the horse who gets the best trip wins (not necessarily the best horse). The only thing you can really do is throw out the turds, look for the best betting values on the board, and cross your fingers. With that being said, the two most impressive prep efforts (IMO anyway) were by #16 Circular Quay in the Louisiana and #2 Curlin in the Arkansas. At 12-1 and 6-1 respectively they offer tremendous value and I will be betting them heavily. Also the best thing that could have happened for Street Sense bettors happened- he lost his final prep race (the Bluegrass) which was run on the new Polytrack surface. Look a little deeper and you will see that Street Sense is a completely different horse on natural dirt. His last two Byers on dirt were 108 and 102. He'll be in my exotics for sure and 5-1 is a great price for a horse of his caliber.

To Win/Exactas/Trifectas: #2 Curlin, #7 Street Sense, #9 NoBiz Like Showbiz, #16 Circular Quay

Live Long Shots: #3 Zanjero, #18 Any Given Saturday, #20 Great Hunter

Flush Them: #1 Sedgefield, #4 Speed in May, #5 Imawildandcrazyguy, #9 Liquidity, #10 Teuflesberg, #11 Bwana Bull, & #13 Sam P

Good luck!
