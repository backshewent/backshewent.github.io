by TheDude24 » Wed Sep 30, 2009 12:00:30

Good comedy podcasts I regularly listen to:

"Comedy Death Ray"- Interviews and conversations from host Scott Aukerman with stand-up comics, actors, and comedy writers. Between each segment they play new and classic novelty songs. Some guests have been Sarah Silverman, Patton Oswalt, Weird Al Yankovic, Andy Samberg, Zach Galifianakis, Jon Hamm, Howard Kremer. Recently the show has featured a game called "Would You Rather".

"I Love Movies with Doug Benson" - Kind of a sister podcast to Comedy Death Ray. Doug talks movies (though he gets off topic occasionally) with his guests, also actors and comedians. Half the show is reserved for movie-related party games.

"WTF with Marc Maron" - This is a new podcast that just started this month, and has a new episode twice a week. Marc starts out neurotically ranting about politics, social issues, or his own hang-ups and then talks to a famous guest about something much lighter. From the way he talks, Marc might remind you of the more famous Richard Lewis.
