slugsrbad wrote:Finished the Mistborn series last night. Really enjoyed it. Once again, Richmond, your recommendations are spot on.

What's next up for you?


I have two holds outstanding on political books, on the Fantasy side I may continue to the next Mistborn trilogy I haven't decided yet. Does it answer some of the questions left from the first trilogy (e.g. the two unknown metals)?

Yeah, it goes into a lot more detail. I'll warn you though--it's not a trilogy (yet). "Alloy of Law" is a standalone. The next two Wax and Wayne books are the first two books in that trilogy. The last isn't due until 2019 (I think). It took me a while to get into those books. The first is okay, but didn't do a ton for me when I read it right after finishing the original Mistborn trilogy. I liked the next two books much better, probably because they get into a lot more detail on the new version of Scadriel and Harmony's relationship with it.

After he finishes that trilogy, which is essentially set in Scadriel's Industrial Revolution era, Sanderson has said that he'll eventually get around to doing a trilogy set in the space travel era for that world. Now that'll be a LOT different from his other stuff.
