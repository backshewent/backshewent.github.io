I find that cars.com and autotrader.com are about equivalent.

Your ideal car - in your price range - would be one of the reliable brands (yeah, Japanese) and then screen for low(ish) mileage. Sometimes taking an older model year as a tradeoff for fewer miles is a good thing.

Autotrader also tells you when it's a "Great Price" for that car/condition.

Mileage and a clean CarFax are key issues.

I did a search for Hondas Toyotas Mazdas Mitsubishis model year 2013-2016 $6k-$10k under 75k miles. PLenty of nice cars like these:
