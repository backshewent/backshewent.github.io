TenuredVulture wrote:Entergy, our electricity provider, just installed an ecobee thermostat for us. Pretty excited about being able to control the thermostat from my phone.

There are now wifi thermostats with smartphone connectivity available for less than $100. The higher end ones add other benefit on type. For example, they don't require someone to program a schedule, since they will learn from own adjustments/preferences. Or, they have multi-room sensors. Personally, I'm very happy with my programmable version that I can alter from my smartphone.

I'm just pointing out a misconception that my sister had, that you need to pay $200 for a thermostat if you wanted to control it from your phone.
