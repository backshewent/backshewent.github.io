The Dude wrote:MAKE YOUR OWN PIZZA IT'S FUN


the prep work can be a bit of a pain, especially if you choose to make your own dough. i think the key is to make everything from scratch and using quality ingredients.

here is my favorite...

1. toss and stretch the dough relatively thin, not quite ny style, but definitely not dominos/pj/pizza hut thick.
2. brush on a liberal coating of olive oil.
3. dice up roma tomatoes and mix in some diced garlic and cover the dough.
4. cover with real 100% mozzarella
5. top with basil (occasionally, i'll add onion + peppers)
6. bake... and if you don't have a pizza stone, definitely get one.

btw... yeah worked in a few pizza places while in school.
