Actually, the Supreme Court said in Roe v Wade that the state has a legitimate interest in protecting prenatal life.

Yes it did. But it said it has no legitimate right to act to protect prenatal life in the first trimester. It also suggested that the time at which stricter regulation is allowable would move into the second trimester as medical advances increased the viability outside the uterus of later 2nd trimester preemies.
