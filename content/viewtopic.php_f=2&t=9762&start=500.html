Re: Photography II

by phatj » Fri May 02, 2014 12:30:54

Strongly recommend dpreview.com for digital camera reviews (they focus mostly on interchangeable-lens cameras like this). Here's their review of the E-PM2: http://www.dpreview.com/reviews/olympus-e-pm2

I cannot comment on that specific camera, but I own the original Micro Four Thirds camera (Panasonic Lumix G1), so I have some experience with the format. The format is not ideal for action photography, because the autofocus is relatively slow compared to SLRs. That being said, huge strides have been made in autofocus performance since my camera came out (late 2008) so it may well be that that camera is plenty good enough for you. Still image quality is generally excellent, and the video mode is supposed to be good as well.
