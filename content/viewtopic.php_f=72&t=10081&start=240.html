Assuming it's literal, Ft. Worth, while part of the DFW metroplex, is not Dallas. Dallas is Cotton and thus Southern while Ft. Worth is Cattle, and thus Western. They are very different in feel.

People from Dallas hate this, but in recent years, thanks in part to Bass family money, Ft. Worth might be the more cultured of the two cities. In addition, the cultural stuff in Ft. Worth is far more accessible to someone of limited means--several small but top notch art museums are free. Forth Worth is a pleasant looking city, probably pretty inexpensive in terms of housing and such, with a fair amount of greenery.

Summers are hot. Winters mild. Texas economy as a whole substantially better than the rest of the country.
