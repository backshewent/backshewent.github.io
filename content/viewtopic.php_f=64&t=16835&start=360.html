It's a gamble. If you sign those guys and they play well, you'll probably get back more value than the draft picks, especially given how few draft picks work out. But if you sign those guys and they get injured or have bad years, you can cripple your farm system. I think it's worth the gamble in this case because the farm looks pretty good right now and the potential payoff is so great. But I think it depends on your level of risk aversion and how sure you think you are about the signed player(s) doing well.
