Re: Don't Fact Check Me, Bro: The Politics Thread

by dajafi » Thu Sep 20, 2012 11:05:21

The notion that voters get more conservative/Republican as they age seems to me based in the idea that as you acquire more wealth, you're more open to the arguments of the party that has a more sympathetic view toward protecting the assets and prerogatives of the wealthy. This isn't illogical, but it overlooks the finding of voluminous research (Paul and jh can substantiate, I'm sure) that people don't strictly vote their economic self-interest. Hence the cultural elites supporting liberals and lower middle class whites voting Republican.

I suspect it also might be an artifact from a time when the parties weren't as far apart as they are now. Someone born in, say, 1915 could have very comfortably voted for FDR, Ike, LBJ and Nixon without feeling s/he'd changed views. Their disagreements were mostly matters of degree; now it's basic philosophical digression.
