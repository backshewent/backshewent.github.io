It's depends on what kind of vacationers you are, whether you have the need to "do stuff" or just chill. Vox and I used to go to the poconos every September before kids, and then in the late summer after kids. It was a timeshare (not Great Wolf, but the other one with the water stuff now, where you pay the stupid quarter toll to get in...) and a townhome setup. We would open up all the windows and let the cool air in, see the leaves start to turn, go swimming in the pool, and read and play video games.

But, for us, it was partly the large space, and free time that made the vacation. The last time we went (with the kids), the same place was obviously in disrepair, and the pool was dirty from over use.

We had the same setup at williamsburg (again, time share). I'd probably pick W'burg if given the choice today.
