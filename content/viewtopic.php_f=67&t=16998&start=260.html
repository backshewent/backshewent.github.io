"It's going to be a little different every year," said Schwartz. "Next year it'll be different than this year. Two years from now it'll be different because we'll have different players. I think the coach's job is to find the best position to put those players in. The common denominators you'll see, traditionally, we're going to be tough. We're going to be physical. We're going to attack. We're going to put pressure on the quarterback. I think those are the things that carry over from year to year, as sort of our starting point."

Schwartz noted that his scheme will fit the players.

"I looked at the players," said Schwartz. "I looked at just about every game Philadelphia played last year. I remember playing these guys a bunch of times, and looked at a lot of these players coming out of college, and that was part of the attraction here. I think we really have some good defensive players. I think we can help them out, put them in maybe in a little bit different position and emphasize their strengths a little bit more."
