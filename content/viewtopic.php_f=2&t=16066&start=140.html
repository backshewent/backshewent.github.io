by TomatoPie » Mon Jul 06, 2015 11:30:07

Rustica was very good, but not as good as I expected/hoped.

I really wanted to try the scrapple & egg pie, but Mrs. TP balked. We got half pepperoni, half andouille sausage.

The crust was very good, despite being cooked on a screen. Good flavor and texture, especially at the bone.

The sauce was pretty mild, and got lost under the sea of cheese. This pie would have been a lot better with half the cheese. The first half of each slice was limp from the weight and moisture riding on it.

Great neighborhood pie, but not in the same league as Pizzeria Vetri, Barbuzzo, Pizza Brain.
