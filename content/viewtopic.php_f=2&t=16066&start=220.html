CalvinBall wrote:felt that the crust was kinda chewy. isnt that a big no no in your book tp?


I'm partial to a crisp outer edge, but chewy al dentiness inside is fine.

A Neapolitan like this is always gonna be softer than a coal-fired pie like Anthony's or an old-fashioned tomato pie like Gennaro's.

It's not my favorite style of pie but it's authentic and legit, and miles better than the generic stuff churned out in most places.

The real magic takes place at La Porta, which is some strange alchemy toward a tomato pie - Neapolitan hybrid.
