Okay, technically I am a colored girl, so I was totally looking forward to catching Tyler Perry's new film, For Colored Girls. Now the movie isn't bad, but it is missing some things that I've come to expect from a movie about colored women. First off, where the hell was Angela Bassett? This is the first black-girls-are-doin'-it-on-their-own movie that she wasn't a part of. What's Love Got To Do With It, How Stella Got Her Groove Back, and Waiting to Exhale were religious experiences for me. I totally missed her in this. On the plus side the movie does star Janet - Ms. Jackson if you're nasty - BUT she doesn't look like Janet Jackson and she doesn't sound like Janet Jackson, gone is her signature Michael Jackson voice. Weird. I'd say the most disappointing aspect of this movie is that it doesn't have a killer soundtrack. There is no awesome Mary J. Blige song about dumping some deadbeat man because he's all drama. There isn't even a song from Janet! Soundtracks are the best part of movies about black girls (hello, Dream Girls?) so the fact that there is not one song in this movie that makes me want to wave a finger is let down. Ugh, this movie could have been so much better!
Fine. You wanna act like you're two? I'll act like I'm one.
