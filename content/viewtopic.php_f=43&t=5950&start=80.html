Random stuff:

September 3, 2007: The Mets and Pedro Martinez beat the Reds 10-4 for their fourth consecutive win. The following day they would win their fifth consecutive game and go on to put together a streak of 10 out of 12 that ended September 12, at which point they were 21 games over .500. At that point they were 7 games ahead of the Phils.

Similarly to this year the Mets only played the Phillies 3 times in September 2007, all at Shea, and of course they lost all 3 (9/14-9/16).

The Mets finished up 2007 against the dreck of the NL east, playing the Nats and Fish 13 out of 14 games. They went 5-9 during this stretch. The 2008 Mets continue this trend of playing well against good teams and down to the level of not so good teams, which is great since they only play 4 tough games the rest of the way (Cubs at Shea 9/22-9/25).
