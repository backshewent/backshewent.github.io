I haven't tried a USB switch, but I do have a real KVM switch. I actually want to maintain two monitors (actually three, because I have one dedicated to those emergency dispatch pages that I sometimes quote), because I frequently will be keeping an eye on something from work while surfing BSG. It's just the keyboards that are causing a real estate issue- I hate having one (or both) on the desktop surface, and also is suboptimal for typing posture also. Especially at night- after I've powered the laptop off, it's dumb having the the kbd taking up space.

I've still got a bunch of tweaks to make in my new setup- my dispatch monitor doesn't fit under the hutch and is wasting valuable workspace, and I can't figure out where to put my TV. Right now I'm using the 24" "work" monitor hooked up to the tivo mini to watch the game, which should do in the short term, but the lack of a remote (for volume control/mute purposes) make it no good for a long-term solution.
