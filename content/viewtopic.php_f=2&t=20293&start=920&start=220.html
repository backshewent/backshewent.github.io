We use InstaCart/Publix for delivery down here. If you really want to make sure you get what you want it’s kind of a PITA. First, you’ve got to go through each item in your cart and pick a replacement (or none) or you never know what they’re going to pick. Second, once they start shopping you have to be available and glued to your cell phone because they’ll chat when they’re making a substitution. Also sometimes you get a shopper who doesn’t know the store well so you literally have to tell them exactly where to look for a specific item which they’ll tell you is not in stock but which you know is.
