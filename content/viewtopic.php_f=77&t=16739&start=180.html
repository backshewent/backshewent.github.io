pacino wrote:Bucky, are you dying? Why are you so interested in family history now?


we're all dying, son. Making it take as long as possible is the goal.

Most of my "known" relatives growing up were from my mother's side- my maternal grandmother's side, to be specific. And pretty much everyone in Rox/Yunk is a cousin of some sorts. I've always wanted to figure out how I'm related to a lot of people whom I was told was a "cousin". Specifically I knew very little about my maternal grandmother's side (who apparently grew up in Tacony (?) on Milnor Streets, near where now is apparently the "Bada Bing Sports Bar".) So a dude I grew up with was doing all this research and gave me a bunch of listing of stuff he found out about my family (one of his relatives married on of my dad's relatives somewhere along the line).

So now it's a pretty cool research project. It's fun trying to track things down!
