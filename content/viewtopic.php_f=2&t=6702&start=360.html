Polar Bear Phan wrote:I'm looking at smaller hatchbacks as possible replacements (e.g. Versa, Fit, Spark).

The Spark is considerably smaller than the other two you mention, FWIW. You might check out the Toyota Yaris. It's bigger than the Spark but smaller than the Versa or Fit.

Why is it you're looking for a small car particularly? Gas mileage? Maneuverability in the city? Cost?

My wife and I went looking at cars like that a year ago because we wanted something with good mileage - our Mazda5, while good on gas compared to full-size minivans, only gets about 25 if we're lucky. We looked at the Versa, Fit, Yaris... and ended up with a used Prius. It's a bit bigger than the others so if small size in and of itself is important to you, it might not be the best option, but you can't beat the mileage. And the reliability record of Prii is outstanding, I would not hesitate to buy used.
