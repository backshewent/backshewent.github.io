Well, I don't drink so therefore I don't drink and post, but this reminds me that something similar almost did happen a while back. Several months ago I took some shrooms. It was a weird trip and it turned a bit dark at times. I was feeling anxious and at one point I decided to try to read BSG for some reason. As you can imagine it was more strange than usual while I was in that state, and I wasn't sure if what I was reading was really there or not, and I was starting to feel real uncomfortable with how confused I was about everything. I was considering posting a bunch of stuff at the time, but I still had enough sense to realize I would regret it, although I'm sure you all would have gotten a kick out of it. Anyway, I distinctly remember reading this post by Trent Steele in the random thoughts thread.

Trent Steele wrote:"She is wearing my grandmother's Holocaust ring."

"I didn't know they gave out rings at the Holocaust."


I remember it scared the crap out of me and I quickly closed my laptop and went back to trying to figure out how to operate my DVD player so that I could watch my Planet Earth DVDs. After who knows how long, I finally figured out how to use the DVD player before realizing that I don't even own Planet Earth DVDs and never did.

Alright, so it wasn't exactly a Phight On! type drug story, and I can assure you more interesting things did happen, but this happened to involve you guys so I figured I'd let you in on it.
