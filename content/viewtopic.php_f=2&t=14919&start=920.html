Was at softball yesterday and this dog wanders into the park. Nobody knows who she is or who her human is. Since the park is just off of a busy road, we didn't want her to just continue to roam free so we leashed her up and watched her until the games were over. We hoped that someone would come looking her but nobody did. A buddy and I decided to walk her and hopefully, she would lead us to her house. The dog left the park and turned right. We walked her for about an hour, following her into different neighborhoods, asking people we saw if the recognized her and even knocked on a few doors. Finally, we had to call Animal Control and we told them we would meet them back at the ball field. We get back to the field and are waiting for AC. After about 30 minutes, the dog is whining so I decide to walk her again. This time, she leaves the park and turns left, we cross the busy street and turn down the first street. A couple houses in is a guy doing yard work. I call to him and as soon as he turns around and sees us, he recognizes the dog and points me to her house.
