Bucky wrote:so who knows anything about assisted living facilites

Like what?

They advertise to the children of the elders that will be living there, so they often overlook what the older adult wants out of the remainder of his/her time. Look into what kind of lawsuits have been filed, staffing considerations (do they pay their people well, do they have a decent sized staff or are they burning out a handful of employees). Do they have single rooms? How many residents per staff member? What does the average day look like for a resident?

Definitely lots of other things to consider
