by Polar Bear Phan » Thu Aug 15, 2019 20:03:21

There was a very distinct peak where NASCAR really went mainstream in the late '00s that has really evaporated over the last decade. This is why the stands look really empty on television, because those seats were needed in 2007, but not anymore.

Due to the individual competitor nature of NASCAR, it has had several small plane/aircraft crashes in its history. Most notably the fatal crashes of Alan Kulwicki and Davey Allison a few months apart in 1993. Team sports generally use larger planes and/or charters that are safer.
