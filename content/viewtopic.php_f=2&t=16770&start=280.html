This is freaking amazing news that I meant to post here yesterday. Don't know if it was posted elsewhere on bsg already.

I'm sure that one or more of you on here have read the first two books of the Kingkiller Chronicles. (If you haven't, and you are in any way a fan of fantasy books, I HIGHLY recommend them.)

Well, Lin-Manuel Miranda is a huge fan of them and he has reached a deal to produce the film/TV adaptations of the books, as well as the short stories/novellas from that universe.

http://www.tor.com/2016/11/29/lin-manuel-miranda-the-kingkiller-chronicle-adaptation-patrick-rothfuss/

If you've read the books, you know how much of a role music/poetry plays in the stories. And if you're just a fan of Hamilton, the tone of the song "The Story of Tonight" was inspired by scenes with Kvothe (the main character) and his drinking buddies from the first book.
