by azrider » Fri Oct 31, 2014 14:18:24

702 wrote:Looking to get my first snowboard. Any suggestions or tips?


I actually prefer Solomon boards and bindings over burton. I've had more than a few concerns with the quality of burton and their price too. And bang for the buck, if you buy last years models you are going to save big time money. From the sound of it, you probably want to go with a board with rocker technology. These types of boards are more forgiving as you won't catch an edge as much landing a jump or just those late in the day conditions when the snow is choppy and you're tired. It will make for a good all round mountain board, however if you plan on some more extreme double black diamond chutes or bowl, not the best choice. Likewise in the park, you will want a board with greater flex for tricks.

Btw... Mammoth mountain is the place to go. Big bear is ok, never made it to Brian head, but been to all the Tahoe and southern Colorado ski areas in addition to both cottonwood valleys and park city in Utah. You should also consider az snowbowl, not a bad place as I go there probably 8-16 times a year.
