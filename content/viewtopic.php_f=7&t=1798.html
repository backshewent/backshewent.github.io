I've done some insightful analysis on these game threads, and discovered the following highly useful nuggets of information.

Our ace so far this year is a tossup between wins (and threads started) leader ColeforPresident (4-2) and undefeated z ipper (2-0). 1 is also undefeated (1-0 appropriately). No other thread starters are undefeated. kenrosenthal is our Adam Eaton with his 0-4 record. Laexile is 2-1, making him one of very few folks with a winning record. Most people are at .500 or are 0-1. 17 different people have started game threads so far this year, with 11 of those starting at least 2, but only 4 (ColeforPresident, kenrosenthal, Laexile, and karn) starting at least 3.

When the thread starter uses a "verbose" description of the game, the Phils are a pathetic 4-10. I defined verbose as including the starting pitchers in the thread title. When only the date and teams are mentioned in the thread title, the Phillies are 10-8. When some sort of weird thread title is used that mentions very little about the game (mostly karn doing this), the Phils are 3-2.

When the previous day game thread starter started the thread for the next game, the Phils are 3-9.

When lineups were not included in the first post, or they were included in some sort of unintelligble fashion, the Phils are 1-2. You could also consider it 1-3 depending on how you view the Opening Day game thread. I said that one did include it since Stu edited it in, but he also started the thread a few months ahead of time.

I hope we can use this brilliant analysis to help turn the season around.
