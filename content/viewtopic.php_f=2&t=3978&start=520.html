by JFLNYC » Mon May 13, 2013 14:23:03

I've got a 900, two Ones and recently got a 650. If you've only got a few devices, get the 650 for $75 on Amazon. The problem (for me) with any fully touchscreen remote (including a tablet) is the lack of any hard buttons. If you like to change channels, view the guide, stop and start the DVR and you like to do them without having to constantly look at the remote, you need hard buttons. I've tried the Samsung SmartView app, L5 remote and RedEye for iPad and have found them all lacking. Like most things it's a matter of personal preference.
