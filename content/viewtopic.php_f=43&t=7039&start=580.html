by karn » Fri Nov 14, 2008 12:57:15

The Phillies have three lists: OF, SP and RP

Manny is at the top of OF, Burnett is at the top of SP and Hoffman is the top RP. Are they likely to get any of those three? No, but none are out of the realm of possibility. Hoffman wants a shot at a ring. Burnett has close affection for Dubee and Manny likes the park, the manager and the team.

There is no Baldelli on the OF list. They want an everyday player. He can't do that.

Two to watch out for: Melvin Mora and Aubrey Huff. Big years, both with corner OF ability could become trade targets for Amaro. The market after Manny is thin to meet their needs. They are not in on Abreu, Burrell or Dunn

Pitcher to watch out for: Mike Mussina, who is not as likely to retire as Girardi seems to think. He'll play for a 1-year deal with either New York, Boston or Philly
