In my moments of real doubt I wonder how Nate Silver or anyone can say “Democrats have an 86% chance of taking the House” - or whatever - because I wonder what that really means. This isn’t a die roll or a coin flip, this is a unique, one-time event with an exact set of complicates, inter-related circumstances, and we will never see this exact set of circumstances again. In other words its outcome will be produced once and only once. It doesn’t pass the sniff test that you could ascribe anything like shaded certainty to the outcome: EITHER the outcome is never in doubt, or it’s a genuine coin flip.
