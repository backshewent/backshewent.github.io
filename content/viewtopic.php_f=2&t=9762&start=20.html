3x2 inches? It shouldn't take much to do a 3"x2" print. What was the resolution of the original (i.e. X pixels by Y pixels)?

Assuming you have no way of getting a higher-resolution source file, there are interpolation algorithms that will produce a smoother result than simply doubling the pixels or whatever, but none of these can make these files more detailed than they were to start with.
