This site appears to be getting hammered at the moment but highly recommend checking it out if/when they get back online:

http://flattenthecurve.com/

Gives a lot of good info on the theory behind social distancing and also gives a lot of practical advice on what to do with yourself right now.

Short version: In general stay away from all crowds. Be mindful of your proximity to others in public. Be super disciplined about washing your hands and not touching your face.
