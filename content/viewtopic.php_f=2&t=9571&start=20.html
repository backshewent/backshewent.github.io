by Woody » Mon Oct 26, 2009 12:18:25

No one has gotten the trout thing correct yet. Many moons ago back on Philliesphans there was a thread about the NFL draft/combine. I posted about an article I read about the Wonderlic test that is given to all NFL prospects. Specifically I posted an example question that was in the article which went (to the best of my recollection) something like this:

Which of these doesn't belong?
A. Dog
B. Cat
C. Trout

I don't have the exact question and answers correct, but it was completely absurd and featured the correct answer of "Trout", highlighting the asininity of dumb football players that get single digit scores

After that, it became a meme on Philliesphans to answer "trout" to any poll or question. Then people started actually building it into the poll questions themselves.

Then, when BSG started and karn posted the "Let's make graemlins!" thread, one of the first ones he made was a picture of a trout saying, "Joboggi". This was in reference to jobo's habit of popping in active threads and talking about something completely random--with no context--that had nothing to do with the topic at hand
you sure do seem to have a lot of time on your hands to be on this forum? Do you have a job? Are you a shut-in?
