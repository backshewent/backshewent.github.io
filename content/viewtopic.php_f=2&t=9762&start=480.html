Re: Photography II

by phatj » Thu Mar 14, 2013 15:48:42

Canon's 18-200 is not a premium lens, but would do pretty much everything your 18-55 can do, plus a whole lot more zoom. Those last 50mm on the 55-250 zoom aren't worth much; I'd Ebay both your existing lenses whatever you can get (probably not much) and put that towards the 18-200. Then start building a collection of decent primes, starting with the basic Canon 50mm, especially if you do portraits. You don't need L glass, even if you're rich. Premium lenses are larger and heavier than the regular versions, for differences that aren't going to even be detectable most of the time.
