by Philly the Kid » Fri Jan 19, 2018 22:53:31

I truly have no instinctual feel for this game.

Everything people say - seems obvious. I don't see the Eagles "establishing the run" - they will have to establish the pass, some trickery, short field, mis-direction - and go deep w Torrey. Minn is a real D. Not 85 bears or Ravens SB D, but they are for real. Cover TEs well, excellent 3rd down, stingy on tds. That said, they do it all much better at home in the dome than on road. Eagles have lost 2 home games since Doug came to town. But most of those had Carson at the wheel...

It's really hard to predict. We know that if the Birds get a pick 6, or force a fumble and run it in - or block a FG, those are the kind of things that can make the diff. But you can't know that will happen, just hope it might.

Keenum doesn't suck, but he's not Brees or Brady or Rogers.

I feel like if the Birds score 24 or more they win. If it's truly a close game, then anything can happen and Jake Elliot an Donnie Jones may figure in to this thing prominently.

I don't know if Zimmer blitzes a lot, but if they plan man and jam the lines, we are not going to bust through and Ajayi is good, but he's not Todd Gurley or Zeke good.

What's weird, is that I could see us beating NE even w Foles and this motley group...

The word is it's a great locker-room and don't underestimate what camaraderie and brotherhood are worth...
