I think what the Rev may be getting at (and correct me if I'm wrong, Rev) is that it seems more and more difficult for us to have a serious, reasonable, non-confrontational discussion on this board (and I don't exclude myself). So one would hope the discussions might be a bit more civil than the normal Internet free-for-all.

Discussions tend to degenerate pretty quickly into insults (especially if there's a whipping boy like PtK to jump on) with everyone trying to be more clever and funny than the next. There's also often a very distinct groupthink/gang mentality which brooks no dissent, reasonable or otherwise.
