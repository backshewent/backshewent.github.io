Also--like every state, Arkansas has weird liquor laws. As regards this product, a liquor store can only sell liquor, beer, and wine. Grocery and convenience stores can sell beer and wine, but the kind of wine such a store could sell was really limited, and excluded most box wines. The law recently changed though, and now grocery stores and convenience stores can sell lots of different wine. The liquor stores of course hated this change, and attempted to work a compromise that would allow them to sell non-alcoholic items but they didn't succeed. So, until fairly recently, such packaging likely would have been illegal to sell anywhere in Arkansas.

Things might have changed, but I recall PA state stores only sold liquor and wine, nothing else, and beer marts sold beer, but only by the case, and you could get snacks like chips and pretzels there, so you wouldn't have been able to buy this product in PA either.
