I kinda like pancakes once in a while. (Believe it or not pancakes with some spam and mustard on the side is somehow scrumdiddlyishous.).


1.5 cups flour
3.5 tsp baking powder
.5 tsp salt
1.25 cups milk
1 egg
3 tsp melted butter
and the key: .5 tsp vanilla extract

don't over-mix, leave some lumps in the batter.
add fruit or chocolate chips if you're feeling crazy.
makes about 4 good sized pancakes, enough to feed 2.

it's just as easy to make your own batter as it is to buy from a box and probably half the cost.
