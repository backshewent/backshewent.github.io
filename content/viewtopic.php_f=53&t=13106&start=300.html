Re: Bill Conlin might be a child molester

by BuddyGroom » Tue Jan 03, 2012 14:52:26

Never got the Conlin hatred. I mean, anyone who ever saw him on Sports Reporters could tell he had a prickly personality, and I guess he rubbed some the wrong way with his military and literary references (many of which, I'll admit, eluded me). And yeah, apparently he could be a real jerk in online correspondence, although I suspect some of those correspondents brought it upon themselves. I had online interaction with Conlin, more than a decade ago, when I wrote him to inform him that some of us in the Philly.com forums had taken to calling Desi Relaford, then in the midst of a 31-error season, DesE-6.

Conlin wrote back that he loved it, and he subsequently used the "DesE-6" moniker in a column.

So I had only one, positive, brief interaction with the guy.

But as someone who has followed the Phillies and Philadelphia sports since the 1970s, I loved that Conlin had so much institutional knowledge of the Phillies. That's the main reason why I always read his columns - I loved the little tidbits about what Paul Owens said at the bar during the 1976 winter meetings, et al.

Yeah, he was old-school. So what? At least admit the guy knew a ton of Phillies lore. With his retirement and Phil Jasner's death, we've lost a lot of institutional knowledge in Philadelphia sports in the past year or so.

So I mourn that, even as I am horrified by what Conlin allegedly did.
