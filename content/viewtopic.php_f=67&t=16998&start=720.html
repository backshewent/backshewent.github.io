There is a chance - that a lot of things go right this year.

Imagine for a moment the following:

Hicks comes back healthy and picks right up where he left off -- Kendricks under Schwartz plays smarter and better in a 4-3 anyway. Alonso gets back to his rookie form which was in Jim's defense already. Suddenly, our pedestrian LB core becomes above average to superb. Barwin/Curry in 4-3 get sax. Fletcher plays inside on 1st and 2nd and outside on 3rd. Graham rotates to help contain the edge on rushing, Logan solid in the middle gets penetration too - and Schwartz who doens't like to blitz a lot - doesn't have to. He's got 3 fast young LBs running around making plays, and some speed rushers and some beef up front. Probably needs another DT and we can see if they will need another DE depending on how Barwin Curry and Graham all fare in this system? They still have Hart and Bair right? Nick Fairly is out there - there are some options.

If suddenly our front 7 with guys rotating - is very solid to superb - then the DBs aren't working as hard - its obviously Rowe, Maxwell and Jenkins for 3 of the slots - and that means mainly they need to fill another S and Corner - maybe Jacorey Shephard comes back - maybe Carrol is in the mix too for 2016.

Then - if that D stays mostly healthy and guys like Alonso and Hicks and Curry really get it done - they can get whatever missing piece or two for 2017 to become a top 10 D ?

As for offense, they already have a deep RB group - and J Math - and if Agholor comes to play - at least give us what Maclin more or less did - then we could get better mostly with just our own guys. Pick up a veteran WR to add to the mix - maybe even two vets - coming off injuries or cuts - who might just do enough. Guys who still know in their hearts they have some game left.

The big question is QB - but this D could be a lot better just because of Schwartz and the change in scheme. They may be more talented than we realize.
