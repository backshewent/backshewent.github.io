most people I know use craigslist. I don't really know of another way unless you have an in with someone who can check listings across a bunch of different realtors. I've made good use of PadMapper.com in the past. It searches across CL and some other apartment listing sites. You can set a minimum and maximum rent and number of bedrooms, but unlike Craigslist you can search within a certain map area. Sometimes the individual listings can be off because of how the addresses are listed in the ads, but it's a nice way of weeding through hundreds of ads a day.
