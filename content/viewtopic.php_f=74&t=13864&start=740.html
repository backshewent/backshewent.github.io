by Houshphandzadeh » Tue Jul 24, 2012 11:40:41

it's kind of just taste in bars and restaurants that will probably separate those two. Grad Hospital has lots of great gastropub type brunch spots and nice but divier (relatively) bars like Sidecar and Grace Tavern. more of neighborhood feel. Washington Square will have you a lot closer to nicer upscale city spots and will have you just closer to the heartbeat of center city, really. if you like doing actual cultural stuff during weekends, Wash Sq has a big advantage there. if money was no object, I'd probably go for Washington Square closer to the Western side of it, as the Eastern side can get to be just old, settled people like Grote said, but you might be able to get a much better deal in Grad Hospital, and it's also a great area
