Fletcher press conference on now

-- "Disappointed" to see Quenneville sign with Florida
-- Need to get better defensively, says our d-men are younger than most of the playoff teams
-- Looking for a backup goalie, vet d-man and another scorer in FA/trade
-- Thinks Patrick has lots of great qualities, just needs to mature, get faster and work on his shot to become the #2 center
-- Anyone on the team is available, but it would take a ridiculous offer to move Giroux or Hart
-- Wants to get offers out to the RFAs sooner than later
-- Relatively healthy group going into the summer, doesn't expect any major surgeries
-- Doesn't think it's an elite free agency market
-- Says the top half of the draft has a lot of great prospects, looks like a good year for goalies
