by RichmondPhilsFan » Tue May 24, 2016 09:55:55

pacino wrote:the Darth Vader comic is ending at #25 and will be replaced by an anthology. Sad to see it go but I like the idea of seeing spotlights on different characters in the universe.

I'm glad that they had a particular end game in mind for it. I really didn't want to have them try to cram 100 issues into the 3-year gap between Eps IV and V. Vader has learned basically everything he needs to find out to set the stage for Ep V (Luke's parentage, Palpatine's lies), and the rest would just be spinning wheels to sell books. My fear was that Vader would have more confrontations with Luke, diluting the gravitas of their battle on Bespin.

My guess is that one of the remaining things to wrap up in this series will be for Vader to kill Tagge (who we don't see in Ep V, so he's presumably dead) and take over command of the Executor, which we saw under construction in the last issue. (That last issue was awesome, BTW. The conversation between Sidious and Vader was some of the most enlightening dialogue between Sith in any SW canon.)

One thing I've found interesting about the new canon is how the authors (especially Claudia Gray in Lost Stars, which is amazing) have discussed the impact that the destruction of the first Death Star and other things such as Vader's penchant for killing officers have left the Empire with a shortage of talented officers in the upper ranks by the time of Ep VI. Basically everyone fought to get assigned to the Death Star, especially the promising junior officers.
