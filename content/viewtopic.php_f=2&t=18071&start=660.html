Re: Random Daze in a Summer Haze

by TenuredVulture » Thu Aug 17, 2017 12:57:01

Youseff wrote:feel like everyone decided you only need one space after a period and didn't consult me or let me know. if you do two spaces on a gmail email it ruins the formatting.


The two space after a period rule goes back to typewriters that did not have proportional spaced fonts. With a proportional spaced font, the word processor automatically gives you the appropriate space after a period. It's especially important if you're justifying your text (that is, the line of type will stretch to the right margin) as adding a bit of space after a period does not distract the reader in the way that adding space b e t w e e n letters and words can. (Hm, it seems like BSG takes the extra space between words out. Interesting.)
