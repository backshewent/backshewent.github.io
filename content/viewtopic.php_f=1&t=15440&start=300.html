Local Mariners are really kicking some Butts lately. They have the second best run differential in the AL. Two guys are hitting the ball well for them, Cano and Seager. Zunino is doing pretty well with the bat and is getting a great rep as a catcher. They have some other guys who are Ok I compared to having lots of awful hitters in recent years. Still not a great offense but good enough a lot of the time.

The pitching staff is the killer though. King Felix is having a monster year. He's 10-2 but his record is misleading. He's pitched better than that. The rest of the starting staff has been doing good work since May. Chris Young, the tall guy is having great success despite not striking out a lot of guys. He's control long the runs via the homer and that is crucial for him

Taijuan Walker is ready to get going now too so things are looking really good.

The Bullpen of cast offs and unheralded youngsters has been outstanding.

Pretty kewl.
