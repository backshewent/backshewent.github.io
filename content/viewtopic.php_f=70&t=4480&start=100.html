I bring my lunch almost every day. Near my house, I know some great delis, Sarcone's being the best. I eat at Subway a few times a year when I have to go the bank and it's always just fine. There are no Mom and Pop deli's that don't suck on the Parkway or JFK Blvd between my work and the bank. I'm glad you and most people know a good deli near you, but the claim that any old privately-owned shop is going to make a good sandwich is just wrong. Plenty of them will just throw some inferior ingredients together and only buy new rolls when they're out of the last batch.
