Hold on, now.

I don't want Blanton if it requires giving up a real prospect (and I'd much rather have Burnett), but if he comes at the cost of a Golson, why wouldn't you want him?

The fallacy that the Phillies need a No. 2 is exactly that. The Phillies are 25-13 in games started by Moyer and Kendrick. Another pitcher who can pitch to an ERA in the low 4s is sufficient. The problem is that you can't have two guys in your rotation with ERAs hovering around 6 (12-24 in Myers/Eaton starts).

Blanton's numbers suck this year, but he could probably come here and be better than Kendrick or Moyer. He was solid up through the end of May and had some really bad starts over the last month. If Burnett and Bedard or unattainable and it doesn't cost you much...well, it's better than doing nothing.
