So, I was supposed to do a focus group last night from 8-10 pm. I also had the Courtney Barnett show to go to which started at 8:30. Figured I'd go and hope it ended early.

We're all sitting there in the waiting room, waiting for this thing to start. The moderator comes in and says, "Hi everybody! I'll be your moderator for tonight's focus group. Now, there are 8 of you and we only need 7. Does anyone have a long drive home ahead of them and would like to opt out? You'll still get paid."

I sort of didn't believe what I had just heard and looked around the room. No one said a thing, then some girl said, "Well my drive's 40 minutes, but I don't mind staying." Still no one is volunteering to GO HOME AND GET PAID FOR DOING NOTHING.

"OK, well, does anyone just feel tired or maybe not feel great and would like to opt out?"

I immediately raise my hand. "We still get paid?"

"Yes."

$100 for showing up to a focus group. Love it.
