by Monkeyboy » Wed Jan 01, 2020 03:06:02

I moved around a lot as a kid. We stayed in the same general area, but my mom was never settled on anything. I was pretty impervious to the whole thing, but I think my brother was bothered by any sort of change. He refused to go on vacation to the beach with my grandparents, for example.

I would say don't make too much of a big deal about it. Kids are smart and very in tune with your own actions and emotions. So don't fuss over him about it. I like the idea of giving him jobs to do, having him carry boxes, etc. Have him keep a few things aside that he can take in the car with him when you make the last move. Make him feel like he's involved, like it''s happening WITH him and not TO him.
