Update on PANDAS: kid has been on antibiotics since that was confirmed, and has generally been doing better. I think I mentioned that one of her issues has been that she isn't eating as much as she used to. We feared she had been losing weight, or at least not gaining as one would expect of a growing child (really we were sure of it but didn't want to get into the habit of checking her weight all the time). Tonight she stepped on the scale on her own and reported her weight... 10 pounds down from the last time she weighed herself, which was probably before we moved in June. I nearly had a panic attack. Then I had a thought; the scale is stored under the bathroom sink, and immediately in front of said sink is a thick bathmat; if she put the scale on the mat, that would make it tend to read low. Turns out yep, that's what she did. She re-weighed herself with the scale on the tiles and it was back where it was before. Obviously we'd be happier if it were up a little but at least no cause for panic.
