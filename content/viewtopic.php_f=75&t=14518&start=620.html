St Patty's Day and New Years Eve = amateur hour. Run,don't walk, away from these days.

Anyway, my brother went up to Czerw's butchers in Port Richmond (right around the corner from both sets of grandparents houses), and brought back a huge haul of Polish food:

5 pounds of smoked kielbasi
5 pounds of kibanosa - dry smoked sausage (Polish pepperoni)
5 dozen pirogi - cheese, sauerkraut, 3 cheese, and cheesesteak (had the cheesesteak last night so effing awesome)
2 cheese and raisan babkas from Marion's bakery
3 pounds of kristichi from Marion's (polish cookies called angel's wings)
2 pounds of chocolate chip cookies from Marions

Best part about it all was my kids loved the food as well. Five generations of my family now indoctrinated in eating Polish food, it's generally bad for you but tastes so good. Great day in general.
