Re: PIZZA is the New Orange

by TomatoPie » Fri Mar 24, 2017 14:55:24

Finally made it to Beddia. $27 for a pie with sausage.

Stood in line for 10 minutes, placed order, came back to get my pie in 2 hours. Not really convenient for suburban visitors.

The pizza is truly superb. The crust was amazing - so thin, yet so rigid. Toward the cornicione, dense and chewy with some good air pockets. The sauce was a role player under a big payload of fresh white mozz and orange "Old Gold" gouda.

Is it better than Pizza Brain? About the same, but I'd love to try them side by side.

It is better than an average La Porta pie, but not the best La Porta pie.

I think the mystique is part of the appeal. "Hard to get" excellent pizza like this gets more headlines than easy to get excellent pizza like Pizza Brain.
