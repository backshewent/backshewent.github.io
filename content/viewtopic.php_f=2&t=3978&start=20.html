by Woody » Mon Jun 23, 2008 08:15:11

I got my wife the Garmin Nuvi 350recently and she loves it. It's small, has the turn by turn directions (with Australian accent!) and is really easy to use. I only looked at Garmin's when buying, but yeah, there are a million models (which will all be obsolete when iphone-type phones are the standard). I did learn that there is very little difference between them all so I'd recommend deciding what the 'must-have' features are and get a bare bones version without unneeded (expensive) bells and whistles. A good way to do might be to sort by price high to low and work your way up the list to compare

Another option to consider: If you're ready to get a new cell phone, maybe just get any one of the 2000 new fancy smart phones that have GPS navigation built it.
