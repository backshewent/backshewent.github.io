Right. The current waiver period was like sometime in April till July 31. After that, waivers reset and you have to "clear" again before you can be sent to the minors or traded. The Phillies will waive Howard, Utley, Hamels, etc etc, they basically waive everyone, and when they do, they'll probably also waive guys like Golson who they might actually want to trade. The hope is, you send through a bunch of guys you have no intention of letting go and then you throw in guys youd like to move at the same time, hoping to throw everyone off. Its like a trojan horse, without the horse
