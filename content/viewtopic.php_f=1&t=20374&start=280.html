The Sixers have made good post-Hinkie moves. Off the top of my head:

Not sure if you want to give post-Hinkie any credit for the Simmons pick since he was the consensus #1, but I give collars partial credit for pulling that trigger.

TLC and Kork may not be Spicy P, but they were good picks where they were drafted.

The Redick signing was excellent.

Belinelli and Ilyasova as buyout guys instead of giving anything up for them was smart.

Covington contract was great.

The Zhaire-Bridges trade was a very good process decision that hasn’t had good results partially because Smith had a freak allergy problem and because the other pick we got was sent out in an overlay trade.

Shamet pick was good (partial credit since they dumped him that season).

Shake pick was good.

Matisse (trade up and) pick was good.

Bringing in D’Antoni as Assistant Head Coach was good and we probably would’ve had more success if he outlasted Brown.

We’ve had *big* misses, but it hasn’t been all bad from a personnel standpoint and Hinkie had big misses too.
