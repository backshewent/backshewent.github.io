by traderdave » Tue Nov 06, 2018 11:13:58

Actually, the Securing Our Children's Future Bond Act is pretty explicit about where the money goes:

Bonds of the State of New Jersey are authorized to be issued in the aggregate principal amount of $500,000,000 to be allocated as grants for the costs of projects as follows:

$350 million for career and technical education grants and school security project grants;
$100 million for school water infrastructure grants; and
$50 million for college career and technical education grants.

As to "general purposes", a quick reading of the Act seems to indicate that the funds are pretty locked down to the above purposes. One caveat is that any income earned on the borrowed funds through reinvestment goes into the General Fund not back into the Bond Fund. That seems a little weird but fairly low-key grift for New Jersey.
