by JFLNYC » Tue Mar 08, 2011 10:28:04

Yeah, I'm not overly concerned about the pitchers and injuries. I figure Oswalt may miss some starts because of his hip and maybe the rest of the starting staff misses a few starts overall. But I just can't get past how iffy the lineup looks, first without Werth and now (probably) without Utley for some significant period. Very tough for any team to lose its top two offensive performers and not feel it.

I fully expect Howard to have a decent and healthy season (last year's injury was a fluke). Jimmy should be pretty good in his contract year. Polanco won't be great, but he'll be reasonably consistent. But I'm really afraid that Raul is cooked and, although I like Francisco, I like him a whole lot more as a fourth OF than as our regular RF. A big year from Vic could make a big difference, as he could easily end up in the 5, or even 3 hole.

With the Phils pitching and defense, the team is going to be good no matter what and, of course, well-suited for the post-season. But I fear the offense is likely to be pretty frustrating this year.
