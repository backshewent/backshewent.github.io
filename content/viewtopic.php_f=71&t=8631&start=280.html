by dajafi » Thu Jun 25, 2009 10:33:43

Grotewold wrote:Was it the games or the girl


Pretty much everything about that week was amazing. That she was a big baseball fan (Twins; we were driving home from college, on her dad's dime) helped. We also just had some weird good luck--my uncle had promised to treat us to tickets for the game in Cleveland, for a game between the Brewers and Indians that we weren't especially excited about beforehand. But the seats were right behind the Brewers' dugout, which was pretty spectacular in the first year that Jacobs Field was open. What I didn't know at the time was that his wife's cousin was the Indians' VP for business development or some such--a guy named Dennis Lehman who had worked in the Phils' front office earlier.

And the game was superb--it went into I think the 12th inning, and the Indians won it on a monster Albert Belle HR. The Tribe are still my favorite AL team.
