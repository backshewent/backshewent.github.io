Bill McNeal wrote:Italianos eh? Never heard of it, but its better than Rita's? I gotta check it out. Are they a chain or is it a local joint?


It's a mom and pop place that is in a row home and if they aren't open for business you would never know it's there. They have like a fake front wall that blends in with the neighborhood. It's not like Geno's or Pat's or Tony Luke's where they yell at you if you don't know the lingo. They're actually really nice. When they are open there is only like 2 parking spots so you need a second person to do the drop-off, drive around the corner, pick up deal.

Many places have claimed to invent the Gelati but Italiano's really did like 30 years ago. They don't use soft serve like most places do. Everything is hand made and I've been going there for as far back as I can remember since my late grandpop lived right around the corner on 10th or 11th and Iseminger. I'm almost positive that it's on 12th or 13th and Shunk.
