milton bernard thompson wrote:choke


Man I hate the term "choke." The Phillies have won a whole bunch of games with a pretty lousy pitching staff and erased a 7 game lead with 17 to play. they are having a bad game today. Anyone who has ever played any kind of sport has had that happen. Sometimes, but not often, it's because of a "choke."

Athletes and teams have bad days. It happens all the time. It's rarely because of "choking." The division crowns and championship titles go to teams that win the most games over a long season. Not to the team that "chokes" the least. I think the whole "choke" thing is bogus and perpetuated by guys like Jim Rome and other commentators and writers who don't really have a clue about what it takes to win and lose ball games and other athletic endeavors.

I don't buy the whole "choke" thing and I think we should fight the whole Jim Rome "choke" attitude that is far too pervasive in sports commentary.
