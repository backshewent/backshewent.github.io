by TenuredVulture » Wed Dec 29, 2010 15:12:26

Pretty much in terms of method. It takes longer though.

And, for that spanish rice effect--try a can Ro-Tel (drained) instead of jarred spaghetti sauce. And a can of beans never hurts rice.

Here's a pretty easy and cheap dinner:

Brown sliced kielbasi, or any other similar sausage in a little oil with a chopped onion. Add a can or two of drained beans. Add a can of drained Ro-Tel if you want. Or don't. Season with whatever you like--I use thyme and oregano from my garden, plus cumin and chili powder, black pepper and salt. Serve with brown rice.

There's a ton of potential variation here--you can use substitute bacon or salt pork or lots of thing for the meat, or go meatless.
