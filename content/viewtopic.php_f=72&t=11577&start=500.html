I signed up for a meteorology course in college. The first class was canceled due to a problem with room assignments. For the second I showed up where I thought I was supposed to, but about five minutes late. I slipped as inconspicuously as I could into the back and got myself settled. I then looked at the board, and to my horror, realized that I had gone into the wrong classroom. The whole board was covered with physics notations. It was too late to do anything now, so I tried to look bright-eyed and attentive and opened up my notebook and started to jot everything down in complete ignorance of anything that was being said in this foreign language. After about ten minutes or so,though, the instructor mentioned clouds, followed by hurricanes. That's when the real horror set in as I realized I actually was in the right class.

That was my last class in meteorology, although I still like to look at the pictures in the textbook from time to time.
