by Rev_Beezer » Sat Jan 26, 2008 16:18:34

I have a friend who moved to San Diego and has done some work in the music recording industry in southern California. He has worked with some has-beens like Vertical Horizon, also worked with Ryan Adams at some point in a production capacity.

He is a huge Bob Dylan fan. HUGE (like Safeco). Well, he got a call from Dylan's management that essentially said, "Bob Dylan would like to work with you. Expect a call soon." He was ecstatic, so happy, called everyone.

The problem? His answering machine was a recording of William Shatner singing "Mr. Tambourine Man". Happens that a certain Mr. Dylan called his house when he wasn't home.

So he gets home after dinner, checks his machine, and there's one message on it. After a long pause and some indiscernable mumbling, a very irritated Bob Dylan can clearly be heard saying, "Hey Man, what the ****"?

The next day, he gets a call from Dylan's management, who simply says, "Bob Dylan does not desire to work with you anymore."
