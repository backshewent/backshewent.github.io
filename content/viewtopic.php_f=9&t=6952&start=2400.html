by Marion » Thu Oct 30, 2008 11:05:59

I haven't had the chance to read through everything yet, so in case no one's mentioned it yet...

We hung around inside the stadium for a little while and got to hear some of the players address the fans. Two of my favorite moments:

Chase Utley
Asks the fans "How's everyone feeling tonight?!"
Fans give week response. Chase says, "Is that the best you can do"
Fans get louder, Chase says, "Okay, that's better"
Then Chase requests, "I want to hear one more boo"
Really loud boo from the crowd
Chase says, "Great, I can't wait to hear that again next year!!!"


Harry Kalas
Begins by saying, "They said we wouldn't win the division, well bye by NY Mets"
"They said we wouldn't win the NLDS, well C.C. you later Sabathia"
Then he said something about the NLCS which ended with "Manny, my fanny!"
He ended with something about us not being able to win the World Series and then "Well, see you later son Todd!"
He of course follwed up with High Hopes.

Fantastic night and I can't believe I was there!
"I tell all kids not to use that word. If they're 29 and they win the World Series, I think they can say that.
But I definitely would say to all the kids out there, 'Kids, it's a bad word. Don't say it. And I'm dead serious.' "
