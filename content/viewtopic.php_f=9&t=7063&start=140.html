by Marion » Fri Oct 31, 2008 21:18:03

Finally made it home around 6:30. What a day!

Boyfriend and I got to Nova Care about 8am. From there, we got on the subway and went up to Walnut/Locust. We then walked our way up to the start of the parade. We were able to get in the front row on Market St between 19 & 20.

As soon as the parade passed, we tried to work our way back to the Walnut/Locust station, only to realize, there was no way that was happening, and we found out they shut down anyway.

So we walked back to about 16th street and started walking towards the stadium. And we walked. Well, I walked and boyfriend pushed along in his wheelchair. And we walked and walked and walked.

Miracle upon miracle, we somehow caught up to the parade and then even passed it! Just when we thought the end was in sight (CBP), we walked into the parking lot only to realize the parade was coming through there and they wouldn't let us into the gates. We pleaded to everyone we could that we had tickets and to let us in, but no go.

So we had to turn around, go down to the Holiday Inn and over to the next street and then up to Pattison to get in the front gates.

Anyway, we made it in. Got there in time to grab something to eat and sit down and relax and watch the remainder of the event at CBP.

WHAT A DAY! But it was so awesome, and I'm so happy we did it. I only had one moment of giving up at the very end when we were standing outside the gates trying desperately to get in. But boyfriend kept me going. And I figured if he could push all the way from the city to the stadium without barely complaining, I could finish the rest of the way.

We had a great time and I'm so happy I was able to be there. Sorry for the novel, but wow, that was our day!

Oh, and I've got a fantastic picture of Wheels with the ball girls for you boys. World's biggest grin on his face! I'll post it later.
