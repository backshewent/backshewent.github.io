On the topic of political satire, has the UK show The Thick of It made it over the water yet? Imagine The Office, but focussing on spin doctors from the labour and conservative parties and how they handle MPs. The level of near constant panic and aggression makes you laugh hard. Its genius, although they do swear a bucket load in it (especially peter capaldi's character, closely modelled on Alastair Campbell, former director of communications and strategy for the labour party). There's very little caricature of actual politicians in it, so I think it would translate well to the states.
