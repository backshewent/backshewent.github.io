by CalvinBall » Sun Jan 24, 2010 00:45:13

Crazy story time.

I was helping my friend move today. He has this 3 piece leather sectional couch that we loaded in to the back of my dad's orange pick-up. For those of you in West Chester this will make sense. After you get off 202 to get on 100 a piece of it blew out. I have no idea how. It is a freaking couch. I saw it flipping down the road in my side view mirror. I then looked to my right to see some guy in a car waving frantically at me to tell me my couch was rolling away, which it was.

Luckily it didn't hit anyone. It was covered in cat hair already so it was natural that it landed on its feet and in the shoulder out of harms way. I finally pulled over about 500 yards up the road and started to walk back to the couch. It was a decently long walk on the side of a busy highway. Everyone was staring at me and laughing.

I got there and got the couch out of the road and recovered one of the cushions that had also blown out. By this time this high school kid name Kevin, who had seen it all happen, pulled up. It is crazy he did this because you have to go to Exton turn around, go back to West Chester turn around again, and then get back on the road I was on.

Anyways, we started talking. Turns out he is DJ and had a gig that night. For some reason he then showed me his DJ equipment in his car. All on the side of a highway mind you. It was surreal.

About then my friend pulled up. We shoved the couch in to his car and drove it up the road. We strapped it down in the truck. The thing only had a handful of rips and what I can only best describe as rug burn on the leather.
