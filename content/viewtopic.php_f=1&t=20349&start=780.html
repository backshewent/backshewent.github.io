Here's some ramblings that I have coming into tonight's game.

Expectations are a hell of a thing. At no point during the season did I ever dream of this team competing for the Stanley Cup. Then they won 9 in a row. Then the league shut down and there's no way they can dust themselves off and pick up right where they left off. Then they dominate the Round Robin. Now we sit here, up 3-2 in a series vs an inferior team. I expected them to win this series by out-talenting the Habs. That hasn't happened to this point. I have no idea what to expect tonight. They are super young in alot of key spots. You are seeing those youth mistakes, even vs this shit team, and those mistakes are ending up in the back of the net. Look, everything broke right for the Flyers to make a run. They have the worst team in the first round, they avoid Boston/TB and get the NYI (another team the Flyers are much more talented than). If they can get out of Game 6 with a win, I expect them to go to Edmonton. Don't fuck around. Come out with intensity. Play with a purpose. We need our top 6 to fucking score. Let's go.
