FTN wrote:I don't know how any of the Abreu haters, who ripped on him for his "lack of heart", can support a player like Polanco.


I know you are joking but I don't see the connection. It's obvious to me why Philadelphia fans hated Abreu and like Polanco. One of the reasons people hated Abreu was for taking too many pitches -- that showed he wasn't trying enough. Polanco does not have that problem. Polanco occasionally dives for balls and as we know Abreu never dove for a ball during his entire career.

Another reason they hated Abreu was because they thought he could be better than he was. They don't think the same thing about Polanco. The biggest sin in the minds of Philadelphia fans is to not play up to your potential. That's why they tend to direct their most vicious venom at more talented players.
