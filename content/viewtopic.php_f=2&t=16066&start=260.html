Long a deep-dish skeptic, I had a life-changing experience in Crestwood, about 10 miles south of Midway Airport.

Louisa's:





Perfectly fresh red crushed tomato sauce, and a biscuit-like crust that was nothing like east coast pizza but wonderful in its own way.

Buried in that red sea was some decent cheese and some excellent sausage.

I'll never prefer Chicago deep-dish to a good Trenton or New Haven pie, but it was great to finally get a good rendition of the deep dish.
