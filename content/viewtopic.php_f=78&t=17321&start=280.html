It shouldn't be spreading after the first two days. You should wash in hot water anything that possibly came in contact with the oils, as the oils will remain until cleaned. That includes shoes, belts, wallet, towels, gloves, yard clippers, sheets, towels, etc. Use soap to clean under your fingernails. You also shouldnt avoid scrubbing in the shower thinking that will spread the rash; you need to make sure that all oils are off your body.

If you remember, I got Poison Ivy from weeding last Sunday, too. My ankles are covered, my right forarm was about 25% covered, and then I had a few other patches elsewhere on my legs and arms. I went to a physician on Thursday and they recommended benedryl/Zyrtec to reduce my body's response to the oil. They also prescribed Prednisone. That Thursday night was the first night I slept through straight. If your rash is still bad, I would make an appt with a physician. It shouldn't still be getting worse, 8 days later. My huge rash on my forearm is basically a large, thin scab now.
