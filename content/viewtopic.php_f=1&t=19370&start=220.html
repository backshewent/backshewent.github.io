Saw this on Reddit for a Salary breakdown.


TL;DRs:
The Flyers have roughly $4M - $5M in cap with a winger and back up goalie to sign. I think this excludes them from the UFA winger market barring a trade since I would expect that, assuming the salary projections are correct, half of the remaining cap space will be spent on a goalie backup. At this point, I would also expect two forwards on cheap RFA or ELC deals to make the team to fill things out. One in reserve and one on the 3rd or 4thline.

If the remaining RFAs of consequence (meaning those expected to sign above the AHL bury threshold) sign for more than projected, that may necessitate a trade. There is not a lot of wiggle room here and with the number of RFAs remaining to sign there is a high degree of uncertainty in the final cap situation.

If Fletcher makes a move to bring in additional salary we will likely see a trade.

This is Fletcher's team now The bets Fletcher has placed, especially on Niskanen and Braun return to their previous form, are sizable. If they hold steady or fall off further this team may not meet expectations simply due to the lack of wiggle room to make changes.
