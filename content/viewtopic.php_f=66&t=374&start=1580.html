by meatball » Mon Aug 13, 2007 10:12:09

Just back from Cincinnati. A couple thoughts...

Skyline Chili cheese coneys are one of the most perfect foods.

They love their festivals & beer in Cincy. Everywhere you go people are drinking, except no one was fighting and douchebaggery was at a minimum.

The downtown reminds me of Baltimore's downtown, which is to say it's not very nice. Baltimore has inner harbor, Cincy has the Ohio River, but it's pretty much all taken up by the stadiums. There is a lot of character in the surrounding neighborhoods, and the people are generally nice. You know, corn-fed and all that.

Speaking of Stadiums: Great American Ballpark kinda sucks. The sight lines are good, but the atmosphere is pretty sterile (plus the team playing in it blows). It didn't help that I had to endure my friend's "insights" on the game, like, "The Reds couldn't trade Dunn because he's not a cluctch hitter", and "Javier Valentin gets all his hits in the 7th inning or later, which is why he's more valuable than Dunn or Phillips." That was just the top of the iceberg. Sad part is he's one of the most intelligent people I know, normally. Anyway, I really wanted to punch him in the face but he's the best man in my wedding.

One thing about GABP I love is the Reds Hall of Fame. It is simply AWESOME. It sure beats the Phils' piddly-ass wall. It's a huge 3-story building with great displays and stuff to do.
