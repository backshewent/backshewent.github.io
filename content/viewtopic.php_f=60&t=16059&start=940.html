OK, maybe I overestimated Lee's worth. I'm just nervous waiting on Hamels given his yearly sore elbow thing. I don't want lingering questions about his health while he's being dealt.

I really think these two trades are the key. If one of them isn't healthy, then there's less margin for error elsewhere.
