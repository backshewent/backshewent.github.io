I think it would be foolish to conclude that JRoll is finished because of what he has done so far this year. His performance over these past few weeks is no different than stretches he has had over his entire career. What he usually does is follow up bad stretches with incredibly good stretches. His past year + of bad performance is magnified because he he never had a good stretch last year mainly because he was never healthy the entire year and because this is the last year of his contract. He might be on the verge of having an incredibly productive few weeks. If so, that would fit his pattern in the past.

He might be done but I'm not considering what he has done in 2011 as enough evidence in and of itself. Perhaps the injuries he has had over the past few years are preventing him from reaching the highs he has reached in the past. He is older, so that is also a variable to consider. But he has been so up and down over his career that a down stretch for a few weeks should not be considered unusual.
