Dune was awesome. I was a bit disappointed by the extremely abrupt ending and it could get a little ponderous at times down the home stretch but overall was a blast to read and rather thrilling at times. I think I was expecting big long walls of text establishing the mythology and culture of the universe so it was really nice that it drops you right into the action and has a lot of faith in the reader to just roll with it. I guess I could see that as frustrating to someone who was expecting more credible science or philosophy which is what I was kind of primed for by its reputation

(on a minor note, Yueh's role seemed like a weirdly glossed over plot-hole. not a big deal, but surprised it wasn't explained in some way)
