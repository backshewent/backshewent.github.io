by etched Chaos » Fri Sep 30, 2011 20:08:21

Was bored again and decided to check our starter's WAR total against everyone else in all the listed years on fangraphs.

Our WAR total of 25.8 is the highest total since '97 when the Braves had a total of 26.4. The 03' Yanks and '02 Dbacks came very close but fell short. Of all the recorded WAR totals on Fangraphs going back to '74 (before that, no WAR totals are listed) the '11 Phils are #2.

*Edit* Doing the same for ERA, the last team to have a rotation with a sub 3 ERA is the '92 Braves with a 2.95 ERA. Then the '88 Mets with a 2.97 ERA.

The '85 Dodgers and Mets are the only two teams in the last 26 years with a lower starter's ERA than our Phils mark of 2.86, the Dodgers with a 2.71 and the Mets with 2.84. So in conclusion this Phils rotation is the greatest starting rotation in the last 25 years by ERA and 2nd best since god knows when in WAR.
