Re: Phillies sign Jake Arrieta to 3-year $75 million deal

by mcare89 » Sun Mar 11, 2018 19:23:59

So, mostly for my own visualization, the deal is:

2018: 30M
2019: 25M
--Arrieta can opt out or Phillies trigger extension---
2020: 20M
--only if extended--
2021: 20M w/escalators based on GS and CYA finishing
2022: 20M w/same escalators

That seems like a good deal for the Phillies. If Arrieta ends up having two big years, he gets paid either way, and the Phillies don't get screwed by the player opt-out, instead getting a pretty favorable built-in extension. In the more likely scenario that he declines, as pitchers in their 30s tend to do, they don't get killed by a long-term.
