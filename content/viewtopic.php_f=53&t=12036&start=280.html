On an unrelated note, for some reason this morning I was thinking about the "old" NL East (the 1969-1993 NL East) and who won it the most often. So, here's the count:

1969-1993:

Pittsburgh - 9 division crowns (1970-1972, 1974-1975, 1979, 1990-1992; however, only team not to win one in the 1980s)
Philadelphia - 6 division crowns (1976-1978, 1980, 1983, 1993)
New York - 4 division crowns (1969, 1973, 1986, 1988)
St. Louis - 3 division crowns (1982, 1985, 1987)
Chicago - 2 division crowns (1984, 1989)
Montreal - 1 division crown (1981)
Florida - only played in the division one year, 1993

Just sort of amazing how good the Pirates used to be. Although, I suppose, for all those division titles, they were snakebit even then: only got 2 World Series appearances out of them (won them both, however).
