My wife packs the kids' lunches. Sometimes, I do it if she has something going on at night. The kids hate when I pack their lunches. They complain that I either pack too little or not the right stuff.

I pack my own lunch. I usually make a lot of something on Sunday...mostly ground turkey and veggies with different flavor profiles. Then I portion them out and keep them in the fridge. Grab a container and a piece of fruit (and 3 hard boiled eggs for breakfast - also made on Sunday), put them in my lunchbox and store it in the fridge over night. Easy peasy.

I also tend to keep a bag of carrots or celery sticks in the work fridge and I have a container of almonds/raisins at my desk to snack on if needed. (FYI - we buy raw almonds and roast them ourselves which taste much better than buying them roasted)

If we have those individual guacamole containers, I may add one of those to eat with my eggs in the morning. If I am feeling really crazy, I'll buy an avocado and mix 1/2 with peeled and chopped hard boiled eggs to eat for breakfast (think green egg salad).
