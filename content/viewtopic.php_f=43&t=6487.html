by DiamondDerby » Thu Sep 11, 2008 10:00:43

Just wanted to see how the prospects we often discuss would get shoe-horned into a 25-man roster 5-6 years from now and where our depth is. Assumes no players past arbitration today remain on team and no players acquired.

This shows obvious depth in outfield and starting pitching. Also, if Utley and Rollins are still on this fictional team, replacing Donald and Galvis (see below), the hitting stays near the top of the NL, IMO. Without them, it is closer to middle of the pack. The biggest upgrade potential is in starting pitching, both in elevating the quality of the starting five and depth in the system.
