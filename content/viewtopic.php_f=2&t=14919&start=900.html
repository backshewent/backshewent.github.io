Be very careful with the amount of pumpkin, especially if she's usually loose. Once threw away a crate and an area rug after feeding a constipated dog too much pumpkin.

Mine gets stubborn about eating sometimes too. The hunger strike is usually over a disagreement in the amount of attention she receives. A little bit of scrambled egg primes the pump every time. She goes back to eating normally after. The other routine snack she gets is carrots which will also usually work if put in her dish. A few drops of oil worked with previous dogs but that might loosen the stool further.

What kind of snacks and people food? This one gets almost zero dog treats. Carrots are #1 followed by completely plain popcorn.

Dog before this one occasionally ate grass but never got sick. Seemed to stop when she got the crunchy part of romaine lettuce a couple times a week.
