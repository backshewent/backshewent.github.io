I used to travel a lot at my old company and it was soul sucking at times. Although, I would travel back and forth to the same city for weeks and weeks and company policy allowed for us to have the hotel do our laundry. My new company does shorter trips like fly there on Tuesday and return on Thursday, which is better on the family life impacts since I'm only away for one full day, but it kind of sucks having to do the whole airport shuffle twice in three days.
