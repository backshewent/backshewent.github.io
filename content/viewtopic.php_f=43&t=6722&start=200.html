Game 1 Festivities
9:00 a.m. -- Majestic Clubhouse Store opens with new National League Championship Series merchandise.

5:00 p.m. -- OctoberPhest Phillies Postseason Party on Citizens Bank Way. Open to all fans (no game ticket necessary)

5:45 p.m. -- Ashburn Alley opens.

6:15 p.m. -- All gates open. Rally towels for all fans, compliments of American Water.

7:50 p.m. -- The Philadelphia Boys Choir and Men's Chorale will perform "The Philadelphia Song" and "America the Beautiful." Military, including the Pennsylvania Air National Guard 111th fighter wing and personnel from Willow Grove, will unfurl a large American flag.

7:54 p.m. -- Phillies and Dodgers introduced.

8:08 p.m. -- All-service Color Guard.

8:09 p.m. -- National Anthem: Sung by Philadelphia Boys Choir and Chorale.

8:13 p.m. -- Ceremonial First Pitch: Thrown by Gary Matthews, the 1983 NLCS MVP, and Garry Maddox, who had the game-winning hit and caught the last out when the Phillies won the 1980 pennant in Houston.

8:22 p.m. -- Phillies vs. Dodgers game begins.

7th Inning -- God Bless America: Sung by Air Force Staff Sergeant Jody Johnson.

Broadcast -- TV: FOX Sports with announcers Joe Buck and Tim McCarver. Radio: ESPN Radio, XM Radio and MLB.com. WPHT 1210 AM will feature one-hour pre- and post-game coverage hosted by Jim Jackson; Scott Franzke, Harry Kalas, Larry Andersen and Chris Wheeler will be behind the radio microphones; WUBA 1480 AM: Spanish Beisbol Network with hosts Bill Kulik, Danny Martinez and Juan Ramos.
