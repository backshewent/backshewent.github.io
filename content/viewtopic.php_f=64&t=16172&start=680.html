HR per plate appearance rate for ASG HR Derby participants. Yea, small sample size but it's been almost three weeks. I looked it up since Frazier, Fielder and Joc are jeopardizing the fantasy team where I'm actually contending.

Frazier 18.3 PA before, 54.0 PA since (winner)
Joc Pederson 15.0 PA before, 32.0 PA since (finalist)
Donaldson 18.8 PA before, 12.8 PA since (semifinalist)
Pujols 13.8 PA before, 15.5 PA since (semifinalist)
Rizzo 23.8 PA before, 17.5 PA since
Fielder 26.9 PA before, 65.0 PA since
Machado 19.9 PA before, 16.8 PA since
Bryant 28.3 PA before, 34.5 PA since

So the two guys in the final have both gone ice cold, while the others have mixed results. The overall totals for the group are a homer per 19.4 PA before the contest, and a homer per 22.4 PA since, so about a 15% dropoff which I guess isn't terrible
