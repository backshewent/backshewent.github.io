I had no real interest in either of these franchises in SB this year. Cam is young and brash. Whatev. I dont like teams getting Lombardis before mine. Peyton has annoyed me a lot over the years - but he's at least not pompous publicly. Like Elway's first one - he didn't do anything to help them win - he had less than 200 yards, no TDs and an INT. But the defense, ST and a few key plays running and in air - they won.

I hate the two weeks and over-hype and production of "Super Bowl" as an event -- just want a quality game and cap to a season.

As for the Birds moving forwards -- I don't believe any rumors now. Foles is not coming back. That's absurd. The cap/economics of it are not viable. He showed no success with Rams - who had a RB that won ROY.

I think they want Bradford but are reticent to pay him like an upper echelon guy and he has a good agent.

I really don't think there are a lot of for-sure options in terms of QBs already in the league. No idea if they are targeting and can pull off a QB in the draft.

Excited to see our defense get better - be fun if it could be a great one eventually. Such a shame that 1988-1992 defense w/ RC couldn't finish the deal.

Bring Shady home! Win SBs.
