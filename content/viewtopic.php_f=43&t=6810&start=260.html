by MattS » Sat Oct 25, 2008 13:11:00

Things that scare me:

LHP Jamie Moyer career numbers against:

LHB Carlos Pena: 10 for 20, 3 doubles, 2 homeruns, 2 bb, 4 k
LHB Carl Crawford: 9 for 19, 1 double, 1 triple, 0 bb, 1 k

No one else has more than 10 ABs against him. The case for the Phillies being more likely to win according to the BP odds is that the Rays have trouble with LHP. This is largely based on the splits of Carlos Pena (.994/.654 in 2008, .894/.752 career) and Carl Crawford (.754/.641 in 2008, .792/.695 career). The other LHB that Moyer will be up against is Iwamura who does not have much of a split at all. Otherwise, the Rays will trot out RHB and switch-hitters.

Moyer's ability to keep impatient hitters in check might be neutralized by the Rays who had the third most BB in the MLB this year.

Moyer needs to have a good game.
