So I have a confession.

I am a hypochondriac. A lot of people use the term lightly or in a joking manner, but it's real and it's crippling and a shitty way to live life. In the past five years, I've diagnosed myself with testicular cancer, melanoma, ALS, and colon cancer. Most of the time, I have to have actual medical tests to ease my mind, and then I go about my way for some time until I blow another symptom out of proportion -- my version of hypochondria is known as "somatic symptom disorder," meaning I have actual symptoms that I automatically assume are caused by something serious.
