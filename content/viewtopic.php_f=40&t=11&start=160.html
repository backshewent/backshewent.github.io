by Official Scorer » Fri Dec 29, 2006 20:57:08

Thank you for the very attractive board. Few, if any, will recognize me.

I used to post on the Phillies.com board prior to the Renegade site existed. Then I was very happy with Dave's site until the end of 2003 when things began getting off topic. That is when I left. Filtering through all the political stuff to find Phillies talk was frustrating to me.

Since then, I used to check in from time to time, but never did follow the flow for very long. I did register as Official Scorer at some point and even made a few posts. The site deteriorated, imo.

I was intrigued by the e-mails last week and all the drama. When I heard the site might go private, I thought I might like to return. But the demand of money and the seemingly off-the-cuff reaction to things turned me off.

I have been browsing all the other boards since and see there is still an active community. I relish sliding back into the fold again. I gladly sent in a donation just now. It's not the money, just the approach.

Because I have been out of the loop, as it were, for so long, while I recognize many of the handles, I do not know the politics of who is who. And I prefer it that way.

Just as an introduction, I like scoring games and am working on the particulars of a project for the upcoming season to collate a scorecard from each Phillies game. I hope many of you will participate.

Thanks for the board and I look forward to Phillies discussion.
