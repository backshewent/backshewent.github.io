Called the dealer today and explained the situation. They let me know that they could do a title search and expedited title transfer without the physical document for a fee of $122.50, which seemed acceptable vs. having to find a notary and go through PennDOT and wait 7-10 days or whatever (and paying $55 anyway).

So we have a new (used) car. I obviously realized this is considerably larger than the car it replaces, but I was concerned primarily with length. The 10" increase in width makes it barely fit in the garage; with both side mirrors extended it clears the sides of the garage door by less than an inch on each side. With one or both mirrors folded in there's a bit more leeway but this makes backing out a little more challenging, and it's not a power folding mirror. Also getting in and out of and around the car while it's in the garage is pretty tight. Fortunately the previous homeowners paved a large patch adjacent to the side of the garage so there's a relatively convenient place to park other than the garage, but we've gotten very used to not having to go out in the elements to get in the car. Oh well, first world problems I guess.
