by nycphils » Tue Dec 15, 2009 10:53:30

Now that the dust has started to settle and it appears that Ramirez is also coming back to the Phils, I look at it this way (reserving the right to change my mind, of course!):

This is 2 deals in isolation - the Halladay end is so expensive (Drabek/Happ, Taylor, D'Arnaud) because they are sending $6 million to us to defray the salary. That's the way it is - if $$ coming back, the prospect cost goes WAY up. This is the the part that makes it hard to get excited about getting Halladay - it came down to money, and not much money in the scheme of things, which harkens back to the dark days of the Phillies pre-CBP. Also, I think Taylor is a sure-fire star - think John Olerud from the right side - so I find that to be a huge price.

Lee for Aumont, Ramirez and Gillies - without Ramirez, this would be terrible - but with him, seems to be OK. Could they get more elsewhere? Maybe, but it would seem that Aumont =Carrasco, Ramirez isn't much of a drop off from Knapp, and Gillies is more boom or bust than Marson or Donald, but is probably not as much value as those two together. When you add in Francisco, who is solid and controllable for a while and the 1/2 great year and playoffs, we seem to have done OK on the Lee end, though not as great as I might have hoped. The hard part is that we stole him originally and now we've given back that extra value.
