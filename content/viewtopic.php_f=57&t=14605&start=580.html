by Bill McNeal » Thu May 02, 2013 15:23:56

I'd rather just keep Howard and trade Lee, Kendrick or whoever and get better prospects. If you can field a team with a couple of (good) starting position players in pre/early arbitration years his $25mil/ season isn't a huge deal. When you are maxed out on payroll at the luxury tax he makes it makes it hard to add elite FA hitters, but if you are bringing them up from the minors it would be doable. Now that said, if you can give him away and pay half the salary, I'm all for that, but I wouldn't diminish a trade asset just to get him off the books. At the end of the day the issue isn't money, even if the payroll goes down, we are still going to be a top 10 payroll team, the issue is talent, we need to whatever we can to acquire talent.
