by TheDude24 » Wed Dec 01, 2010 14:35:58

I enjoy these podcasts for movies.

-Film Junk (4 guys in Ontario talk movies and joke around. Organized in regular segments: news, new movie reviews, trailers, other stuff we watched, listener mail, new on DVD.) filmjunk.com

-Row Three (I usually skip past the reviews of the week and just listen to them talk about other things they watched, the reviews are too long and spoilery, but I love the rest.) rowthree.com

-The Movie Club Podcast (Includes hosts from Film Junk and Row Three and others. Every 3 months or so they discuss 2 movies with a common theme.) movieclubpodcast.blogspot.com

-The Documentary Blog Podcast (one of the hosts is Jay from Film Junk. Guests are documentary film directors) thedocumentaryblog.com

People sometimes ask me where I heard of all the cool, weird, or interesting films I mention to them. Mainly it is because of these podcasts. Check them out--especially Film Junk, if you love movies.
