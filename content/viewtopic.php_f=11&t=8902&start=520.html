by FTN » Tue Jul 21, 2009 13:15:34

since we're now just sitting around choking the collective chicken waiting for something to happen, i went to BP and looked at the PECOTA cards for the prospects being mentioned most frequently in the deal for Halladay. As you know, PECOTA is updated every year, and the projections on the player cards are based on preseason projections. So right now, PECOTA doesn't know what Taylor has done this year, or that Drabek is back from surgery and healthy. But you can look at how these prospects were valued by PECOTA heading into 2009. The Upside and MORP values are for 2009-2015, so a 7 year period

Drabek; MORP of $3.85M, total Upside of 9.0, WARP of -3.6
Taylor; MORP of $5.18M, total Upside of 26.3, WARP of 3.4
Marson; MORP of $22.63M, total Upside of 81.1, WARP of 13.7
Donald; MORP of $8.48M, total Upside of 44.9, WARP of 4.4

Again, this includes zero data from 2009. But Pecota saw a lot of value in Marson. I wonder how that's changed, if its changed.
