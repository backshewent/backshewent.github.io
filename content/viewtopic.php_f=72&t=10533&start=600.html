On the plane ride home last night, she delivered another daughter. The rest of the time in Clearwater was meetings, alcohol, swimming, alcohol, tanning, alcohol, dinner at Mike Alstott's restaurant, alcohol.....alcohol....um...more meetings...so yeah, awesome time...
