It's a little late to be typing this up but here is how I am handicapping the Preakness. There are two horses who can win this race IMO- the #7 Hard Spun and #8 Street Sense. Early speed seems to be holding up very well today so I am going to make a nice size play on #7 Hard Spun to wire the field. The only other speed horse in the race is the #2 Xchanger and he is one of the two horses that I am throwing out. He has no business running in this race and Hard Spun will easily put him away allowing HS to control the pace. Despite being the most or second most talented horse in the field, Street Sense doesn't really have a good running style for this track nor race and at 3/2 there simply isn't enough value to bet him to win. Don't get me wrong it wouldn't surprise me one bit if SS wins the race and he'll be in my exotics but the value isn't there to bet him to win.

The other horse that I am throwing out is #1 Mint Slewlep. No shot.
