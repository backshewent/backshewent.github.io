by Rockinghorse » Tue Aug 29, 2017 17:44:53

I haven't updated this thread in a while as we moved from Dallas to Center City. Unfortunately our senior (15 yo) was laid to rest a couple weeks before the move, which was for the best. We were planning to bring him but over the final few weeks he had a recurrence of vestibular disease (dizziness) and the meds didn't help. So we found an at-home service to peacefully put him to rest on his favorite bed looking out the window.

So now it's just the 8 year old, Maggie. She was an excellent passenger on the 3-day road trip and put up with a few more days in an AirBnB in Ardmore before we got into the house.

City living is a huge adjustment for her. She loves the squirrels everywhere and frequent attention she gets from people on the streets. But the potty situation has been rough as we have no grass for two full blocks - male dogs have a huge advantage is this area as they can just hoist a leg anyplace. Luckily we have a small patch of mulch/overgrowth in our yard.

I'm sure I'll have updates with questions and complaints. Wish we could still do the puppy fostering but the whole point of that was prepping Texas pups for the trip up north for adoption. Our house is too small now anyway. We'll probably head to Syracuse once in a while to visit/help out the end point of our former foster program.
