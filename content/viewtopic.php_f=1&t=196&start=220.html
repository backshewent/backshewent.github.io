Re: bowling (OT)

by SK790 » Sun Feb 15, 2015 11:12:55

the oil patterns are more variable on PBA patterns. if you miss too far toward the gutter, your ball is going to either come in light or miss the pocket completely. you miss too far to the middle, your ball is going through the face or cross to the other side. you can miss by a board* or two on "house" patterns and be okay. On PBA patterns, sometimes you miss by a half board and you miss.

*if you look down when you're bowling, you'll notice several slender "boards", it's useful for aiming.
