I loved KC ever since a friend of mine turned me on to them in college. It might have been obvious with my old avatar which was the logo for the Discipline album, but I wasn't sure anyone would ever catch on to that.

I listen to a ton of prog and what is loosely defined as "math rock", but I never got into Floyd that much outside of a few albums in the middle of their career. I'd recommend another King Crimson album like In The Court, but that album is pretty much one of a kind. It was the only album they made with those members and the only album that has that sort of sound.

The history of King Crimson is pretty interesting, but without getting into major detail, the band is pretty much just Robert Fripp and whoever he wants to play around him, forming and disbanding the band whenever he pleases. Any thing you hear from them is sure to be very good musically, due to the level of talent they've had over the years.
