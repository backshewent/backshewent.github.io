Disco Stu wrote:
CFP wrote:Weird thing is he sucks at CBP. OPS is 106 points higher on the road. He's having a really strange season.


It's called an anomoly.

Perhaps not. The Phillies are scoring 5.2 runs and have a .786 OPS on the road. They have scored 4.9 runs and have a .768 OPS at home. They have a 3.80 ERA at home on a .719 OPS. They have a 4.03 ERA on the road with a .770 OPS. CBP is playing as a pitcher's park thus far this year. While Feliz would be expected to be better on the road, his difference is bigger than expected. His home OPS is similar to what he did in San Francisco. His road OPS is much higher. If CBP keeps playing as a pitchers' park, his road OPS should drop closer to his career averages and his home OPS probably won't increase much.
