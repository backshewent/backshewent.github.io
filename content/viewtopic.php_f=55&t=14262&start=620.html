Steaks latest article has Michael Young as an option for Phils to play 3rd.


Third base remains another area that the Phils would like to upgrade. They are looking for a short-term fit as not to block the path of prospect Cody Asche. Free-agent Kevin Youkilis is an obvious fit. Veteran Texas infielder Michael Young could also be a fit if some hurdles could be cleared. Young has a no-trade clause and is signed for 2013 at $16 million. Sources say the Phillies have interest in Young, but the player would have to waive his no-trade clause and the Rangers would have to pick up a chunk of his salary. Young has spent his entire 12-year career with the Rangers.
