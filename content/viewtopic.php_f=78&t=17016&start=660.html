I find that the beef stick is often the most overlooked piece of American cuisine. Once upon a time the only choice was a slim Jim or if you were lucky to have a butcher that made these for their deli counter. Over the past few years, jacks links and tillamook have entered into the foray with many other "micro" sticks.

Today I found arguably the tastiest six inch beef stick that I ever put in my mouth. It was called King Kalibur spicy black angus beef stick. You could taste the difference the black angus makes from the very first bite. The texture was amazing and the "spicy" did not overwhelm the flavor of the beef but totally enhanced the enjoyment of the stick.

Flagstaff Arizona is the only place I've seen them sold in a retail store, however they do have a website.

http://www.kingkalibur.com

If you ever get the opportunity to bite into one of their black angus beef sticks you will not be disappointed.
