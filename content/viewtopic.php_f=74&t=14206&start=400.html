phatj wrote:As good at what?


I think Swift is a major songwriting talent. She will have to broaden her palette and get beyond "this guy I dated turned out to be a jerk" but songs like Mean are simply excellent. I think she has a lot more in common with Alanis Morrisette (who I like a lot) and maybe even Tori Amos than say Miley Cyrus or Rihanna. Of her pop contemporaries, I'd say only Adele is in her league, though Adele has a more interesting voice. However, from what I've heard off Red (remember, Lil' Vulture is an 11 year old girl, so I hear a lot more of this music than I'd like) it's not as good as Speak Now. So maybe she won't realize her potential.

She's in a weird place--straddling the line between pop star, country star, and singer-songwriter. I don't know if it's possible to balance all that.
