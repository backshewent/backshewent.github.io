You have to buy the plastic bags in europe and china, so I got into the habit of bringing bags. It's great.

On a similar note, I started taking take-out/tupperware containers to places when I order take out. It only works if you are ordering the take-out in person, but it saves all the extra take-out containers and plastic stuff. Sometimes, the restaurant will even give you extra stuff because they don't have to pay for the cost of containers and they like not using the extra plastic. Another side benefit is that the containers usually seal better than those the restaurant would give you, so less chance of spillage, which was an issue when riding the train and getting chinese an hour away from home.
