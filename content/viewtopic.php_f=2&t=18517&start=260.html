Understanding that most of this would never fly, here is an idea:

- age restriction (possibly 21 or prior military/law enforcement service)
- extensive background checks (paid for by purchaser) for all purchases. background checks include criminal, national security, health exam and mental health evaluation
- need a license acquired through written and practical tests
- licenses and background check renewal required every 5 years
- each gun must be registered annually
- each gun must carry liability insurance
- if your gun is involved in a crime, you are liable regardless of who committed the crime
- each purchase involves a waiting period
- restriction on magazine capacity
- Ammo tax (proceeds specifically to go towards improved mental health care)
- You are not allowed to sell your gun to anyone but law enforcement or a registered gun dealer.
