by Trolley Wire » Sun Aug 28, 2011 18:48:09

I remember taping that '87 highlight film off Channel 29 before a Spring Training game in '88. That might be the first time I've seen it since then

The Luis Aguayo walkoff against the Mets shown in the intro took place during the final week of the season. The Mets were basically on life support in the NL East race by that point and pretty much needed to win out to catch the Cardinals. They won the first game of the series at the Vet, but the Phillies took the last two. Don Carman pitched a 1-hitter in the middle game, the only hit an infield single by Mookie Wilson. I believe it was the only baserunner the Mets had that night. Then Aguayo hit the game-winner off Jesse Orosco in the final game, which I think knocked the magic number down to 1 for the Cardinals, who would win the East and the NL pennant
