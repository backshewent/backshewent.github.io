As long as we are talking about fast food places, the one I really miss is Roy Rogers. I still try to hit one whenever I am going West on the Turnpike or South on I-95 to Maryland/DC. They were always my favorite. There are still a few free-standing ones (read: non-rest-stop locations), mostly in Maryland and Northern Virginia, but I did manage to hit one in Connecticut back in 2006 on my way back from Boston.

There were two Roy Rogerses around my area when I was a kid. One of them is now and has been a Boston Chicken/Market (Willow Grove) since that franchise first appeared, and the other is some kind of pizza joint (Warminster).

In my sillier moments, I think it would be awesome to own a Roy Rogers franchise and help bring it back. It was so much better than McDonald's, BK, etc. And I even like the Whopper.
