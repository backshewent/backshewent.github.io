Bringing a little European flavoring to the early life of this thread, but I found this article interesting. It's well known how burdensome the French tax codes have been on spurring economic recovery, especially given Hollandes platform of reversing the entitlement regulatory reforms made by Sarkozy. Still, the degree to which France is levying new taxes is pretty crazy, it's to a point now where even the French are acknowledging its excessive and causing an exodus of sorts of talent out of France.

As a disclaimer I'm not saying this is what Obamacare is going to do to America or drawing parallels to here, not even close. If people think we are taxed to death here than they should really look at France. This just kind of highlights a different approach to the Merkel's of the world who would prefer austerity measures to cut deficit gaps.
