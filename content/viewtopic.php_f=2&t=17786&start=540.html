How does the longer video show RyanCare is a better way? All he says is that instead of the larger population of individual insurance buyers paying for the sick, the states will be able to set up high risk pools which will be subsidized by the Federal government.

IOW instead of healthy insurance buyers subsidizing the unhealthy, healthy taxpayers will subsidize the unhealthy. It's just a financial shell game.

Of course what he's really saying is that, because the high risk pools will be subsidized through Medicaid block grants and those grants will be frozen, there will be less and less over time to pay for subsidies. Someone will have to make up the difference, whether it's the states and their taxpayers or the unhealthy themselves. If not, those in those high risk pools will no longer be able to afford health insurance.

Ryan doesn't care whether the result is higher state taxes or the unhealthy not being able to afford health insurance. He will have capped costs of Medicaid at the Federal level and if the states increase taxes to cover increased costs or let people go without health insurance he can blame the states. Not his problem!
