Did some quick research since the Phils will probably be in last place at the All-Star break. Since the split to six divisions and the addition of the wildcard in 1994, here's the list of teams that were in 4th or 5th place at the All-Star break and went on to make the playoffs:

1995 Yankees = 4th place, 8.0 GB = won the wildcard
1995 Mariners = 4th (last) place, 5.0 GB = won the division, beat NY in the ALDS
2003 Marlins = 4th place, 13.0 GB - won the wildcard, won the World Series
2004 Astros = 5th place, 10.5 GB - won the wildcard, beat Atlanta in the NLDS
2007 Rockies = 4th place, 5.5 GB - won the wildcard, won the pennant
2010 Giants = 4th place, 4.0 GB - won the division, won the World Series

The only team to come back from down 10.0+ and win the division was the 2006 Twins, who were in 3rd place, 11.0 out.
