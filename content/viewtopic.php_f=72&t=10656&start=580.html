The Frankensandwich:

1 KFC Double Down
1 Wendy's Triple Baconator
1 Friendly's Grilled Cheese Burger Melt
1 large Pizza Hut's Meat Lover's pizza
6 eggs
1 cup flour

Scatter components of the three sandwiches over the pizza. Roll pizza burrito-style around sandwiches. Secure with baling wire.

Whisk together eggs and flour in a wide shallow dish. Dredge the Frankensandwich in the batter. Deep-fry.

Serve with fries and Diet Coke.
