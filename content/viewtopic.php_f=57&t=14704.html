I know you guys had to listen to me bitch and whine before the season started about this stuff, so no need to recount the previous stuff here. But I think the thing that's annoying me the most right now, and it's something I didn't mention before the season, is that the team was constructed so that it basically has no strengths anymore, beyond the starting pitching (which hasn't really been a strength, though I don't think that's his fault). The defense is bad and the speed is bad and the power is bad and the on-base skills are bad. If he wanted to remake the team in his own twisted image, it would have been nice if there was something it did well. It would be nice if we were given a reason to watch.
