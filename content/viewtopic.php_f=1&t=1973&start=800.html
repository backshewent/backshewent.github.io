I would reraise allin, no questions asked. it's no time to get cute. 95K is in the pot, 2 people are still to act with two in the hand already and you may get a caller who thinks you are trying to steal from teh button. Calling is a horrible option here, IMO. You are hoping an ace doesn't hit? 175K reraise allin makes the pot 270, 175k to call, but the originally raiser has only 80k. This is a hand where all hte money gets in the middle, it may as well be right away.

Is there really somehow another way to play this? Are peopel really going to fold an 80k bet postflop, even if an ace is out there? CU's range is pretty high and is probably looking to see a flop. Knock him out with the allin reraise, if it's possible. 115k to call 350 may not knock him out, but it *could* knock some out, and you are probably ahead anyway.
