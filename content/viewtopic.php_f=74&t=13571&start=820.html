don't feel bad, most don't

Mine didn't either, but I accumulated enough in my flexible spending account over a few years and didn't pay a cent. Also almost went blind in one eye. So.


How expensive is it roughly? I get $1200 a year for eye/dental, was thinking about getting it done in the fall. Blind part has me skeptical.

Mine was just around 5 grand.

Long story short- They didn't give me enough anesthesia in my left eye, so I started feeling the apparatus holding my eye open and tried to blink which caused the thing to scratch the hell out of my eye. The whole thing happens so fast (in the blink of an eye?) that there was literally nothing I could do.

I woke up the next day unable to open my eye because it was so scratched up and my eyelid was therefore irritated. Had to live life with Sloth-eye for a month before it healed and they did PRK on that eye.

So in closing, make sure they give you enough local anesthesia. Mine may have been a special case because it took longer to set up the machine due to my deeply set eyes. I have no regrets and would do it again.

Also, do not take Percocet without eating.
