by JFLNYC » Thu Jul 30, 2009 10:30:48

Yes, I think the confusion may be over so-called "Repeater Rights" under the MLB CBA and has to do with:

(a) "Any Player who has 5 or more years of Major League service at the time of the assignment of his contract and whose contract was executed prior to October 23, 2006 and covers the next succeeding season;" and

(b) Whose "contract with the signing Club contains a no-trade provision which limits the signing Club’s right to assign the Player’s contract to no more than sixteen (16) Clubs designated or subsequently to be designated by the Player."

If a player who qualifies requests a trade or free agency, he cannot exercise that right again "until he has completed an additional 3 years of Major League service."

BTW, if he requests a trade or free agency, he loses his Moving Allowance.
