It's been awhile so I think I'll run down the list of those on my podcast app, in no particular order

Dude Soup (Funny takes on video game news with Funhaus, IGN refugees that joined Roosterteeth's news crew "The Know")
Dead Authors Podcast (Paul F Tompkins is H.G. Wells interviewing comedians pretending to be dead authors, basically an hour long improv scene)
Weekly Planet (Two Australians discussing comic book movies and TV shows or summer blockbusters)
Roosterteeth Podcast
Doug Loves Movies
In Our Time w/ Melvyn Bragg (Dry, British, fact-based discussion abut historical or science topics with relevant experts)
Official Eagles Podcast
Comedy Bang Bang
Radiolab
Infinite Monkey Cage (Science discussion with experts and comedians)
Nerdist
Superego (Well produced and edited improv show)
You Made it Weird w/ Pete Holmes
How Did This Get Made
The Patch (Roosterteeth's original video game podcast)
Professor Blastoff
Cashing In w/ T.J. Miller
Mike and Tom Eat Snacks
ESPN Football Today
ESPN Fantasy Focus Football
Indoor Kids
Wits (NPR sketch show w/ comedians and musicians)
FEAB (Matt Mira from Nerdist and Scott Mosier from Smodcast)
James Bonding
Rachel and Miles X-Plain the X-Men
Welcome to Nightvale
Ask Me Another (The NPR show that isn't Wait, Wait)
Thrilling Adventure Hour (Radio Play style comedic serialized stories)
Mission Log (James Bonding for Start Trek episodes and movies, now up to TNG)
Cracked Podcast
Dining with Doug and Karen
Nerd Poker (Brian Posehn and friends play D&D, eventually)
Crit Juice (not well known actors and comedians play D&D with drinking rules)
Critical Hit (Nerds play D&D for real)
Hardcore History
Common Sense w/ Dan Carlin


Basically, I'm a giant dork that doesn't watch TV or listen to the radio while driving.
