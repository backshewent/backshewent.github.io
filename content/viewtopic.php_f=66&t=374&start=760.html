This infuriates me to no end.

When I'm passing, but not quite fast enough for the guy behind me, I will move over... unless you pull a !@#* move by tailgating me or (worse) flashing your brights. Whenever anyone does that to me, I match the speed of the car I'm passing for as long as I can in response.

I think tailgaters drive me craziest of anything on the road. If I'm on a one lane per direction road and someone tailgates me, I won't slam on the brakes. I will, however, take my foot off the gas, and coast until the guy behind me finally gets the picture and backs off. I won't resume my speed until that happens. Safer than doing a brake check, and more fun since I get to watch the @($#!*)'s face turn purple with rage behind me.

You can speed all you want past me, as long as you're a little patient and use your turn signals. Fail to do those simple things, though, and retribution will be swift and merciless.
