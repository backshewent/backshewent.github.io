by jerseyhoya » Sun Nov 04, 2012 00:44:51

If you or a relative have been displaced by Hurricane Sandy in NJ, you (or they) can apply to vote via email. You have to submit a mail-in ballot application by fax or email to your county clerk, and then they'll email you a ballot. If you go this route you also have to sign a waiver of confidentiality for your vote, as whomever receives it will see who you're voting for. And I think you have to include both the ballot and the waiver for the vote to count.

Contact your county clerk for more info on what/where/how to send.
