by CFP » Tue Jul 24, 2012 20:43:53

There is actually another work-around, but I don't know if I should share it/it's complicated.

Basically click on a link from the Times site, then try to stop the page before it loads all the way (by stop I mean the X in your browser where the refresh button would be). If you do it right, it won't count against your limit, and the entire article will load. Yes, this is confusing and takes some practice/is not a foolproof way, but it has worked for me. In the end, I have a subscription right now and get free digital access, but that subscription runs out soon so I will probably go back to this way.
