Dallas Cowboys owner Jerry Jones was a little taken aback Wednesday when informed of defensive end Greg Hardy's curious choice of words to describe his return from a four-game suspension stemming from a domestic violence charge.

Jones, who is attending the NFL owners meetings in New York, then paused before continuing.
