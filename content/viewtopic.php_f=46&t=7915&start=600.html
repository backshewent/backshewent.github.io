by SideshowBob » Tue Mar 17, 2009 16:33:48

BigEd76 wrote:And apparently Chipper is whining about the WBC, saying he'll never do it again under this format because there's too many off days...


I actually agree that there are too many off days and no reason not to compress the tournament (though the off days seem to be there purely for TV purposes with maybe some travel). That said, why can't the national teams just play exhibitions in between the games on the off-days if that's the issue?

And, furthermore, the biggest issue IMHO is that this should not be in lieu of spring training. It's really pretty simply to have the WBC teams start their "spring training" 2 weeks before the regular spring training starts. They can play against the other national teams or they can do intersquad scrimmages. Other national teams in other sports have training camps where the players work togerther and get into shape. And other WBC participants have as well (Japan starting working out in Jan and most of the Carribean teams feature players who played in winter ball).
