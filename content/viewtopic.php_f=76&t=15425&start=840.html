by Bucky » Wed Apr 16, 2014 20:19:33

Oh OK, if you insist...

In the case of multipage documents, the most effective way to safeguard against fraud (page-switching) is to emboss all pages of every document notarize. If someone protests your embossing, tell them that you don’t have TIME to go to court after they do something fraudulent with their document, therefor, you take precautions against any tampering by embossing every page. It is hard to forge an embosser, and hard to use it in the same way a notary uses it. It might be easy to spot a false notarization which is important to get you out of court fast. Imagine how many hundreds you would lose every day you were hijacked by a court case! - See more at: http://blog.123notary.com/?p=1706#sthash.mu5ipLm6.dpuf
