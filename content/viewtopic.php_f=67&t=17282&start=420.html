Summary of the BA Draft Report Card on the Phillies 2016 Draft:

Best Pure Hitter: Moniak (says Josh Stephen shows promise with the bat)
Best Power: 1B Darick Hall (but also notes SS Cole Stobbe as having plus raw power)
Fastest Runner: Moniak
Best Defensive Player: Moniak (with a mention of OF David Martinelli)
Best Fastball: RHP Kevin Gowdy (with mentions of LHP JoJo Romero and RHP Blake Quinn)
Best Secondary Pitch: Gowdy (with a mention of RHP Grant Dyer's plus curveball)
Best Pro Debut: Moniak (with mentions for LHP Cole Irvin, Dyer and Quinn)
Best Athlete: Moniak (with a mention that Gowdy has a black belt in Tae-Kwon-do)
Most Intriguing Background: LHP Jonathan Hennigan is the grandson of a former major leaguer. Moniak's granddad got drafted.
Closest to the majors: Nobody obvious but mentions Romero, Irvin, Dyer and Quinn as moving faster.
Best Late Round Pick: OF Josh Stephen with a mention of LHP Kyle Young
The One That Got Away: SS Logan Davidson and RHP Davis Agle

On the League-wide lists, mentioned Phillies picks:

Best Drafts: #4 - Phillies (1. Reds, 2. Braves, 3. Brewers, 5. Red Sox)
Best Pure Hitter #2 - Mickey Moniak OF
Best Defensive Player - #1 Mickey Moniak OF
Best Pro Debut HS - #2 Mickey Moniak OF
Best Late Round Pick - #2 Kyle Young RHP
Closest to the Majors HS - #1 Mickey Moniak OF
