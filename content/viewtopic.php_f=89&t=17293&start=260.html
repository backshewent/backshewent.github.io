by Gimpy » Thu Jun 16, 2016 14:47:25

Noel's skills (shot blocking, overall defensive impact, and steals) are skills you see in bigs who swing playoff games and his deficiencies don't kill him.

Okafor's warts take more off the table than what his skills bring to it. I'm sure he'll improve, but it is what it is right now.

Also, regarding trading a protected pick, the weakness there is that it limits you in the future since you owe a pick, but you don't know what year you owe it yet. Like if the Lakers had tried to trade a pick last year, it would have had to convey two years after ours so they'd likely have to weaken the protection on the next pick each year. It's conceivable that they'd end up giving us a top five pick next year and then lose another high pick two years later. Or look at the Kings who had to send us a pick that's going to be unprotected in 2019 because of their trade with the Bulls. I'm counting on us not being terrible year over year, but you still are putting yourself in a shaky position later on if you have outstanding picks years away.
