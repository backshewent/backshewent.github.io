by philsfan1979 » Mon Apr 13, 2009 14:52:01

This is such a sad day.

After a game during the last year of Jack Russell Stadium in Clearwater, my friend and I went up to the broadcast booth to try to get Harry's autograph. But my friend saw that the people in front of us handed him a cell phone and he did whatever they asked him to do with it. So my friend decides to call another friend of ours and gets him on the phone just as we approach Harry in the booth.

After Harry talked to us for a few seconds and signed our programs, my friend handed him the phone and asked Harry if he could just talk to our friend for a bit. Harry says, "Sure, what's your friend's name?" We tell him that it's Jim and Harry takes the phone and all we hear is something like this:

"Hey, Jim, this is Harry Kalas. How are you?...Watching the NCAA Tournament, eh?...Well, take care, Jim. It's getting late so I am outta heerrrrre!"

We again shook Harry's hand and thanked him. This is what our friend on the other end says: "That...was...awesome. I gotta call my dad."

I just sent that friend a text message saying I was thinking about that time. In his reply, he said "...that's how cool Harry was. It's like a small piece of my life is gone."

We will miss you, Harry.

RIP
