Last night, I made a kick ass pot roast. I believe the key to its success were--plenty of liquid (water, wine, and a can of cream of mushroom soup) and seasoning (salt, pepper, garlic, onion, fresh thyme and fresh rosemary) and cooking it for 3.5 hours at 350. I cut it away from the bone, and threw the bone in the pot as well.
