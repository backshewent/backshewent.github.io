by Drugs Delaney » Sun Sep 30, 2007 22:51:18

Today was the best day I've ever had as a Phillies fan. I was six in 1980 and wasn't able to fully appreciate it. I was 19 in 1993 and fully appreciated it, but I was in college in Dayton, OH and was a little detached from it. This year has been the best because I've been able to go to more games than ever before and I've been better able to follow along with every pitch than I have before. It's just been more fun the whole season.

Today just caps it beautifully. The crowd at the game was simply incredible. I've never exchanged as many high fives in my life as I did today. It was just an amazing experience seeing it all in person. Awesome stuff.



I was enjoying the game so much that I forgot to take pictures earlier.





Nice view of Aaron Rowand from section 146.





I think this is Chase Utley at the plate.





Rally towels were everywhere. The Phils did an awesome thing giving them out to everyone. They really do add something to the game experience.





Division champs!





The main scoreboard says it, too!





Better shot.





I couldn't get a good shot of it, but that's a 2007 NL East Division Champions flag just to the left of the Bell. Awesome that the Phils were prepared for it.





More than an hour after the game, the scoreboard still said it while we did some post-game tailgating.


This day was just incredible and I caught some video of the last couple outs and then the post-game frenzy. Can't really see the action on the field, but these videos will tell you a little about how awesome it was to be at the game today.
