by RichmondPhilsFan » Tue Dec 11, 2012 16:57:25

Bill McNeal wrote:I'm going to get my wife a tablet for Christmas. Anyone have any experience with the kindle fire? I'm either getting her that, an iPad or an iPad mini.

Unless you're an Amazon Prime subscriber, go with the iPad over the Fire. Even if you're an Android user, you'd have have to repurchase nearly all of your apps anyway b/c Amazon locks you out of the Google Play marketplace (for obvious reasons). One of my coworkers got the Fire HD shortly after it came out. He likes it, but hates being stuck with the Amazon infrastructure. I've played with it before and chose the Nexus 7 b/c I didn't like it very much. It seems like more of a "hey this is a great tablet for the non-tech-savvy parents and their youngish kids to share" type thing. The only real upside IMO is the HDMI port but that really isn't a big deal unless I'm traveling b/c I can stream Amazon video to my blu-ray player.

I chose a 7" tablet partly due to portability reasons and partly b/c I really wanted a Nexus device (but the 10" starts at $399). If price isn't an issue you might as well get the full-sized iPad and skip the Mini. Hell, the iPad 2 is downright affordable at this point and there's not much of a difference between the two devices for non-iSnobs.
