Other things you can do:

-- Limit its exposure to the cold weather
-- Charge the battery when it's below 10%, and don't let it run all the way to zero
-- Change your email settings to check less often, synchronize only a few folders, etc.
-- Disable as many app notifications as possible
-- Remove any apps you rarely/never use
-- Switch vibrations to sounds
-- Limit the web browsing
