by jeff2sf » Mon May 11, 2009 21:12:48

Let's say you have two columns. Column A is Customer - ranging from # 1 to 100. Column B is Revenue from customer and you've sorted it such that Customer 1 is the one with the most revenue, Customer 100 the one with the least. Make a Column C called Cumulative Revenue. For customer 1, this is 0 + his revenue (cell B1). For customer 2, it's cell C1+B2, Customer 3, cell C2+B3 and so on. Then make a column D where you do % Cum Revenue which simply is each cell in Column C divided by Total Revenue

Now, graph this with Column A as the horizontal Axis and Column D as Vertical Axis. Bring it into powerpoint and you have a chart I've wowed customers with.
