Talk about a weird song association. Whenever I hear Better Than Ezra's "Desperately Wanting" (I remember running through the wet grass... falling a step behind...), I remember a mix tape I put together.

I remember I would have my fingers poised (pointer finger over the play button, middle finger over record), and whenever a new song came on the radio, I would instantly hit play. Upon hearing the opening chords, I'd realize it either was or wasn't a song I wanted on my mix. If it wasn't, I'd quickly rewind to find the exact conclusion of the previous song (before the DJs started jabbering) before the current song on the radio was complete. Good times. I remember Desperately Wanting occupying the last 30 seconds of one of my mix tapes. I didn't want 30 seconds of blank tape. Thus, whenever I hit the 30 second mark of this Better Than Ezra song, I expect the tape to stop.
