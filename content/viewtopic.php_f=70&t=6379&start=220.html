First gig of my life- we got called to encore. Problem is, we'd already played all the songs we'd rehearsed. Bass player decides we should do freebird. Guitarist, vocalist, and drummer all also knew it- just me who didn't. So I pop a church organ tone onto my Roland SA-90, and the bass player gets me started on the song, as I have the intro all to myself. On the tape of the show, you can hear him yelling out the chords. "G!" "D" ! "E Minor" "E Minor" !
