by phatj » Wed Aug 14, 2013 19:54:37

Went car shopping today, as my 2002 Mazda Protege5 is showing its age. The idea is to get a very economical car to be our daily commuter/weekend trip car and the Mazda5 we also own can be the hauler, with the eventual intention to replace the Mazda5 with a larger vehicle that can haul even more (my wife does a lot of wood-working stuff and wants something that can haul 2x4s and sheet plywood and stuff).

Went to a Toyota dealer first, intending to look at the Yaris as it's their econobox. They only had one new one on the lot, and one used one. The latter was a manual. I drove it and it was fine. They also had a 2009 or '10 Prius that we drove - it was a bit more than we were looking at spending, but a much nicer car than the Yaris. We also drove a Scion xD which was the slowest car I've ever driven, including the '04 Kia Rio I had as a loaner years ago. I mean, I had the pedal to the metal and it went like a grocery cart. Anyway, we both loved the Prius, it was a far more premium-feeling car than the other two and promises at least 10 MPG better in our daily use. But the dealer experience was not good. The first thing we said when we sat down with the sales rep was that we were just starting to look and weren't going to be buying today. While we were there we reiterated this and the guy looked at us and shook his head with a smirk. Before we left he got some other guy to sit down with us to try to talk us into it.

On the way home we stopped at a Kia dealer, and drove the brand-new Rio. A much (MUCH) nicer car than the loaner I had back when. It was nearly as smooth and quiet (other than under acceleration, when I actually like to hear the engine) as the Prius, with a remarkable amount of standard features. When we were done we told the dealer that we weren't interested in having them look at our current car for trade-in purposes, and he didn't push it.

Anyway, we're planning to look at the Honda Fit, Nissan Versa and Ford Fiesta still. Thoughts on any of this? I'm leaning towards the Prius at this time despite really not liking the dealer.
