Borderline epic fail.

Got there shortly after nine, right by the gate we were supposed to use to get in. Capitol police told us to get in another line, which ended up screwing us up. Gates were supposed to open at 9, but apparently people had snuck into our section on the lawn, so the police had to clear them out. By the time they opened the gates at 10, it was pretty clear a whole lot of people weren't going to get in. I got close, actually was probably about 100 yards from where the cannons did the 21 gun salute.

Some really awful things were said to the Capitol Police. I could not get out of that crowd fast enough. I mean, you can understand why they were irate. People came from far and wide, and many paid a lot of money for those tickets. But I wasn't about to get trampled.

I managed to get Chipotle on the way home, so all was not lost.
