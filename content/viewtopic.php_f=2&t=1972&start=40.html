As a BSG newbie who's too impatient to read thru the previous LOST thread, here's my take on the season 3 finale.

What we saw in Jack's "flash-forward" is the basis for next season. Jack and Kate and the rest who were waiting to be rescued will be back in the real world, minus Locke, Ben, Alex and Rousseau, who stay behind of their own accord.

This will leave those 4 on the island, along with Desmond, Mikhail, Juliet, Sawyer, Sayid, Hurley, Bernard and Jin.

The season will be about the people on the island figuring out that they've been left behind and are, for some reason, still unable to be found. Meanwhile, back in the U.S. events leading to Jack and Kate's confrontation will unfold, including Jack learning that Claire is his half-sister, "Turniphead" is his nephew, Kate clearing her name and/or being reunited with Nathan Filion, Sun having her baby etc.

On the island, Alex will be torn between Ben and Rousseau; all the other Lost-aways will be separated from people they've grown close to SINCE the crash; and since Mikhail seemingly can't die, I think we'll see other resurrections due to the island's properties, maybe Charlie, maybe Libby, maybe Kiele Sanchez's character, maybe Shannon, maybe Boone.

And definitely Christian Shepherd, who I think is tied in with the whole Jacob mystery.
