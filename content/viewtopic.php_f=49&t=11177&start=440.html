Not sure I should post this here or in the "pity/hate" the Mets thread, but, I think I'd heard this once before, but its significance never really dawned on me:

Wikipedia wrote:There were occasional attempts to move the Dodgers back to Brooklyn. State senator Tom Bartosiewicz tried hard to persuade them in the early 1980s, but was rebuffed. A stronger chance was in 1998, when the O'Malley family sold up to Rupert Murdoch's Fox company. In the course of bidding, a committee convened by the City and State of New York (including Roger Kahn, author of Boys of Summer) made an offer to the club which was turned down, despite being larger than the eventual sale price.


There was a serious offer to buy the Dodgers from a group that wanted to move them back to Brooklyn, that had the backing of the City and State of New York?

Holy shit, they really never did get over that out there in New York, did they? Most of all, I just love what this says about how much they just LOVE the Mets: even though it had zero chance, at all, of happening, the CITY AND STATE OF NEW YORK backed an effort to bring the Dodgers back to town. This, despite the fact that the Dodgers have led all MLB in attendance - by a margin of nearly 30 million over the second-place... you guessed it... NEW YORK Yankees - since they moved out to LA in 1958. I mean, honestly, by any measure, the Dodgers' move to Los Angeles is one of the all-time business success stories in ANY business, let alone baseball. But hey, let's get together a committee to make a serious bid to buy the team and move them back to Brooklyn - it's either that, or another godforsaken season of Mets baseball!

Apparently, the whole city and state government would do anything - ANYTHING - to have a NL alternative to Mets baseball.

Maybe the Wlipons, instead of ponying up any more dough for free agents, should just save their pennies and then offer Frank McCourt $100M or more for the name "Brooklyn Dodgers." Goodness knows McCourt is going to need it, and once Vin Scully retires, who gives a shit what they call that team?
