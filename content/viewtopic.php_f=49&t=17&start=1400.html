by dsp » Thu May 17, 2007 13:50:00

I do like Fox launching a drama at 9. But Sundays are a tough night.

During the fall, every week is thrown off because CBS and Fox both get huge lead-ins from football. Then in January, the lead-ins are even bigger because of the playoffs, then you have a primetime Championship game, a week off, the Superbowl, the Grammys, a weeks off, the Oscars, later on CBS gets the Masters lead-in and 3 weeks of College Basketball. It goes on and on.

So starting new shows in January is tough, that's why NBC was smart to schedule established shows there, knowing that the core audience and others will find it.

But it's worth a shot for Fox, it's about time they paired The Simpsons and Family Guy together.
