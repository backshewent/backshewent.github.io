CalvinBall wrote:Can you briefly explain why it is so crazy?


Because teams that do not break slot are taking guys who are going to require 100% more than slot recommendations. The Padres never break slot and took a guy who wants more than $6M, and they never take prep players early. The Rockies are one of the cheapest teams in baseball and have a lot of picks in the first few rounds, so for them to take a guy who wants $7M+ is crazy. Of course, they pick 32nd, so maybe they did this just to grab an extra pick next year and they just didnt want anyone else taking him. Green was a stud over the summer and falls all the way to Oakland. Pollock was in the 30-40 talent range and ends up going 17th, Alex White slid all the way to 15, the Pirates overdrafted a guy by like 20 spots.
