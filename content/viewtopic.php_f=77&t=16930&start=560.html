zayda's is more of a philly thing, a microbrewer amongst hot condiment providers. It was started by on guy, a comedian who goes by "the pickle man" because his other flagship product is hot pickles. He used to make them himself and go around himself selling & delivering. I remember Steve coming into a bar I worked at in the early 80s with his pickles & horseradish. Fun guy.

He's got 38 products in his catalog now, so I imagine it's more than just him now. Kelchner's is fine stuff (and I do use it a lot because Zayda's isn't everywhere) and surely is hands down better than any national brand, but if you ever see it pick up some Zayda's.
