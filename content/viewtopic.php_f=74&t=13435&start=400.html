Polygamy is the generic name for a marriage which includes more than two partners. So Obama's father was a polygamist. Polygyny is when a man has multiple simultaneous wives, which would be the case for Obama's dad. Polygyny is the name for a common subset of polygamy. The other subsets of polygamy are polyandry, when a woman has multiple husbands, and group marriage, when husband(s) and wives all live together. But all three situations are commonly referred to as polygamy.
