-- The Cardinals and Reds had the best overall records in the NL divisions but neither one made the playoffs because they didn't win either half
-- The Royals were 50-53 overall and would've been 4th in the AL West, but since they won the 2nd half, they made the playoffs
-- The Cardinals and Pirates only played 102 games while the Giants played 111

The strike was due to owners wanting compensation whenever they lost a major free agent. The original plan was to have the signing team protect 12 players and the losing team could pick from the remainder, but the players argued this diminished the value of free agency. They eventually agreed to a plan where the losing team could pick from unprotected players from any team, not just the signing one. There was also an agreement where players could only become free agents after six seasons.
