I read something this weekend about the essential similarities between Obama and Romney--I think the implication was that ultimately neither has the courage of his own convictions, and that they'd likely be similar as presidents.

This strikes me as incorrect--not the premise, but the outcome. In the case of Obama, essentially a moderate to conservative Democrat, this pushes him to where Romney probably starts: toward a presidency resembling that of George H.W. Bush. In the case of Romney, I think it would make him a not personally convincing Rick Perry imitator.
