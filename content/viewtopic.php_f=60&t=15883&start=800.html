by LastTrain » Fri Oct 10, 2014 22:57:26

BigEd76 wrote:"Team option can vest" = the player has incentives/requirements that need to be met for the extra year(s) to be picked up by the team. For example, CLIFF had to pitch 400 IP this year + next year for his 2016 salary to be picked up, and since it won't, he'll be a FA after next season


This assumes the team doesn't want to pick up the option, which is likely correct in Lee's case and in most cases in which a vesting minimum is not reached. A team could always pick up a team option that has not vested into a guaranteed deal.
