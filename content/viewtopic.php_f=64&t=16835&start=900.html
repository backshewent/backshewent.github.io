Goeddel inf:
Team: Montgomery Biscuits (AA) ETA: 2016
Position: OF Age: 23 DOB: 10/20/1992
Bats: R Throws: R Height: 6' 4" Weight: 186 lb.
Drafted: 2011, 1st (41) - TB
VIDEO

Scouting grades: Hit: 50 | Power: 45 | Run: 55 | Arm: 60 | Field: 45 | Overall: 45
Goeddel's older brother, Erik, is a pitcher in the Mets organization and made his Major League debut in 2014. Tyler had his best season in the professional ranks in 2014, but still has a ways to go before he too reaches the big leagues.

Goeddel was projectable and athletic when the Rays drafted him in 2011 and he's still working to hone his raw tools. He has a loose, line drive swing that should lend itself to hitting for more power once he physically matures. He knows how to use his speed on the bases and has stolen at least 20 bases in each of his first three professional seasons.

Defensively, Goeddel began as a third baseman, but the Rays moved him to the outfield in 2015. He's seen time at all three spots, and his strong arm and speed are assets there.
