Mike in Midlothian wrote:Let's assume the Phils interest in Rendon is real and he signs here. What if the plan was to move on from Hoskins this offseason and move Bohm over to 1st and just deal with the early growing pains. If Bohm were to open the season as our everyday 1b next year, how close could he get to Hoskins production/numbers in 2020? Would he be a disaster defensively?


In 2020, he'll be, IMO, significantly worse than Hoskins offensively - 40-70 points of OBP and SLG each. Bohm hit .265 in AA last year, and hasn't seen AAA yet. But a full major league season of .250/.320/.430 wouldn't be surprising at this point. I'd probably land around .250/.360/.500 on a Hoskins projection. I don't think Bohm's 1B defense would be a disaster, but it would be a downgrade in his first year, I believe.

If the goal is to win in 2020, you want Hoskins and Rendon at the corners. Move Bohm to fill another need, or give him an OF glove in AAA.

If we fail to get Rendon, which seems likely, I think he could end up a Dodger. If he does, we could do worse than taking Turner or Muncy off their hands to alleviate their crowded infield.
