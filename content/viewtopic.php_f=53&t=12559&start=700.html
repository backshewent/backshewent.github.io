Rowand has had a crazy go nuts kind of career. The last year he was in Philly (2007) he hit 45 doubles; 27 homers; walked some and put up an .889 OPS. He was an All Star and a Golden Glove in CF and he even drew some MVP votes. That season nearly matched his superb 2004 when he hit .310; slugged .544 and OPSed .905.

In 2005 the ChiSox won it all and he got a lot of credit even though he was pretty average. In fact, besides 2004 and 2007; Rowand has been pretty mediocre. The last two seasons he's been just plain bad. He only had 11 post season at bats last season for the Giants and, in fact, he hasn't been very good in the post season.

This guy has two WFC rings and an 11 year career fueled by two superb seasons and a reputation for moxie and stuff.

There were quite a few folks who wanted to keep him in 2007. Glad we didn't.

He has a .766 career OPS as a pinch hitter including .820 and 1.115 in the last two seasons. The Phils are often impressed by stuff like that.
