If we drink we will die;

If we don't drink we will die;

So we might as well say what the hell and drink until we die;

Hey; Hey; Hey; Hey:Hey

(And when you do the Hey-Hey Hey part you also do the Cossack dance).


Anyways, Duke got us to sing that song and do the Cossack dance during the hey-hey-hey part and all that stuff and we all had a good time.

He swore he learned the song from the movie Taras Bulba.

So when I get back to the States, I notice one day that Taras Bulba is on TBS or some such channel. And I watch this whole stupid movie (Yul Brenner with hair and Tony Curtis with a hairpiece) and dammitttohell -- no Cossack song. WTFIUWT??????

So then I'm thinking the Duke might have been a little teched in the head and all that stuff.

But some time later, the Battle of the Bulge comes on TBS or one of those kinda channels and I watch it again. One of the best parts is when the young Nazi Tank guys who are all gonna die soon start singing Der Panzerleader whilst stomping their feet to inspire General Robert Shaw (who later became a shark hunter I believe).

Anyways, when the song was supposed to start, TBS or whatever channel it was cuts to commercial and there's no Panzerleader song.

I think maybe the same thing happened to the Cossack song in Taras Bulba.

So anyways, sorry Duke, for thinking you were teched in the head.
