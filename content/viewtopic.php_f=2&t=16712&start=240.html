Pulled pork/chicken is a good one. Soup I freeze the most is a pasta fagiole/minestrone - just keep the pasta out of it before freezing.

It's not exciting, but I also make ahead meatball parm sandwiches for my oldest's lunch. Wrap up the sandwich in foil before freezing, take it out the night before to thaw a little, then I cook it in the morning and throw it in a thermos.
