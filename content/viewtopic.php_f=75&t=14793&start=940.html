by TenuredVulture » Sun Jun 30, 2013 21:00:10

PrattRules wrote:How the fuck did people not kill themselves before air conditioning?



I grew up without air conditioning. My dad would respond to repeated requests for air conditioning with "There's only a few nights out of the year where it's too hot to sleep." On those nights, because my room was the warmest in the house, I'd sleep on the porch. We did have ceiling fans and an attic fan. My parents did not get air conditioning at all until the summer I got married (1995). It was an especially hot July that year, and my mother said there was no way she could get ready for the wedding in that heat. So they bought three big window units. Shortly after that, they moved to a house with central air.
