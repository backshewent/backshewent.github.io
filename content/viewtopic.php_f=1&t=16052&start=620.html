How do trades work in the NFL? I would think Foles as a trading chip would be far more valuable if he's under contract for 5 years or so. Otherwise, he's just a one year rental. by stevemc » Thu Feb 15, 2018 14:12:53

The problem in signing Foles to an extension in that scenario is the potential bonus/guaranteed $. It doesn't make sense for the Eagles to sign him, give him a bonus and then trade him in most scenarios, because they would have to eat that bonus as a cap hit. What makes more sense is to allow a prospective team to talk with Foles about an extension prior to the trade and then make the trade once the handshake deal between Foles/Team is agreed upon.
