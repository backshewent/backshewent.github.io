Had a strange 5 minutes on Fri night at happy hour. We were at a bar in Center City with coworkers, and it's a fairly young crowd there. A mid 40s black woman comes in, orders a PBR and sits by herself right by my group of coworkers. Since we came from work, I had a laptop bag with a laptop in it, as did a few other people. The lady seemed a little paranoid and sketchy, but of course, my white liberal guilt kicked in and I felt bad for even thinking that. So she finishes her beer, gets up quickly and leaves. I told the people I was talking to that she seemed sketchy and even joked about the white liberal guilt thing. Girl I'm talking to says her bag was right next to her and goes over to look through it. Yep, her purse is gone. She runs out to see if the lady is still in the vacinity, meanwhile, her boyfriend starts going through her bag to see if the purse was buried in it somewhere. There's all sorts of stuff in the bag and the guy pulls out her shoes and puts them on the bar. Bartender sees shoes on the bar and tells him to remove them. Boyfriend, already pissed about the scene, and apparently a total hothead, tells the bartender to not bother him. Bartender tells him to remove the shoes. Boyfriend tells him to fuck off. Bartender tells him he's gone. Neck tatted bouncer comes over and gets all aggro bouncer and ends up tossing him out a back door. Turns out the thief hopped in a cab to South Philly (paid with the girl's credit card) and spent $500 at Target.

So in a few minutes span, I witnessed so many deplorable human behaviors (some my own): racial profiling, liberal guilt, theft, meatheads, power tripping bartenders and bouncers, and girls who carry way too much shit in their bags.
