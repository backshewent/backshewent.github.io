by TenuredVulture » Fri May 11, 2007 22:34:38

Bakestar wrote:Moral dilemma: If you have reason to suspect that a good friend and co-worker is about to be canned, do you warn him/her?


Is there anyway this friend can use the information to his or her advantage without doing serious harm to the company? That is, can they possibly salvage their position, take advantage of another offer, or in some other way does knowing this help them out? If so, you should tell them. On the other hand, if they use this information to sabotage the company (and by extension your own livelihood) then probably not.

Also, it depends on how you came by the info--if you were just sort of looking through the bosses desk and came across a memo, well, then, that's something different.
