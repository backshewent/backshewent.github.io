Hmmm...epiphany moment!!

I used to roll my eyes about Tesla's "ludicrous mode" and their quest to make their cars REALLY REALLY FAST. I mean, the technology is about going to a sustainable energy source, not about drag racing. It actually turned me off on the company a little bit.

BUT...now it just hit me how brilliant it is. Just think...Tesla could've made a great electric car without the speed and captured only the "Green" portion of the car market but been largely ignored by everyone else. But by going ludicrous he also captures a portion of the fast car enthusiasts, thus expanding the electric car market non-organically. So car guys as well as granola-crunching tree huggers will be on the tesla bandwagon.

Nicely done, Elon.
