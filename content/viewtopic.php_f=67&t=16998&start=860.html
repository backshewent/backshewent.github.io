There is no clear path wrt QB.

Bradford everyone (almost) agrees that at a fair price and a deal that they can get out of in 2 years, would be willing to see what he can do another year removed from the knee and in Doug's offense?

RGIII presents some potential for excitement - no one knows if he can learn defenses and put in the study time and would be a new guy for a new team? I'd love to see him make Wash pay and regret it? But --

Kaepernick - no one is trading for him and he'll have to be cut and become a FA before anything happens there, that's a month away. Who would trade now knowing he's going to get cut by SF on Apr 5th deadline for roster bonus? I just don't trust it. If Chip was still here and but it looks like he doesn't even want to go w Chip who better for him? A coach who can play to all his strengths --

Chase Daniels - is a joke of an idea. I just think of all the Matt Flynns and Whitehurts and Kevin Kolbs and usually if an understudy was that talented - he'd have been traded for a good draft pick the way the Birds got a 2nd for AJ. Don't trust, don't want.

Guys have ties to Texas - that's a thing. Dal and Houston need QB

I think in an ideal world, the Birds would keep Bradford at the price they want - a price that allows them to extend Fletcher and pick up a G and S in FA -- and then find this year or next a draft pick QB to groom for a couple years and then see how it all goes from there.

I think 16mil a year is about the best Sam get's from Phila, for 4-5 years, with a way out for the team to make more or less a 2 year deal.
