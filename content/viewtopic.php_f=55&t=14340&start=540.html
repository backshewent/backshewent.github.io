by JFLNYC » Sun Jan 06, 2013 00:45:00

We can all agree that Upton had an off year last year. Yet, even in an off year he ranked 15th in WAR among MLB RF (ahead of Jay Bruce, Hunter Pence and Nelson Cruz, among others), his OPS+ was still 107, his wOBA .341. All this in an off year.

I think some Phillies fans may have been spoiled by having three elite, MVP-caliber players for so long in Howard, Rollins and Utley. That caliber player is very hard to find, especially when they're young. There's no one in the Phils' farm system who currently fits that description.

With our elite 3 no longer elite and on the downside of their careers, the number one, top priority for this team is to find one or more young, elite position players. One is now available and his name is Justin Upton. Yes, he has a question mark or two attached to him. But with the farm system being so barren in elite position player prospects and players of Upton's ability and age being so rare and difficult to acquire, Rube should move heaven and earth to get him.
