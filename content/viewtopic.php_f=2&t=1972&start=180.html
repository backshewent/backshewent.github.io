BuddyGroom wrote:Pause the new promo at 1:03. It looks like Jack's father sitting in the "Jacob" chair.


That's definitely Jack's Dad. Whether it's the Jacob chair - not sure.


If Jack's father, Christian Shepherd, is Jacob, though, it indicates that whether this was the plan from the start, the writers might not just be going around in circles. There's been a lot of mystery around Jack's father from the beginning, even though he's not a main character.

If he somehow is Jacob and somehow drew all these people to the island, including the son who feels responsible for the father's death and the illegitimate daughter, who now has had an illegitimate son ...

Well, I don't pretend to understand all of it, but I always thought the name Christian Shepherd had to have some meaning. Now, if he some has led all these people to this island, like a shepherd, and the island has something to do with redemption, as Christ did ...

I've always looked at this show more in terms of the characters and what they mean and how they relate (in a variety of meanings) to one another, moreso than the science that may be behind at least some of the answers to what is going on in this show. And I think who/what Christian Shepherd is/represents is a, maybe "the", key element to unlocking the whole thing.
