With all the sads about our division opponents getting top picks, I just thought I would point out the following:

2010 - Hall (1,1)
2011 - Nugent-Hopkins (1,1)
2012 - Yakopov (1,1)
2013 - Nurse (1,7)
2014 - Draisaitl (1,3)
2015 - McDavid (1,1)
2016 - Puljujarvi (1.4)
2017 - Yamamoto (1,22)
2018 - Bouchard (1,10)

So, with a median draft pick at #3 over the past nine seasons, the Edmonton Oilers have made the playoffs ONCE with their median season equaling 74 points (2012-2013 season calculated pro-rata due to the lockout).
