heyeaglefn wrote:If I was a Carolina fan I would be pissed at Williams. To not be able to pound Nova inside really was terrible, they had so many mismatches and a good entry pass would have led to an easy basket or a foul. Give Nova credit for what they did in fronting the big guys but for Williams to not adjust to that really was amazing.


Jay did something really smart with his defensive strategy. They didn't just front the post, the pressured the ball hard as well making the entry passes really difficult. He managed to get his kids not to let up on the fronting (which isn't easy at all--the kids get lazy and they get beat up), and not to get physically worn down (although Nova lost the last 5 minutes of both halves pretty badly). He also adjusted the switching on the inside so that defenders were flashing in front of post players from the weak side.

I think Roy didn't adjust because he was beating the strategy by shooting threes and shooting them well. There really was no need to adjust since their 3s were going in. UNC was more focused on figuring out how to defend Nova rather than improving their offensive execution.
