by EndlessSummer » Wed Mar 05, 2014 16:26:43

Radiolab has had some super interesting shows recently. Loved these two in particular:

"Brown Box" is about a reporter who worked undercover in an Amazon.com-esque 17 million square foot warehouse. It's pretty staggering to hear how the operation works:
http://www.radiolab.org/story/brown-box/

"Cut and Run" explores why the Kalenjin tribe in Kenya has produced so many world class runners. It goes through the physics of their body types and societal pressures to run, but ends up talking about the insane ritual they have for males at age 13 that makes them seemingly impervious to pain. It'll make you a little queasy if you're prone to that.
http://www.radiolab.org/story/runners/
