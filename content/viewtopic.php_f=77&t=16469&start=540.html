As a side note, it's totally OK to commiserate about stuff like that here. But a common social foe paw I see is people saying to someone who is having / is a guest of honor at a party "oh, I have so many wedding (birthday parties, etc.) this month". Their wedding/party is a lot of work and is very special to them. Making it seem like "just another entry on your social calendar" is really boorish and downplays the significance of their particular event. So, when talking to the guest(s) of honor, it's best to keep the riches of your calendar to yourself.
