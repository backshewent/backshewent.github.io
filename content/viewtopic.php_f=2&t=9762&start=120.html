by momadance » Mon Aug 02, 2010 00:01:27

phatj wrote:I like this a lot. How'd you get the sky like that? Red separation, or a filter, or ?


Here's the original sky:



I ran it through one of my B&W Filters for lightroom. Then I cooled the colors a bit with split toning. In the B&W color mix The greens and aquas are set pretty high. Reds and oranges not so much around +20 or so. Blue's are set to -100. What really got the sky like that was pulling the blue out of the color mix and re-doing the blue with the split toning to get it to a cooler tint. I have two presets that got it done. One for the B&W color mix and another for cooling of the tint. I can send you over the preset files for lightroom if you want them...
