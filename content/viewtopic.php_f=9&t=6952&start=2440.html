by kruker » Sun Nov 02, 2008 18:28:50

I spent the last week down in Philly without the internet, so I didn't get a chance to tell my game 5.1 story. I was at a party in South Philly for the game and as soon as the game went to rain delay, my friend and I ran across Broad Street and sneaked into CBP. The talk was that the game was going to be played, so I was convinced that I was going to see the last out of the WS.

Figuring we shouldn't even be in the stadium, we went up to the suites and knocked on doors until two guys let us in their company suite and told us to eat and drink whatever we wanted. After the game was suspended, we walked down past visitor services to see how many people had lost their tickets during the game. We spent the next 2 hours in the rain while avoiding security going foul pole to foul pole looking for tickets. No such luck, for us at least. Afterwards we went to Benny the Bums for a beer only to meet a guy who a minute before had been given 2 tickets by a gentleman who had to leave town the next day and could not attend the continuation of the game. So that's as close as I got to seeing the final outs of game 5. I ended up spending Wednesday night at Chickies, which was a pretty amazing time in and of itself.

After partying all night Wednesday into Thursday and the mayhem of Friday, I'm just now starting to come down from cloud 9.
