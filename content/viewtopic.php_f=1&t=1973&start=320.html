by Shore » Sun Jul 01, 2007 13:12:40

Played at the Borgata last night - they rape you, at least on holiday weekend Saturdays, as the $100 NL SnG had a $20 vig. 10 players tourney, pays top 2 (700/300). The play was crazy early - at least 5 people went all-in in the first 10-12 hands. 3K chips to start, 15 minute betting rounds, but the jumps were big... 2 hours in, we were at 1200/2400 with a 400 ante, and 3 of us left, 1 big stack at about 18K, me at 7K, third at 5K. I knocked out the 5K, so it was ~18K to ~12K, and the big stack offered me a chop, and I took it.

I was ultra-conservative; I may have played 2 hands in the first 40. I folded hands I theoretically could/should have played (AK in the small blind), due to all-ins before me.

It was a blast, but I guess at least half of that is because I won.... Hope to go down again soon.
