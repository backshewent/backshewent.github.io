"supper" is actually a smaller, lunch-like meal eaten in the evening when you've had your main meal ("dinner") in the early afternoon. In the early 20th century, Sunday would be the day for having "dinner" (main meal) at lunchtime, and then your evening, smaller meal would be "supper".
