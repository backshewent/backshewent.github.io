Because egg is on Lurie's face around Chip, and things got crazy there for a bit, to put Chip and Howie out there - as if they have some obligation to explain themselves to the otuside world, which they do not - or rehash what happened with the end of Andy and the rise and fall of Chipper - isn't anything they plan to expose.

Lurie will be dignified. The media don't date push Jeff too far for fear of many consequences - and Jeff never has acted like a d-head - he's always dignified. He will say a bunch of stuff that picks up where he left off - and he will introduce Doug - talk about how he's known him for years - knows how talented he is, and how he can work well with the org and excited abotu the talent he inherits. Blah blah.

There will be 1-2-3 questions attempted about Howie or more broadly, "who is running personnel" Jeff will say there is a braintrust that confer, Donahoe, Howie, and everyone reports and recommends to him - and Doug is a voice in the mix, and maybe they are looking for another exec blahblah.

He can't simply say "Howie is back in his old role" because it brings up the "when did Howie not be in his old role and why, and what now?" And they aren't going to go there. Eskin will try and a few others to get something around this - and Lurie will get uncomfortable and push it aside and move on.

We'll get very very little actual information other than what we know. Doug is in, his coaches are such and such They are evaluating the entire roster and looking at schemes and contracts and injury blah blah --
