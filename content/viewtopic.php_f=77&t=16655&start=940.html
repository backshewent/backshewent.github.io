The two biggest winners coming out of the debate last night were Fiorina and Trump, not necessarily in terms of content or performance, but in terms of positioning.

Fiorina is phenomenal in these settings and got her moment when Tapper lobbed up the softball to her about Trump calling her ugly. I thought her first hour was incredible but then she kind of tailed off after Christie told her to STFU. She will absolutely get a bump.

Trump came out well too because he made no major mistakes. Everyone was attacking him and the format was set up for everyone to take him on. He toned it down a bit but did not back down at all and dominated the time on screen. He's an alpha male who is given a major forum to act like an alpha male which he always does. 18+ minutes of talk time is insane with 11 candidates on stage. Fiorina is probably the only candidate who will get a real bump out of the debate, and people moving over to her certainly won't be those who are supporting Trump. Trump played well to his base. If anything, he could pick up more Cruz voters. I thought Cruz was awful.

The actual politicians were all par for the course. Maybe Rubio helped himself a bit with his foreign policy talk. Most

The guy who was hurt the most IMO was Carson who I think will lose support to Fiorina.
