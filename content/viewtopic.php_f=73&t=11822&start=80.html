I just want to let everyone know that the purpose of this thread was just to have a little fun. I'm not trying to offend anyone. If that happens I'm just going to stop doing it because it's supposed to be a fun thing and nothing more.

I started doing this in response to the off-season Hall of Fame hi-jinx. This stuff is all supposed to be fun things to do whilst waiting for the off-season to end.

I began by doing a lot of folks who I think I have a fairly clear idea about as far as their personality has developed on the board (which isn't necessarily how they are in real life).

Most of those guys have received some support in the H of Fame thing and were guys I voted for or considered voting for or something like that. So I, for the most part, used H of Fame players as comps.

The player I associate with a poster is not some kind of judgment about the worth of a person in real life or anything like that. I'm just having some fun with pals and I'm hoping everyone else is too. If not, I'll just stop.
