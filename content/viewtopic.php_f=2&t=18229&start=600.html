by swishnicholson » Wed Oct 18, 2017 21:01:22

If this isn't the place to confess that I am some combination of the stupidest and luckiest sumbitch alive where is?

I coach cross country, and this afternoon I was using a leaf blower on the course to prepare it for a middle school meet. When I went to get something out of my car afterward, I noticed that my keys were gone. Almost certainly they had fallen out of my pocket somewhere along the course when I was restarting the blower. This was highly annoying but not devastating. There wasn't anything I really needed from my car right then, I live less than a half mile away, the keys would probably eventually turn up, and I had a spare set back home. True, I hadn't seen the spare set in a few days, but I was sure they were hanging around in the pocket of a pair of shorts I hadn't worn since the weather changed or under something on my dresser. At the very worst I'd be missing the set that had a few store cards on the chain for a while.

I took a look but couldn't find them, and I also had the runners keep an eye out (since the high schoolers were also helping out). Then I got back to helping out at the meet myself. About a half an hour in, one of the runners brought me a set of keys and asked if they were mine. I definitely recognized them, but , weirdly, none of the store cards were attached to the chain. They definitely opened my car, though. Then, a half hour later, someone else said they had found a set of keys. These were the ones I had definitely dropped, with all the cards attached.

The first set was my backup, which I had managed to lose a couple days ago. I must have had both sets of keys on me that day since I didn't even notice. The second was my main set. For an hour or so I had been totally screwed and didn't even know it. I don't know whether that means I should play the lottery or shut myself up away from the world, since clearly I'm not qualified to cope.
