Just a few notes on the Phillies in post-season play as we get ready to head into the 2011 post-season:

1) The Phillies have played 98 post-season games in their history; game two of the NLDS will be their 100th.

2) The Phillies all-time post-season record is 47-51.

3) More than half of the franchise's post-season games have taken place since 1993.

4) More than half of the franchise's post-season wins have occurred since 2008.

5) The Phillies have never played in a game-7 in a post-season series. They have played in a game 5 in a best-of-five series twice, beating Houston in the NLCS in 1980, losing to Montreal in the division series in 1981.
