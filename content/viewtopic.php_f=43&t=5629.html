by Trent Steele » Mon Jun 02, 2008 15:39:55

Phillies are 15-7 when Jenkins doesn't start and 8-1 when he doesn't play at all.

Excluding the rain delay game where he pitched one inning, the Phils have won Kendrick's last 7 starts. Kendrick has a 4.98 ERA in those starts.

Adam Eaton has a 4.99 ERA. Excluding the first 4 innings of his 1st start last year, at no point in 2007 did Eaton's ERA go below 5.

Eaton had 8 QS last year. He has 6 QS already. Of course, he had none in his last 13 starts last year (unreal), so past performance is not indicative of future results.

Chad Durbin's percentage of infield flies per fly ball is 23.5%, which is nuts, leads the league of anyone with more than 20 IP, and, one would think, is completely flukish.

One thing that definitely is flukish is Durbin's 3.5 HR/FB rate. Last year, in Comerica Park, it was 13.9%. I believe there is a regression in order, good sir.

Chris Coste has a 27.4% LD rate. Carlos Ruiz has a 14.4% LD rate.

Only Arizona hits fewer ground balls than the Phils, which is good thing.

WPA

Hitters: 3.74
Starters: -3.17
Bullpen: 3.43
Total: 4.00

Phillies' OF RZR is the worst in the NL, but have made the 2nd most out of zone plays. Not sure what the deal is there.

The Mets IF defense is terrible - worst RZR and fewest OOZ plays.
The Mets OF defense is awesome - best RZR and most OOZ plays.

Remember when the Phils set the ML record for SB% last year with 88%? This year they are at 87%. God bless Davy Lopes.
