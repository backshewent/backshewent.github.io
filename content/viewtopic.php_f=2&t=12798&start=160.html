by TenuredVulture » Wed Sep 12, 2012 14:33:10

While it seems likely that the kind of calorie matters, the amount of calories matter as well. And it's not really carbs per se, it's sugars and white flour (and potatoes and white rice) that are not good things to eat if you're trying to lose weight. And fruit juice is bad too, though fruit itself is okay, and better than a lot of other things you might snack on. And a lot of "whole grain" is really not good either. But if you think you can eat carb free crisco and hotdogs without the bun, well, that's probably not a great plan.

Also, consider "hidden" carbs--ketchup is pretty much 1/4 sugar (or HFC) honey mustard and relish probably have sweeteners added to them.
