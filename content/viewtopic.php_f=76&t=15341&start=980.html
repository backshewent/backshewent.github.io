The Dude wrote:Bucky, have you ever given piano lessons?


tl;dr answer = 'no'.

I've probably done my share of music teaching in the day, but never "lessons". I find myself in a very weird spot in the musical community- there's usually the people who have studied and been taught and taken lessons their whole lives and become proficient and/or incredible and those who are totally self-taught and become proficient and/or awesome. I straddle the middle ground, but lean towards the latter, so I have too many unorthodox methods and stylings to ever consider giving lessons. To be truthful and with a rare display of self-deprication and lack of modesty, I wish I had a LOT more technical training because my self-taught hands are well behind a lot of others- both taught and naturally gifted- but my self-taught ears and musical instincts are well above most.
