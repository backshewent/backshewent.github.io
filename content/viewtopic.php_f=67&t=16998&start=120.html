As for how much to pay a player or how to structure a deal, I don't have a lot to say there, out of my expertise.

I do think Ertz is not yet in the strata of best TEs - but he could be. Yes, he has left something on the field it seems most games even his good ones but he showed something in those last two we hadn't seen before that is very encouraging. This was a solid signing, he's a solid young core player for the future. Doesn't really matter who the HC or OC are - Ertz would fit most passing-minded coaches with a modern view of TEs these days.

Banner is a prick. Why sour the kid when he's feeling good?

I think Demarco Murray, Ertz and things being more settled down post-Chip will go a long way toward Sam "wanting" to remain with the Birds all things being equal. It will still come down to money, guaranteed money and length of deal.

Celek. Not sure. Pederson knows him. Chip loved Celek and Demeco. Demeco has had bad injuries and is maybe a little older not sure, but Celek is around 30, hardly 35. And he's still solid. I'm guessing Vinny Curry is next. And Lane Johnson.

The salary cap is set to go up significantly. It's projected to be between $150 and $153.4 million for the 2016 season which would be a $7-10m jump from 2015.
