Why would he be kidding?

Don't get me wrong, he's an idiot for saying that publicly, but that hit absolutely changed the game. The other players said the same afterwards.

1. You get a great trade off of Rinaldo for Letang out of the game
2. You take what started as another leisurely Sunday skate with no emotion and turned it into a war where the Pens were off their game
3. Finally, for the first time all year, the Flyers played with passion and emotion and actually acted like they wanted to be on the ice.

You cannot play a contact sport soft and without passion or emotion. I don't care how much the rules have changed. If a level of violence is part of the sport, you will be swallowed up if you play soft. They have been complete garbage all year and one of the easiest teams to play against in the league because they have played a soft, passive brand of hockey in the name of "discipline." It's a joke and it never, ever works unless you have all-star level talent up and down your lineup like Detroit has had in previous years.

I applaud Rinaldo and only wish it was Crosby that he had hit. Couldn't care less if he gets suspended (probably will help since he sucks). He finally made a contribution on the ice and did what he is supposed to do.......get his team going.

If the Flyers brought that effort and level of aggression (and I'm not talking about fights) every night they wouldn't be the mess they are right now. And make no mistake about it, a ton of that falls on the coach.
