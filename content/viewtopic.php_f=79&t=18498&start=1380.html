by Rockinghorse » Mon Feb 05, 2018 09:38:59

Was amazing being on Broad Street (Blue Duck) the whole night. Late 4th quarter tons of cops and a few fans were peering in the windows to watch the final minutes.

We spilled out onto Broad when it was still kind of empty. Surreal to watch the crowds roll in like waves from both sides of Locust, then Spruce, until it all filled up. I actually used to daydream that exact scenario when I lived in Texas and one of our teams looked like they had a shot. I even flew my wife up and got to Broad Street just as Bud Selig was giving his rule book pc about the rainout.

Last night my wife thought my 92-yo mother wanted to come out to Broad so we went up to her apt. She was still awake but in PJs and no interest in joining that madness. So I grabbed a few beers from my stash in her fridge, initially careful about drinking openly until I saw every other person holding a bottle or can. I gave my last one to a girl doing the cheer against a pole, who thanked me and said "I'm soooo fucking tired."
