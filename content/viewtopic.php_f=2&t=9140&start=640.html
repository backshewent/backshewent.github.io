The annoying voice. Hard to characterize other than it's usually a woman with a high pitch, a piercing sound, and lack of volume control.

On a flight, had two nasally midwestern housewives yabbering across the aisle inanely for the full duration. That episode prompted me to drop $250 on some Bose noise-cancelling headphones.

At Dubu in Elkins Park, the Koreans chew loudly, but it was a table full of young excited Merkins who proved the most annoying - just one whiny B with a shrill piercing sound and no awareness of how everyone in the damn place could hear her. Someone needed to tell her to use her inside voice.
