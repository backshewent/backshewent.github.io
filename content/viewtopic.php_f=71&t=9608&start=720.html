I know I've told the story before, but when I was working as a garbage man at the beach, the recycling would usually end up 10% trash, and if it was buried in the can, we weren't making enough money to go sorting through it. Into the garbage truck it went.

Throwing recycling in a garbage can isn't really a big deal since it's only the one can/bottle that you aren't recycling. Throwing trash in a recycling bucket is really lazy and screws up a greater amount of refuse.
