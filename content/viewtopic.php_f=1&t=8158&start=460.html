by Fusilli Jerry » Sat Apr 18, 2009 16:25:25

Just returned home. The Phillies did a phenomenal job putting this together. It was a beautiful tribute to Harry.

Kane's speech was touching- I am amazed at how composed he remained. Also, a nice poem read by Rich Ashburn, Jr. (and written by his wife).

The sendoff, passing Harry's casket down the line of current and former members of his "traveling party", was incredibly moving. Those around me who had yet to cry started tearing up at that sight, and those who had already been crying began to cry even harder.

The only thing that was dismaying was the size of the crowd. Understanding that many people had already come and gone, I thought for sure that the turnout at the actual ceremony would have been far greater.
