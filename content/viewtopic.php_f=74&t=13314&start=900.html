I lived in Charlotte for a summer back in 02. You can get a huge house for not a ton of money. Traffic's really bad on race weekends. Decent amount of culture, food scene is just alright. Will be hard to get decent pizza. Good BBQ though. Typical suburban strip mall layout. Lot of money from the banking sector when it was doing well. People are generally pretty friendly. Lots of transplants and not a lot of people native to the area, so you'd fit right in.

My parents live in Richmond and I live in NYC. I see them maybe 2-3 times a year now, same with the in-laws. If they're used to seeing you a lot, it may cause issues if they end up seeing their grandkids less than they want to or are used to.
