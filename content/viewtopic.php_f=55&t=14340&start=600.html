by bleh » Wed Jan 09, 2013 17:14:04

1 wrote:so are the odds set up that it would be impossible to win in that scenario? i don't see how that's possible as long as a team is worse than 30-1.

Not impossible just very unlikely. And that bet wouldn't make sense or be beneficial at all. It's set up to only make money if underdogs win, so you might as well put money on just the underdogs. And the way to "minimize your downside" is to just not bet as much money.

The slightly sensible thing would be to figure out what you think the odds of each team winning are on your own, then compare it to the odds they give and pick whatever teams you think have (significantly) better odds than they are giving. But since they are Vegas and you are not you are unlikely to pick more accurate odds than them.
