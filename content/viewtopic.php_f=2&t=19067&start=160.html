It's not just people not answering the phone. They show refusals to participate or wrong numbers as no answers too.

NYT is polling off a voter list and I think asking for exact person, which means they'd call you and ask to speak to you. If they called your house landline and your wife picked up but they were trying to talk to you and you weren't available, that'd go down as a no answer.

The benefit of exact person is you can use everything off the voter file - most importantly vote history & party registration (in party reg states). The downside is it takes a lot more time and costs more because response rates are necessarily lower.
