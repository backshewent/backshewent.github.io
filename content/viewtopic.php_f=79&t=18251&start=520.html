by Philly the Kid » Sun Sep 17, 2017 17:10:39

Tough loss.

I think Eagles fans just have no perspective. The team that Chip left us with - was really bad. Especially on defense. You can only do so much in one-two off-seasons. They got their QB, rebuilding the D, got some WRs. Next year they will tweak Oline, add RB, Sid Jones and Darby will look good, and probably add a better LB too.

The Eagles are a good team, they play hard - but they are not an elite team - not yet. They cannot afford to make mistakes and not play really really precise against good teams with explosive weapons. KC Dline was as menacing as our own especially late. Two turnovers deep in our territory that lead to 10 points and a missed 29 yard fg, were too much to overcome, in a game that close. On the road.

Watching Wentz, I see lots of great characteristics. I have no regrets that they did what they did to get him. But he's not perfect yet either - and he still doesn't have a set of elite weapons. Give him Shady and Julio or Dez and Zeke and it's a big difference.

Encouraging:

-- Ertz, Alshon, Torrey and Mack - all showed some stuff and Ags got a TD.
-- 4 sacks
-- rookie kicker despite missing easy one, made 2 others
-- despite losing Watkins and Mcleaond, McDougle and Rasul held their own
-- Hicks is a beast

Discouraging:

-- one run over 5 yards other than Sproles or Wentz
-- over-pursued and Andy baited them using Hunt and Kelce effectively late
-- turnovers were bad luck, a bonk and an unusual on Sproles who had a good grip on the ball - but Eagles can't afford to lose TO-differential against good teams on road though
-- D got gassed late
-- ST not adding value

Good news?

If we're going to lose - lose to AFC on the road

Should be able to match up better w Giants who also lack a running game and don't have Hunt
