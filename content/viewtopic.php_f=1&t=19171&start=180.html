I think it is important to point out that most of the Flyers poor special teams rankings are the result of a horrendous October and November. Since Dec 1, the Flyers are killing penalties at an 82% clip; good for 14th (17th overall since Gordon took over). You'd have to parse a bit more with PP success, but over the past month, the boys' PP% ranks 21st. Again, not great, but there has been improvement in both areas as the season has gone on and particularly since the coaching change.

I will disagree just a bit with rolex in that Provorov has looked putrid nearly the entire season. He seems to take as much as he gives. Last night is a great example. He had two assists but turned the puck over multiple times, including one that led to an odd-man rush that Hart bailed them out of. I still think he is going to be a star but he needs to calm down and just play his game. It appears he is in his head at times.
