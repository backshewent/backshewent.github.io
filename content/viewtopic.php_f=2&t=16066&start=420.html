by TomatoPie » Wed Mar 08, 2017 10:26:36

Old Forge pizza is worth the trip (near Scranton). Some places like the acclaimed Revello's use a pre-made shell, but rival Arcaro & Genell is a real step up. The true magic happens at Elio G's, the take-out only place where pies are made by the grandson of Nonna Ghigiarelli, the woman who invented Old Forge style in the 1920s to feed poker players in her husband's bar.

The red pies look sickly due to the ghost white processed cheese on them, but it somehow all comes together.
