Playoff expansion plan being discussed:

-- Best team in league gets a bye to the Divisional Series
-- Other two division champs and the top 2nd place team will be seeds #2 through #4
-- Three more teams make the playoffs as part of a wild card pool
-- During a Sunday end-of-season "selection show", the #2 seed would get first choice of who they want to play from the wild card pool, then the #3 seed would choose next, leaving the top wild card (#4) to play whatever team remains
-- Wild Card round would be best-of-3, with the higher team hosting all 3 games (so the wild card pool teams would never host a playoff game unless they reach the DS)

So using last year's NL as an example:

#1 Dodgers get a bye to the NLDS
#2 Braves would host a best-of-3 against their choice of the Brewers, Mets or Diamondbacks waiting in the wild card pool
#3 Cardinals would host a best-of-3 against their choice of the remaining two teams in the wild card pool
#4 Nationals would host a best-of-3 against the last remaining team
