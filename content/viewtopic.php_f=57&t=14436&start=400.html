NOT GOOD: You know that part in the trailer for 42 where Jackie Robinson gets beaned in the face? That happened against the Phillies. He was proudly wearing the Phils uni. And by casting the nice-guy Alan Tudyk (Dodgeball, Tucker And Dale Vs. Evil, Suburgatory, Firefly) as Chapman, it somehow looks even worse.
