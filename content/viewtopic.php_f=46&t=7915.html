More importantly, America (Fuck Yeah!) starts tomorrow, playing Canada (America North) at the Rogers Centre in Toronto. 2 PM, ESPN. As for the WBC, I love international competitions and have taped the games so far, but I doubt I'll watch much of them. I just can't get into watching taped games, especialyl once I know the result (unless I have a vested interest in an involved team, which I don't).

The changes to how the groups are handled -- with double elimination -- is a massive improvement over the last edition. I think the tournament will go well.
