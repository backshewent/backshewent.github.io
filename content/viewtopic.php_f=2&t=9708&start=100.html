Hmm, never responded to (or even saw) this thread before, so I might as well contribute.

Instead of listing my 10 favorite Beatles songs, I'll list my favorite Beatles song from each of their albums (their original British albums, the ones now on CD, rather than the bastardized American versions):

Please Please Me: "Please Please Me", followed closely by "Anna (Go to Him)"
With the Beatles: "It Won't Be Long"
A Hard Day's Night: "If I Fell"
Beatles for Sale: "I'll Follow the Sun"
Help!: "You're Going to Lose That Girl"
Rubber Soul: "If I Needed Someone", followed closely by "Run for Your Life"
Revolver: "Tomorrow Never Knows" followed closely by "She Said She Said"
Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band: honestly, none (if I had to choose, I think I'd pick "A Day in the Life")
Magical Mystery Tour: "Your Mother Should Know"
White Album: "Dear Prudence"
Yellow Submarine: "Hey Bulldog"
Abbey Road: "I Want You (She's So Heavy)" followed closely by the "Golden Slumbers" medley
Let It Be: "One After 909"
Past Masters Volume 1 (i.e. singles/EPs from the Please Please Me to Help! era): "I Feel Fine" followed closely by "She Loves You"
Past Masters Volume 2 (i.e. singles from Rubber Soul to Let It Be era): "Day Tripper" followed closely by "Rain"
