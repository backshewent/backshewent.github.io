Continuing my "they're playing chicken" theory, if the Sixers are bluffing they're going to pick Embiid if Cleveland trades down, then Cleveland's now trying to say "That's fine, we'll just pick Exum!" They know Exum isn't flying to Cleveland tonight, and that's why Exum's camp wanted assurance that they aren't being used as a pawn. But it's just another piece to make the Sixers blink.


How does it make the Sixers blink in a hypothetical where Sixers would take Exum over Embiid at 3 anyway

Also how could the information that Cavs will take Exum at 4 sway their decision to take Embiid at 3 (now assuming here that they prefer Embiid). They have to assume that Exum would be gone anyway, even if Sixers try to pick up another pick in back end of top 10

I think the Cavs want Embiid. They don't want to pick him at 1, because quite frankly, they don't have to, they can trade back and get something, anything from somebody and still get Embiid.

They know Milwaukee isn't going to pick him.

Philly's telling them "Do the trade with us, or we're going to pick Embiid." Whether they actually would pick Embiid or not, who knows, but Hinkie's telling Cleveland that's what he'll do. I don't think Cleveland wants Exum (or anybody in the next tier), and Hinkie doesn't think so either.

Every offer that's been leaked (and everything is leaked for a reason) has seemed designed to get Philly to improve their offer. Everytime one of these trades has been leaked, Philly's come back and leaked something else. Right after that Afflalo rumor was leaked, all of a sudden, here comes Dei Lynam to inform us that Philly does in fact have Embiid's medicals. So the response to that is Cleveland saying "That's fine, we don't need Embiid, we'll just pick Dante Exum!"
