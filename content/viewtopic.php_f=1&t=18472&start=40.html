by Philly the Kid » Tue Jan 09, 2018 20:16:14

it's a unique situation. the only time in recent memory the birds went in to a playoff game with a backup was Jeff Garcia - and he was a guy with a pedigree and who could move.

nick foles is what he is. he's tall and he can at times pass the ball reasonably well. but he's immobile and the only QBs that don't use their legs much and succeed are gun-slingers. quick releases, good at stepping up in pocket fearless -

the giant game w 4tds was impressive. the raider game and 1-2 drives dal - not so much.

we don't know what they've devised with all this time to think about it? he's gotten more reps. in theory guys are more rested and healthy. i expect tremendous pressure on nicky and a lot of blitzes and he will have to demonstrate he can read it and handle it - and burn them to make it stop. can we run? maybe. not at first when they bring 8 in the box and say - go ahead and try.

this requires a really good opening game plan that keeps the d off balance and doing some things they haven't seen on tape before.

individual guys have to make plays. ajayi breaks a tackle. alshon snags a tough ball.

the rest has been stated - d has to play well, low penalties, no turnovers and no st gaffes.

we beat Atl last year btw. And they are on a roll but as others have noted - they are not a top to bottom powerhouse and not as good overall as a team as last year. we can end their season in cold philly if we're ready to play and nicky just does enough.

we can eval the following week - depending on what happened in the other game and who is coming in? Nola is scary as that offense is hard to stop. I liked Carson to win a shootout w Brees - but our D would have to really step up. Carolina did a great job killing Ingram and Kamara. But Brees just went off with 4 TD passes and his accuracy is still phenomenal - Minn D is really playing well and so while Keenum Latavius and the gang aren't the 2nd coming of 98 Vikings - they can score.

we have won a lot of our games this year with Carson and offense. Nick will have to do some things. Some dream of a game manager and ball control like it's the 70's and we just rush with Csonka or Riggins all day is not gonna fly. Have to be able to convert 3rd downs, have to be able to effectively pass, play action and beat the blitz.

same for following two games if we get there --

Atlanta is riding high w confidence but sports and NFL is a funny thing - and you can kill a teams sense of themselves if you have success early. execution baby. I know Doug will have a starting game plan and they need to score a TD early first or 2nd drive. And then hopefully D can force a turnover - get another quick score. Things shift if we're up 10 or 14 to 0 by end of 1st quarter. I don't like our chances playing with a deficit in the middle to late 3rd quarter. down 20-10 with 4minutes int he 3rd is not game over - but not a good position to be in.
