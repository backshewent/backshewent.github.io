There is a wonderful anecdote about asparagus and Babe Ruth. In the 20’s Ruth was the lion of the New York sports world and received an invitation to dine at the home of Mrs. August Belmont. Before he went to the dinner Ruth was lectured by Yankee owner Jake Rupert to be on his best behavior as a representative of the Yankees and professional baseball. Ruth promised to be so. He was seated next to Mrs. Belmont at the table and when the butler brought in the first course, asparagus, Mrs. Belmont asked him ” Won’t you have some asparagus, Mr. Ruth?”
“No thank you, maam,” said the Bambino, “asparagus makes my urine smell!”


Three are a lot of variations of this story. Probably the least believable part of all of them is having Ruth say "urine."
