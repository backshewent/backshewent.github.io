I think you're off the mark on all counts here.

1) They do have some assets. Holiday is an all-star guard locked up long-term to a fantastic contract. Thad has played into his contract. They're going to have lots of cap space after this season. This is not a dire situation at all.

2) I think you're underestimating our fan base. Our attendance was middle of the road this year, 17th in the league. We're the 4th largest television market in the country. There's no way they'd want to move to Seattle.

3) I think Hinkie will have plenty of rope. He's the type of GM this ownership has wanted from day 1. Besides, the old boy network is evaporating quickly. Advanced analytics is where this league is heading long term. And I don't think he'll need the rope. I think this team will be successful under him. I'm not sure he'll win a championship, but I think he'll get us out of the 7th-10th range of the East.
