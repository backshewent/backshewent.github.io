it was 0-0 after 5 innings. we scored one in the top of the 6th to take the lead into the bottom of the 6th. they scored one in the bottom of the 6th to tie it up. we had runners on in all 3 extra innings included bases loaded nobody out one time, but couldn't put anyone across the plate. our one kid saved the game when they had 2nd and third 2 outs and they hit a liner right at him at the pitching position and he nabbed it out of the air. fairly certain that was our first out by a caught ball the entire season.

they had 2nd and third 2 outs in the bottom of the 9th (had been raining since the 5th) and hit a slow dribbler at the pitcher. he stopped it and bobbled, made the throw wide to first base, game over.

tons of fun. i think 3 kids knew what was going on. my daughter managed a foul tip first time this season i think.
