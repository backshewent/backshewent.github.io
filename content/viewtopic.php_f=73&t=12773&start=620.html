That's because the circuit being open/closed is physically handled within the jack itself. When a plug is in the jack (even if just an extension cable sans phones), the headphone circuit is complete and the speaker circuit is open. Vice versa when the plug is out. It's essentially a physical switch.

This illustrates how the plug pushes up the sound contact and seperates it from the speaker contact so the contact is with the plug and not the speaker...
