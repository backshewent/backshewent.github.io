One big factor is the length of the hospital stay. For vaginal delivery, you're basically there two days. So, really, not much time for anyone visit. C-Section is longer, so that changes the dynamic some. But remember, new parents are pretty tired, and if it was a C-Section, recovery can be longer. So, I'd wait at least a week until after the baby comes home before asking to see it. And bring a present and food.
