Re: bitchin' about wawa, starring random thoughts

by Bill McNeal » Thu May 12, 2011 12:06:16

Sheetz is pretty awesome, when I lived in the Lehigh Valley I used to go to the one in Bethlehem if I was up that way, but that wasn't very frequently. Turkey Hill in the LV is also solid, not in the same league as Sheetz or WaWa, but better than the generic quick stop type joints, I like the crushed ice they have for fountain soda. Quick Check in NJ is pretty solid as well. They have (had?) a buffalo chicken bacon ranch sub that is pretty much awesome, also self-checkout lanes which is half annoying and half awesome, plus their iced coffee is actually really iced coffee, not coffee flavored syrup that WaWa sells. I'm a pretty big fan of Royal Farms down here in DE and points south, the coffee is better than WaWa, and they have a store brand line of energy drinks that are pretty good and only $1.50/can, but their sandwiches suck.

I would rank it like this

WaWa
Quick Check
Sheetz
Royal Farms

Turkey Hill
7-11
