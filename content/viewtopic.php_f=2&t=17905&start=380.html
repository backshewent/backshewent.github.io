I mentioned on here way back that I was diagnosed with Rheumatoid Arthritis. I had taken Humira, Embril and then now Actemra which has worked great and I generally feel fine and fully healthy. Well recently I have started having some insane panic attacks. Never had one before or even knew what it was really, seriously thought I was having a heart attack the first time. I ended up in the emergency room for 5 days including two days in ICU because my heart rate was exceeding like 200 bpm. I am on metropolol now and that corrected the racing heart that made no sense. The panic attacks have continuously happened.

Now I am told that they believe I may have had Lymes disease the past five years which could have led to the arthritis and that one of the final stages of Lymes is the tingling sensations I get in my joints and the panic and anxiety. Just wonderful. Just thought I'd share because like I said I feel fine and healthy and at the drop of a hat I just go into a panic. I am pretty upset at the thought of it being undiagnosed Lymes for five freaking years.

I would like to get back to being active and living MY life again.
