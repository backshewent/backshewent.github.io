I received my free copies of my book from my publisher today and I must say I think it looks great. The long, long wait is over and I'm so happy about it. I had these terrible thoughts in my head these last six months that something would happen to stop publication (bad economy forces publisher to shut its doors, bad economy forces publisher to halt production on all but the sure thing bestsellers, or something else I can't fathom). I'm just so glad today finally arrived and now nobody can take it away from me..

It's funny, I knew it was coming, but didn't know when. I came home from work tonight and saw there was a UPS notice of attempted delivery. I called them and asked if I could pick it up from their service center. What a strange ride it was to pick up the package. Phillies pre-game is going on the radio and I've got tears flowing for the incredible Harry memories yet I'm driving to get something I've wanted since I was a little kid. Strange juxtaposition of emotions.

By the way, if you pre-ordered my book from Amazon, they began shipping yesterday. You'll probably have it sometime next week.
