"Runner's knee" is usually identified with chondromalacia. I've had that, too. Patellar tendinitis is often called "Jumper's knee". I don't like any injuries, of course, but tendinitis is at the top of the bottom since it almost always responds to rest and ice and can often be managed within an exercise program since the pain tends to lessen after warming up. That doesn't help a start and stop activity like baseball very much , though.
