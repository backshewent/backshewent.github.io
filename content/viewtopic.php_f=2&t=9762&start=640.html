by momadance » Mon Jun 22, 2020 21:43:49

Finally did my taxes today. I kept putting it off because I thought I was going to owe a bunch of money like last year. Turns out, I got a nice refund. I haven't used my old camera gear in awhile because I've had a ton a problems with it the last few times I used it, especially with the focus. My first mistake was not buying a Canon or Nikon years ago and going with the Olympus 4/3 system. Which I liked because the lenses were superb. But that whole system is dead and the adapters to use 4/3 lenses with Micro 4/3's are complete trash.

I really wanted new gear for my trip this summer but I didn't want to spend the money thinking I owed at least a grand in taxes and my trip was expensive, but I already have the money set away for it. Much to my surprise, I got a nice refund. Enough to buy a bunch of new gear. It took a bunch of shopping around, especially for the body (since just about everything comes in a kit). I ended up with the Canon Rebel T7 body which I ended up find new on Amazon for $270. I didn't want the kit, just the body because the 18-55mm lens is just so-so. So for $900 I ended up with:

Canon Rebel T7 body ($270, kits are around $450)
Canon EF-S 55-250mm f/4-5.6 IS STM Telephoto ($183)
Sigma 17-50mm f/2.8 EX DC OS HSM ($338)
128GB 150mb/s SD Card ($29)
Car charger for the battery ($20)

Really wanted a faster lens instead of the kit lenses they offer. The kit lens with 3.5 aperture would just drive me nuts. Really looking forward to getting back into it. Will probably end up buying a 2nd SD card as well as the Canon EF 50mm f/1.8 before we leave next month.
