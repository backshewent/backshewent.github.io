Pretty shitty day with my neighbor.

Came home from visiting my parents to my parking permits from the PPA left on my stoop. We've had a lot of problems with mail around here and of course they end up with this lady. She leaves them there with a note: "These came to the wrong house - the person you called the police about several years ago. We tend to be neighborly around here... perhaps you didn't know that. -Cathy"

Shortly after moving into the neighborhood this lady parked behind my house, blocking my way into my parking spot. I had been having a problem with someone else doing the same, so I had assumed someone thought that because the house was for sale they believed it was vacant and nobody cared or something. So I called the cops instead of knocking on doors at 10 PM to find out whose car it is. Didn't think it was a big deal, she came out and moved it, cops told me it was a neighbor. I've never talked to this lady despite living 4 doors down from her. We share an alley with a few other folks, a typical rowhome. Her spot is so overgrown that she doesn't park there. So here we are, almost 2 years later, and she decides to snipe at me with some wrongly delivered mail about calling the cops on her. This lady's husband came out about 6 weeks ago, right in front of me, screaming at a Philly U kid "What the hell are you doing? Stop shoveling snow into my house!" after a storm and then ran back inside. They seem like wonderful people. Not sure what my next step is.
