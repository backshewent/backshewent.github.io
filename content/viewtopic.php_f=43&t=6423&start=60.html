by Phan In Phlorida » Thu Sep 04, 2008 01:31:27

The pen, which was a strong point, is now worn out... that's what happens when you use the same 3 guys in just about every game. The talking heads and "average fan" would say the team isn't "inspired" or "has no heart". With baseball, that's basically BS rah rah stuff. But there does seem to be something amiss for the last month and a half plus. Not being privy to the inside, we have no way of knowing if there's internal strife, if they're subconsciously overwhelmed, mentally beaten since interleague, shrinking under the weight of expetations, or whatever. It's just... well, I haven't really had a good feeling about this year's Phils since interleague. And lately, it just seems like they don't expect to win every day (while there have been flashes, it hasn't been consistent of late). It just feels like they're treading water.

Last season, the Phils were the primary reason the Mets went into a freefall. This year, we only have three left with the Mets. So unless the Phils sweep the Mets in NY this weekend, I don't see much of a chance the Mets repeat last year's freefall. At worst, the Phils need to take 2 of three, or it's basically over because time is running out (I don't see this Phils team playing 700 ball here on out without the psychological boost of beating the Mets this weekend).
