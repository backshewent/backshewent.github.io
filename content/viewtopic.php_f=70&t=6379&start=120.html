by Scarlett » Mon Sep 01, 2008 10:59:20

Bought tix at StubHub for yesterday's Yankees/BlueJays game because BF is a longtime Yankee fan and wanted to see the stadium one last time before they close it down. The tix were part of his birthday present, and because 1)yankees home games are always sold out and 2)everyone and their brother wanted to see one last home game befoe the stadium's gone, the stubhub price was many multiple of the actual face price of the tickets. Ouch!
Took the Yankee Clipper to the game, which provided an awesome view of Manhattan from the water--definitely would recommend it!
The Yankee Stadium--provided some great visuals--a classic and memorable stadium, no doubt.
The Yankee's performance--so disappointing. It's one thing to play really well, and lose because you either played a better team, or a good team who had an outstanding day. To be paid professionals, well-paid professionals at that, and lose due to lackluster play--it's so disappointing.
Despite the poor play and the loss, it was a great day for a ball game.
