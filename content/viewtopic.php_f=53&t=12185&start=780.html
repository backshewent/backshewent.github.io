I mean, I realize the Phils are much better in a lot of ways, but the one thing that concerns me about this team is that they could theoretically get outslugged. Halladay and Lee are around the plate a lot, and so can give up some hits. I think the worst matchup for the Phillies will be a team that can score in bunches, because the Phils' lineup has shown a distinct inability to do so against even mediocre pitching.

So, teams like the Yankees and Reds, for example, are the sort who could give the Phils' a problem, insofar as the Phils offense has a tendency to make decidedly mediocre pitchers look like Cy Youngs. Whereas the Braves don't scare me at all.

Wondering if the Mets could join the former class.
