I think from the POV of the PTB the plan is rational: Keep the big names on the team, try to patch and hope to get lucky -- at least until the new TV deal is done. They must know that, given the state of the farm system and the FA market, serious rebuilding is going to take multiple years, during which time the team's performance is likely to get worse before it gets better, attendance will dip and ratings will fall. So their approach is not necessarily irrational, just shortsighted and, at least IMO wrong-headed, since it delays the inevitable rebuilding.

But their primary concern right now is getting as much as they can from the new TV deal. From an owner's perspective that's rational. From a fan's perspective, in the short term it sucks.
