This might seem weird, but Tiger's win reminded me a bit of the US Men's Curling gold medal, because both were about guys who had something to prove.

There are other elements here operating as well--it's not just redemption, it's a huge athletic victory by someone over 40. Old guys love that stuff.

Weirdly too, until now, there was a sense that Tiger's career was somewhat disappointing. At first, most thought he'd break the record for most majors, but he hasn't really gotten close. (And really, I think it's still unlikely that he'll get another 4 or 5 majors to break or tie Nicklaus's record.) But this one win in some sense removes the sting a bit.
