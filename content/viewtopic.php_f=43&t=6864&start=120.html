On another subject, I've been thinking about the whole first game DH thing, and I'm no longer very troubled by it. Playing Bruntlett (with Burrell as the DH), it's not something you'd want to do regularly. But it's only for 1 game in the Series. OTOH, if Charlie decides to go with Coste as DH, the risk that Ruiz might get hurt and they'd lose the DH for the rest of the game doesn't strike me as much of a gamble either. If necessary, you just use PH's in the DH slot the rest of the game. Plus Hamels is no automatic out.
