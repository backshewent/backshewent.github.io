It sounds not dissimilar to an accident I had about 6 years ago. I was running for a meeting on site with my bike under one arm and my courier bag over my shoulder. on a wet wooden walkway in cycling shoes. Anyhow like you I came down on my back and I think I twisted hard as I fell (bikes not being the lightest of things). I was in severe pain and had to spend a day on my back, chewing ibuprofen and something else and watching JLA on cartoon network. The hospital had seen me pretty quickly, and the verdict was it was entirely muscular.

Which was why I spent two days not moving over christmas. If it's only muscle, these things are recoverable, but the longer you leave it, the more you'll be working to rectify it.
