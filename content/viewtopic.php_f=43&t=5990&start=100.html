Blanton concerns me because he seems to benefit greatly from his home park. Over his career he has been consistent in this. Oakland's park has a huge foul territory which results in many more outs on popups that go out of play most everywhere else.

Oakland's home park combined wiht their MLB leading defense would help a guy like Blanton more than many others I'd guess. The A's seem to understand this too as he has 14 starts at home and only 6 on the road this season. That seems kinda amazing doesn't it?
