My understanding is the beer doesn't really go bad in the sense that it might cause stomach problems. Indeed, one reason people drink beer is because it's much more likely to be safe to drink that water. Alcohol (and hops) are pretty effective at killing micro organisms. The 6 month thing has more to do with the hops going skunky, which might have an unpleasant taste, but isn't going to make you sick. Some high-gravity beers are considered even better aged. (I've got two bottles of DFH 120 I'm keeping around for the purpose.)

Chicken is a likely culprit. Or it could have been a virus. Flu for example comes on pretty suddenly. If you're still feeling funky, you might want to check that you don't have a fever.
