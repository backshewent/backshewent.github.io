Holy Tomato in Blackwood, NJ. They make a tomato pie, Trenton style, as pictured here with sausage. They also make a marinara ( "RED" ) pie and a "Chicago" pie which is described as a white pie with dollops of crushed tomato, but which differed little from the Trenton pie.

Wafer thin crust that is nicely crackly at the cornicione, but softer in the middle due to the weight and moisture of the cheese and sauce.

Very good, almost great pie. Busy as hell on a Tuesday night, great prices, great salads, amazing desserts (sopapilla cheesecake).

Run by a jolly blonde and her three daughters.
