i only got through page 10, so my apologies if someone else already expressed this opinion

-- i remember when j-roll signed his deal there was quite a bit of hand-wringing on pp that he was being overpaid and his value wouldn't hold up over the course of the contract. turns out, that was a bargain for us.

-- howard is a beast right now. he will be a beast for the next 3 years. what's more, he's improved physically and defensively the last 3 years running.

-- yea, it might blow to have him on the team 2014 onwards, but we really don't know how things are going to break between now and then. he might get traded. he might get even better. (but most likely he'll get worse and we'll all be sad).

-- there was no way he was going to get less than $20mm/yr if he went on the free market. no way. yeah, he ain't no a-rod, but he's our guy and he's probably worth it to us.

-- we have a million more OF prospects than 1B prospects, this makes more sense than signing werth.

-- maybe we're raising payroll. werth will be a phillies, too!

whatever, i'm a bit dismayed at the 2014-2016 implications, but overall, i'm glad ryan howard will remain a phillies.
