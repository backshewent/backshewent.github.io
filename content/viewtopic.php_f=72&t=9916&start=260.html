by Barry Jive » Wed Jan 06, 2010 14:05:43

Dude, I only found a couple calls for in-progress jobs where people saw anyone breaking in, both guys got arrested. Any other calls were reports of where people found the house broken into when they got home. It looks like it started a couple days before Christmas and has slowed down since New Year's. The other in-progress jobs were unfounded, meaning either nothing was going on or the guys who were trying to break in ran away before they made any progress.

If it's any consolation, those two arrests came when there was someone home at the time. Basically, if you or your wife hears anything that sounds like someone's trying to break in, call the police. They'll almost always be there in two minutes or less, usually faster.

The response time blows for reports or lower priority jobs, but in progress jobs, anything more important than a vandalism in progress or a domestic dispute or a serious auto accident, they're there in a jiffy. In fact, even if there's someone suspicious outside prowling around, that's a pretty high priority too. People call for the dumbest bull shit all the time and police have to respond. So never be afraid to call the cops.
