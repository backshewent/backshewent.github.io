by BigEd76 » Mon Jan 12, 2015 11:45:29

The series had seven of the best (Meals, Nelson, Cooper, Reynolds, Barrett, Wendelstedt, Kellogg....not garbage like Bucknor and Barry), and it was neat to see behind the scenes where Torre calls each one personally to invite them to work the World Series and they're legit excited. It's also funny seeing what these guys wear to the games (Barrett is a pimp and Cooper wears football jerseys) and Wendelstedt talks about how he gets a WS ring to go with his dad's collection and can finally retire Harry's brush and clicker that he's used all these years. They also show Torre meeting them in the locker room after every game and giving them handshakes and hugs and all, and you can hear their conversations at the plate (including speaking Spanish to Salvador Perez!), at the bases between innings, Pence asking about rules he doesn't know about, how they handled the bench-clearing incident when Perez and Strickland got into it, etc. Just interesting stuff that they don't normally show
