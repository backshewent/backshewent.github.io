The guys talking about cats in new user thread reminded me, time to revive the doggie topic. Some may recall I used to post lots of foster puppy pics when we lived in Texas and were part of one of those "underground railroad" programs (southern strays go north to a better life). Now here in Phila, we don't have as much space given our 75-lb Maggie is already on the scene.

But my wife discovered City of Elderly Love which finds temp homes for senior dogs who have come free for various reasons. We just hosted 12-year-old Scooby for a couple weeks - wish it was longer but a Jersey shore family snapped him up as soon as he was eligible. Great experience, we'll probably host another next month.
