Continuing the beer pong discussion from the COVID thread, in the mid-90s at Lafayette College, the game called "beer pong" was a modified version of ping-pong, played with paddles. You had one or two players per team, with one cup per player. The cup was filled with one 12-oz (cheap) beer at the start of each game. If a cup was hit during play, and the team whose cup was hit could not return it, they would have to take a drink. If the ball landed in a cup, they would have to finish the beer.

The game that most know as "beer bong" we called Beirut.

Even at that time it seems Lafayette was an outlier.
