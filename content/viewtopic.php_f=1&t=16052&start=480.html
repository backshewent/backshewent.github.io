by Philly the Kid » Mon Feb 05, 2018 15:08:51

I really can't believe we're having this discussion already - but let's be clear:

Carson Wentz is our future. This season doesn't happen without him. He's our first chance to have a dynastic QB. This coaching staff found a way to get Nicky 6 comfortable - and Nick played like he did in 2013. I have no confidence he could play like this for a full 16, or the next 10 years. And because of the way things go side-ways in Phila. with fans and media - we cannot allow some loyal distorted Nick-faction to make it were Carson wants out and we can't keep him and the whole thing goes south.

This is Carson's team.

If this was the 60's we'd keep both perhaps, but I remember the Craig Morton/Roger Staubach arguments in Dal, the Billy Kilmer/Sonny Jurgenson arguments, Earl Morral/Unitas Morral/Griese arguments.

Nick was brilliant for two games. He executed.

Carson does amazing things when the execution breaks down. When the D is ready.

This is not even a debate.

I'm also not convinced that the demand for Foles will be as high as many believe, because they saw that he was effective in a particular system, with Pro Bowl and All Pro line - and a good arsenal of weapons. Does he want to start somewhere else?

Carson is our Aaron Rogers. There is no debate.

My concern - is that Carson won't be ready on Day 1, and may need some games to be himself again...
