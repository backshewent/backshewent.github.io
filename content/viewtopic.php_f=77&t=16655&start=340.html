by TenuredVulture » Mon Aug 31, 2015 17:57:26

It's important to keep in mind these polls may be quite off. I know you all read Nate religiously, so I won't go into the whys, but there's every reason to discount these findings. Now that doesn't mean that Pataki has a chance, but it's quite likely that systematic problems with the polls (how they handle cell phones v. landlines, likely voter screens) could be very distorting, especially with such a large field as the Reps. have. Even a fairly small error can make a big difference--consider Monmouth has Fiorina 3rd with 10%, while the Des Moines register has her 7th, with 5% of the vote.
